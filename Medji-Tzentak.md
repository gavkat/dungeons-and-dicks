# Medji-Tzentak

**Tags:** #Faction #Lore

---

## Overview
"Sons of the Keeper" (also rendered *Madji-Tzentak*) — an order tied to [The Keeper](The%20Keeper.md) and the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md). [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md) claimed to be its last member; in truth he had slaughtered his own village as "repentance" and served the Pharaoh's revival instead.

Whether any true Medji-Tzentak survive is unknown.

## Known History
- [Session 7](Session%207.md): [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md) introduced himself as one of the order.
- [Session 11](Session%2011.md): his betrayal revealed the claim was a corruption of the order's purpose.
