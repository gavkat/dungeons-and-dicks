# Medji-Tzentak

**Tags:** #Faction #Lore

---

## Overview
"Sons of the Keeper" (also rendered *Madji-Tzentak*) — an order tied to [[The Keeper]] and the [[Tomb of Tzentak]]. [[Ouraeus]] claimed to be its last member; in truth he had slaughtered his own village as "repentance" and served the Pharaoh's revival instead.

Whether any true Medji-Tzentak survive is unknown.

## Known History
- [[Session 7]]: [[Ouraeus]] introduced himself as one of the order.
- [[Session 11]]: his betrayal revealed the claim was a corruption of the order's purpose.
