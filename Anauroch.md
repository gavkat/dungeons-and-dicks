# Anauroch

**Tags:** #Location #Desert

---

## Overview
A scorching desert (also rendered *Auronoch*) — territory of the [[Itju-Sha]], site of [[Ouraeus]]'s oasis, and home to the [[Tomb of Tzentak]]. [[Riptide]]'s lead placed the cure for [[Marisya]] here.

## Sub-locations
- **Itju-Sha hideout** — a settlement built into the colossal skeleton of a desert leviathan, with fighting pits ([[Session 7]]).
- **The Oasis** — [[Ouraeus]]'s home; burned to a blackened scar by the [[Itju-Sha]] ([[Session 9]]). [[Rik]] dreamt of its waters turning to blood ([[Session 6]]).
- [[Tomb of Tzentak]].
