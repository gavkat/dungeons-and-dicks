# Anauroch

**Tags:** #Location #Desert

---

## Overview
A scorching desert (also rendered *Auronoch*) — territory of the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md), site of [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md)'s oasis, and home to the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md). [Riptide](Riptide.md)'s lead placed the cure for [Marisya](Marisya.md) here.

## Sub-locations
- **Itju-Sha hideout** — a settlement built into the colossal skeleton of a desert leviathan, with fighting pits ([Session 7](Session%207.md)).
- **The Oasis** — [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md)'s home; burned to a blackened scar by the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md) ([Session 9](Session%209.md)). [Rik](Rik.md) dreamt of its waters turning to blood ([Session 6](Session%206.md)).
- [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md).
