# Session 9: The Oasis

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** [[Blackjack]] becomes the Shepherd beneath the sea; the party finds [[Ouraeus]]'s oasis burned and ends [[Tlincalli]] for good.

## Summary

In the crushing depths of the midnight ocean, Jack was reborn. Rescued by the sea elves [[Vespera]] and [[Kaelen]] and brought to a bioluminescent sanctuary, the truth of his burden was revealed: the obsidian amulet — the [[Shard of the Great Devourer]] — had begun to beat in synchronicity with his heart. Where others rotted into necrotic husks, Jack became a vessel. [[Kaelen]] spoke it into the currents: if their brother **Valyrian** is the [[Sovereign Prophet]] of the beast, then Jack is **the Shepherd**. After days of training, Jack dreamt he inhabited the gargantuan body of [[The Great Devourer]] itself, forcing his will upon a titan to save his friends. Before departing he attempted, catastrophically, to woo the regal [[Vespera]] — emerging from the depths with new powers, a new title, and a tall tale about a "sea girlfriend" who is definitely, totally real.

Meanwhile, the rest of the party followed [[Ouraeus]] home to find a waking nightmare: the oasis a blackened scar, the work of the [[Itju-Sha]]. The sand erupted in ambush, led by the chitinous terror [[Tlincalli]]. Bagpipes screamed, a fireball roared — but the turning point came from the water. Aided by [[Koa]] and a runic nautilus shell, Jack took a "Fathomless Plunge" and emerged from the oasis pool, impaling [[Tlincalli]] with a spectral tentacle and ending the ambush.

Reunited and battle-worn, the party salvaged carapace armor and fallen steel, then traveled north through the dunes until the horizon gave up its secret: *"There it is, the [[Tomb of Tzentak]]."*

## Key Developments

- [[Blackjack]] revealed as **the Shepherd** of [[The Great Devourer]]; the [[Sovereign Prophet]] is named: **Valyrian**, brother of [[Kaelen]].
- [[Tlincalli]] slain; the party reunited.
- [[Ouraeus]]'s oasis home destroyed by the [[Itju-Sha]].
- Arrival at the [[Tomb of Tzentak]].
