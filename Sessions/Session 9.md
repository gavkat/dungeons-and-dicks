# Session 9: The Oasis

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) becomes the Shepherd beneath the sea; the party finds [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md)'s oasis burned and ends [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md) for good.

## Summary

In the crushing depths of the midnight ocean, Jack was reborn. Rescued by the sea elves [Vespera](Vespera.md) and [Kaelen](Kaelen.md) and brought to a bioluminescent sanctuary, the truth of his burden was revealed: the obsidian amulet — the [Shard of the Great Devourer](Shard%20of%20the%20Great%20Devourer.md) — had begun to beat in synchronicity with his heart. Where others rotted into necrotic husks, Jack became a vessel. [Kaelen](Kaelen.md) spoke it into the currents: if their brother **Valyrian** is the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md) of the beast, then Jack is **the Shepherd**. After days of training, Jack dreamt he inhabited the gargantuan body of [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md) itself, forcing his will upon a titan to save his friends. Before departing he attempted, catastrophically, to woo the regal [Vespera](Vespera.md) — emerging from the depths with new powers, a new title, and a tall tale about a "sea girlfriend" who is definitely, totally real.

Meanwhile, the rest of the party followed [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md) home to find a waking nightmare: the oasis a blackened scar, the work of the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md). The sand erupted in ambush, led by the chitinous terror [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md). Bagpipes screamed, a fireball roared — but the turning point came from the water. Aided by [Koa](Koa.md) and a runic nautilus shell, Jack took a "Fathomless Plunge" and emerged from the oasis pool, impaling [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md) with a spectral tentacle and ending the ambush.

Reunited and battle-worn, the party salvaged carapace armor and fallen steel, then traveled north through the dunes until the horizon gave up its secret: *"There it is, the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md)."*

## Key Developments

- [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) revealed as **the Shepherd** of [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md); the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md) is named: **Valyrian**, brother of [Kaelen](Kaelen.md).
- [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md) slain; the party reunited.
- [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md)'s oasis home destroyed by the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md).
- Arrival at the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md).
