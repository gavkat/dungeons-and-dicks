# Session 5: The Bird & The Worm

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** The [Mage Guard](Mage%20Guard.md) comes for [Dave](Dave.md), the party crashes onto [Mintan](Mintan.md), and [Koa](Koa.md) reveals the truth about the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md).

## Summary

After a desperate three-day flight from the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md), the exhausted party was lost over the [Sea of Swords](Sea%20of%20Swords.md). They shared their personal quests: [Icarus](Icarus.md)'s "rehabilitation" from eating birds, [Rik](Rik.md)'s search for a cure for his cursed wife [Marisya](Marisya.md) at the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md), and [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md)'s hunt for a missing guildmate.

The [Mage Guard](Mage%20Guard.md) interrupted, accusing them of violating the [Draconic Containment Accord](Draconic%20Containment%20Accord.md) and demanding they surrender [Dave](Dave.md) for "sanitization" or be branded "collaborators in contamination." Refusing, the party tricked the guards, used the guards' own banishing box to save Dave, and escaped through a portal — crashing onto the island of [Mintan](Mintan.md), where a monstrous Ankheg erupted from the sand and killed one of their pursuers. With the help of a tusked warrior named [Koa](Koa.md), they defeated the beast and the remaining guards.

Over a campfire feast of Ankheg meat, unsettling whispers of *"pieces of six"* echoed from the trees. [Koa](Koa.md) revealed the chilling truth: the Prophet was once his friend — a prince twisted by an amulet from the Devourer, a god of the [Dark Six](The%20Dark%20Six.md). Koa, in hiding, asked them to investigate the whispers and warned they too were now marked by [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md).

Venturing into the jungle, the party fought through powerful personalized hallucinations before discovering the whispers' source: a teleporting parrot once owned by [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md) ([Polly](Polly.md)). The visions, they realized, came from the tainted Ankheg meat.

## Key Developments

- [Mage Guard](Mage%20Guard.md) and the [Draconic Containment Accord](Draconic%20Containment%20Accord.md) introduced; the party are now fugitives.
- The [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md)'s origin revealed: a prince corrupted by a Devourer amulet.
- The party is *marked* by [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md).
- [Polly](Polly.md) found on [Mintan](Mintan.md).
