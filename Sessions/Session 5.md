# Session 5: The Bird & The Worm

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** The [[Mage Guard]] comes for [[Dave]], the party crashes onto [[Mintan]], and [[Koa]] reveals the truth about the [[Sovereign Prophet]].

## Summary

After a desperate three-day flight from the [[Sovereign Prophet]], the exhausted party was lost over the [[Sea of Swords]]. They shared their personal quests: [[Icarus]]'s "rehabilitation" from eating birds, [[Rik]]'s search for a cure for his cursed wife [[Marisya]] at the [[Tomb of Tzentak]], and [[Wild Fury]]'s hunt for a missing guildmate.

The [[Mage Guard]] interrupted, accusing them of violating the [[Draconic Containment Accord]] and demanding they surrender [[Dave]] for "sanitization" or be branded "collaborators in contamination." Refusing, the party tricked the guards, used the guards' own banishing box to save Dave, and escaped through a portal — crashing onto the island of [[Mintan]], where a monstrous Ankheg erupted from the sand and killed one of their pursuers. With the help of a tusked warrior named [[Koa]], they defeated the beast and the remaining guards.

Over a campfire feast of Ankheg meat, unsettling whispers of *"pieces of six"* echoed from the trees. [[Koa]] revealed the chilling truth: the Prophet was once his friend — a prince twisted by an amulet from the Devourer, a god of the [[The Dark Six|Dark Six]]. Koa, in hiding, asked them to investigate the whispers and warned they too were now marked by [[The Great Devourer]].

Venturing into the jungle, the party fought through powerful personalized hallucinations before discovering the whispers' source: a teleporting parrot once owned by [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] ([[Polly]]). The visions, they realized, came from the tainted Ankheg meat.

## Key Developments

- [[Mage Guard]] and the [[Draconic Containment Accord]] introduced; the party are now fugitives.
- The [[Sovereign Prophet]]'s origin revealed: a prince corrupted by a Devourer amulet.
- The party is *marked* by [[The Great Devourer]].
- [[Polly]] found on [[Mintan]].
