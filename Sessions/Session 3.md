# Session 3: The Choke

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** Ghost pirates in the fog, [Riptide](Riptide.md)'s confession, and a dominated [Sauriv](Sauriv.md) — the cursed amulet falls to [Blackjack](Blackjack.md).

## Summary

[The Tempest's Temptation](The%20Tempest's%20Temptation.md) was swallowed by the unnatural fog of [The Choke](The%20Choke.md) — a claustrophobic channel of jagged black cliffs and a graveyard of ships. The party noticed [Icarus](Icarus.md) had mysteriously vanished. A spectral galleon crewed by ghost pirates emerged, forcing a desperate naval battle; [Gearsley](Gearsley.md) fell unconscious before [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md) and [Riptide](Riptide.md) arrived to clear the threat.

In the drunken celebration after, [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) overheard the Captain plotting for a "reward" and the need for the crew to "survive one more day." [Riptide](Riptide.md) confessed to [Rik](Rik.md): a dark pact with a shrouded figure called [The Traveler](The%20Traveler.md) for a cursed amulet (the [Shard of the Great Devourer](Shard%20of%20the%20Great%20Devourer.md)) now causing black rot to consume his body. He apologized for bringing Rik aboard and revealed [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md) had promised him salvation in the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md) five years ago.

The search for the captain's bird led to a horrifying find: [Icarus](Icarus.md) caged, compelled by an unseen voice to stay, and [Krell](Krell.md)'s wyrmling [Chomp](Chomp.md) missing. [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) and [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) found [Rik](Rik.md) hanging dead in his room — then were ambushed by [Sauriv](Sauriv.md), the lizardfolk bosun's glazed white eyes marking him a puppet under someone's control. The party defeated him, and the amulet fell into [Blackjack](Blackjack.md)'s possession.

## Key Developments

- [Riptide](Riptide.md)'s pact with [The Traveler](The%20Traveler.md) and the rotting curse of the [Shard of the Great Devourer](Shard%20of%20the%20Great%20Devourer.md) revealed.
- [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md) named as a place of promised "salvation."
- [Sauriv](Sauriv.md) was being controlled by an unseen force; [Chomp](Chomp.md) went missing.
- [Rik](Rik.md) was found hanged (and yet lives — never fully explained).
- [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) now secretly holds the amulet.
