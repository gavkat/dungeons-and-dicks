# Session 3: The Choke

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** Ghost pirates in the fog, [[Riptide]]'s confession, and a dominated [[Sauriv]] — the cursed amulet falls to [[Blackjack]].

## Summary

[[The Tempest's Temptation]] was swallowed by the unnatural fog of [[The Choke]] — a claustrophobic channel of jagged black cliffs and a graveyard of ships. The party noticed [[Icarus]] had mysteriously vanished. A spectral galleon crewed by ghost pirates emerged, forcing a desperate naval battle; [[Gearsley]] fell unconscious before [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] and [[Riptide]] arrived to clear the threat.

In the drunken celebration after, [[Blackjack]] overheard the Captain plotting for a "reward" and the need for the crew to "survive one more day." [[Riptide]] confessed to [[Rik]]: a dark pact with a shrouded figure called [[The Traveler]] for a cursed amulet (the [[Shard of the Great Devourer]]) now causing black rot to consume his body. He apologized for bringing Rik aboard and revealed [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] had promised him salvation in the [[Tomb of Tzentak]] five years ago.

The search for the captain's bird led to a horrifying find: [[Icarus]] caged, compelled by an unseen voice to stay, and [[Krell]]'s wyrmling [[Chomp]] missing. [[Wild Fury]] and [[Blackjack]] found [[Rik]] hanging dead in his room — then were ambushed by [[Sauriv]], the lizardfolk bosun's glazed white eyes marking him a puppet under someone's control. The party defeated him, and the amulet fell into [[Blackjack]]'s possession.

## Key Developments

- [[Riptide]]'s pact with [[The Traveler]] and the rotting curse of the [[Shard of the Great Devourer]] revealed.
- [[Tomb of Tzentak]] named as a place of promised "salvation."
- [[Sauriv]] was being controlled by an unseen force; [[Chomp]] went missing.
- [[Rik]] was found hanged (and yet lives — never fully explained).
- [[Blackjack]] now secretly holds the amulet.
