# Session 4: Bottom Feeders

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** Murder accusations, the [[Sovereign Prophet]] revealed, [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] devoured — the party is scattered.

## Summary

After a long rest, the party was summoned to deck by [[Captain Siren Shellsword]]: [[Riptide]] and [[Sauriv]] had been murdered overnight and the amulet was missing. [[Krell]] publicly accused the party — citing [[Rik]]'s drinking with the guard, the wrestling match with [[Sauriv]], his missing wyrmling [[Chomp]], and [[Icarus]]'s mysterious escape from his cage. The crew turned hostile and a battle for survival began.

It was interrupted from the depths. The [[Sovereign Prophet]] of [[The Great Devourer]] announced himself with a disembodied voice, heralding [[Sha'Argan]], a massive sea monster, and declaring the ship marked as "a meal." The Captain was revealed to be in league with the Prophet, having promised 40 men as sacrifice; for falling short, the Prophet had the Devourer snatch and consume him. [[Icarus]] tried to bargain for the party's lives but the Prophet would not be persuaded.

The Prophet's sahuagin began throwing the crew overboard to feed the monster. The party was forcibly split: [[Blackjack]] was rescued by mysterious (and wildly attractive) sea elves ([[Vespera]] and [[Kaelen]]), while the rest escaped the plank by leaping onto [[Dave]] and flying away.

## Key Developments

- [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] devoured by [[The Great Devourer]]; the [[Bottom Feeders]] crew destroyed.
- [[Sovereign Prophet]] and [[Sha'Argan]] introduced.
- The party split: [[Blackjack]] beneath the waves, the rest airborne on [[Dave]].
