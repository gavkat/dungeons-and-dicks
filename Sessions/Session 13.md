# Session 13: Code Black, Whiteout

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** An oracle's warning names [[Tzentak]] a [[Vox of the Dark Six|Vox]]; a trek north toward [[Hartsvale]] on polar bears; a [[The Consortium|Consortium]] "Code Black" abducts [[Dave]]; [[Icarus]] strands himself as an owl; and a timid Frost Giant named [[Vlad]] emerges from the blizzard.

## Summary

The brutal arctic cold brought haunting visions and dark revelations. Still comatose, [[Blackjack]] witnessed an apocalyptic warning from the [[Sovereign Prophet]]. Reacting to it, [[Polly]] went rigid, its eyes flashing sapphire blue as it chanted *"Pieces of Six!"* The colliding arcane forces let the elven oracle [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] briefly possess [[Rik]] and deliver a dire warning: by destroying Pharaoh [[Tzentak]]'s heart, the party shattered a cosmic phylactery and **executed a [[Vox of the Dark Six|Vox]] of the [[The Dark Six|Dark Six]]**, triggering the catastrophic magic surge that froze the world overnight. The remaining **five Voxes** are now furious and weaving a net of darkness across the continent.

With [[Blackjack]] conscious again, the party realised they needed more transport to head north toward [[Hartsvale]]. They swindled a local merchant into selling them two massive **polar bear mounts** for a mere ten gold pieces, and set off — [[Wild Fury]] scouting ahead on his wyvern, [[Dave]].

Catastrophe struck on the road north when [[The Consortium|Consortium]] [[Mage Guard|Mage Guards]] ambushed the trek, but they were easily handled by the tactics and teamwork of the [[Boner Bros]]. The fight escalated when the cornered ground-level soldier called in a **"Code Black"** reinforcement: a mysterious figure cloaked in warping ash tore through a portal, out-manoeuvred the group, and swiftly **abducted [[Dave]]**.

Battered, freezing, encumbered and out of food, the party wandered in circles through a blinding whiteout blizzard. Seeking a path ahead, [[Icarus]] polymorphed into a Giant Snow Owl — but his avian appetite got the better of him, and he lost his bearings, becoming entirely separated and stranded alone in the wastes.

Finally, as the remaining frostbitten survivors huddled around a sputtering campfire, the screaming gale died down. A massive twenty-foot shadow loomed over the camp, and a timid, hunched Frost Giant named [[Vlad]] stepped forward into the light.

- The party bought **two polar bear mounts** for ten gold and set out north toward [[Hartsvale]].
- **[[Dave]] abducted** by an ashen figure summoned as a [[The Consortium|Consortium]] **"Code Black"** ([[The Ashen Figure]]).
- [[Icarus]] polymorphed into a Giant Snow Owl and stranded himself alone in the blizzard.
- New ally? A timid Frost Giant, [[Vlad]], steps out of the whiteout.
