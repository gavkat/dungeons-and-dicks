# Session 8: Wild Fury Road

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** [Icarus](Icarus.md) reborn pink-haired and half-orc; a vehicular escape from the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md) in full Mad Max style.

## Summary

In the fighting pits, [Rik](Rik.md) earned the title of Champion at the cost of executing [Icarus](Icarus.md) before the roaring crowd, and was welcomed by [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md) to his personal den for celebrations. Back in the cage, grief sent [Gearsley](Gearsley.md) spiraling, but [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md) stayed calm — with a hand that melted into serpents he snatched the keys from a guard, revealing to [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) that he is the last of the [Medji-Tzentak](Medji-Tzentak.md), waiting for the moment to strike.

Death proved merely a suggestion for [Icarus](Icarus.md). After declaring himself an "Immortal God" in the void, he was stitched back together in the refuse heap — reborn as a hulking half-orc with shocking pink hair — and proved his identity to a skeptical [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) with a very specific dietary confession.

The escape bled into a high-speed battle on wheels: [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) cleared the path with a magical suggestion, [Gearsley](Gearsley.md) hot-wired the getaway vehicle, and [Icarus](Icarus.md) screamed his divinity while unleashing a Fireball to cover [Rik](Rik.md)'s retrieval. The [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md) gave chase with "The Harpoon" and "The Pig" — Icarus incinerated the Harpoon, and [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md) summoned spectral fangs to tear through the Pig. The [Boner Bros](Boner%20Bros.md) drove their stolen rust-bucket into the setting sun, battered but free.

## Key Developments

- [Icarus](Icarus.md) reincarnated as a pink-haired half-orc.
- [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md) revealed serpentine powers; claims to be the last [Medji-Tzentak](Medji-Tzentak.md).
- Escaped the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md) with a stolen vehicle, destroying two pursuit craft.
