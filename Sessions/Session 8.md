# Session 8: Wild Fury Road

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** [[Icarus]] reborn pink-haired and half-orc; a vehicular escape from the [[Itju-Sha]] in full Mad Max style.

## Summary

In the fighting pits, [[Rik]] earned the title of Champion at the cost of executing [[Icarus]] before the roaring crowd, and was welcomed by [[Tlincalli]] to his personal den for celebrations. Back in the cage, grief sent [[Gearsley]] spiraling, but [[Ouraeus]] stayed calm — with a hand that melted into serpents he snatched the keys from a guard, revealing to [[Wild Fury]] that he is the last of the [[Medji-Tzentak]], waiting for the moment to strike.

Death proved merely a suggestion for [[Icarus]]. After declaring himself an "Immortal God" in the void, he was stitched back together in the refuse heap — reborn as a hulking half-orc with shocking pink hair — and proved his identity to a skeptical [[Wild Fury]] with a very specific dietary confession.

The escape bled into a high-speed battle on wheels: [[Wild Fury]] cleared the path with a magical suggestion, [[Gearsley]] hot-wired the getaway vehicle, and [[Icarus]] screamed his divinity while unleashing a Fireball to cover [[Rik]]'s retrieval. The [[Itju-Sha]] gave chase with "The Harpoon" and "The Pig" — Icarus incinerated the Harpoon, and [[Ouraeus]] summoned spectral fangs to tear through the Pig. The [[Boner Bros]] drove their stolen rust-bucket into the setting sun, battered but free.

## Key Developments

- [[Icarus]] reincarnated as a pink-haired half-orc.
- [[Ouraeus]] revealed serpentine powers; claims to be the last [[Medji-Tzentak]].
- Escaped the [[Itju-Sha]] with a stolen vehicle, destroying two pursuit craft.
