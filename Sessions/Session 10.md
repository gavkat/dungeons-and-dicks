# Session 10: Mummy Issues

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** Riddles, traps, and mummies in the [[Tomb of Tzentak]] — a narrow, bloody victory.

## Summary

The [[Boner Bros]] stood at the mouth of the great [[Tomb of Tzentak]], paying respects to the followers of [[The Keeper]] before facing the monolithic pharaoh sentinel. After several failed attempts they deciphered its riddle, whispered the word *"gold,"* paid their toll, and were granted entry. Inside they survived a gauntlet of snake pits, mimic ladders, and a crushing ceiling halted only by a well-placed [[Immovable Rod]].

The costs were brutal. [[Blackjack]] claimed a [[Ring of the Rotten]]; [[Icarus]] ventured into a dark void for a [[Wand of Secrets]], only for time to warp around him, leaving his body shattered by a wave of exhaustion. In an ancient trophy room, ibis- and crocodile-headed mummies waited. [[Wild Fury]] channeled his ancestors and smote the undead with divine energy, but rotting fists knocked both him and [[Icarus]] unconscious. The tide turned when [[Blackjack]] snatched a [[Flame Tongue Whip]] from the altar and tossed it to [[Rik]], who carved through the undead with fire. As the last mummy lunged, the arcane tension of Jack's Booming Blade snapped, vaporizing it to dust. The party was left standing — bloody, breathless, and barely victorious.

## Key Developments

- Entered the [[Tomb of Tzentak]] (toll: "gold").
- Loot: [[Ring of the Rotten]] (Jack), [[Wand of Secrets]] (Icarus, at heavy cost), [[Flame Tongue Whip]] (Rik).
- [[Wild Fury]] displayed divine smiting power channeled from his ancestors.
