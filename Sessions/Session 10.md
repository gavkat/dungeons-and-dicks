# Session 10: Mummy Issues

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** Riddles, traps, and mummies in the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md) — a narrow, bloody victory.

## Summary

The [Boner Bros](Boner%20Bros.md) stood at the mouth of the great [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md), paying respects to the followers of [The Keeper](The%20Keeper.md) before facing the monolithic pharaoh sentinel. After several failed attempts they deciphered its riddle, whispered the word *"gold,"* paid their toll, and were granted entry. Inside they survived a gauntlet of snake pits, mimic ladders, and a crushing ceiling halted only by a well-placed [Immovable Rod](Immovable%20Rod.md).

The costs were brutal. [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) claimed a [Ring of the Rotten](Ring%20of%20the%20Rotten.md); [Icarus](Icarus.md) ventured into a dark void for a [Wand of Secrets](Wand%20of%20Secrets.md), only for time to warp around him, leaving his body shattered by a wave of exhaustion. In an ancient trophy room, ibis- and crocodile-headed mummies waited. [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) channeled his ancestors and smote the undead with divine energy, but rotting fists knocked both him and [Icarus](Icarus.md) unconscious. The tide turned when [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) snatched a [Flame Tongue Whip](Flame%20Tongue%20Whip.md) from the altar and tossed it to [Rik](Rik.md), who carved through the undead with fire. As the last mummy lunged, the arcane tension of Jack's Booming Blade snapped, vaporizing it to dust. The party was left standing — bloody, breathless, and barely victorious.

## Key Developments

- Entered the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md) (toll: "gold").
- Loot: [Ring of the Rotten](Ring%20of%20the%20Rotten.md) (Jack), [Wand of Secrets](Wand%20of%20Secrets.md) (Icarus, at heavy cost), [Flame Tongue Whip](Flame%20Tongue%20Whip.md) (Rik).
- [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) displayed divine smiting power channeled from his ancestors.
