# Session 12: The Midvinter Special!

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** A holiday special in [Holly Hollow](Holly%20Hollow.md) — food-construct attackers, kidnapped children, [Agatha the Hag](Agatha%20the%20Hag.md) as Krampus, and [Glenn](Glenn.md) ascending as Father Klaus.

## Summary

The [Boner Bros](Boner%20Bros.md) woke to find their sweltering desert surroundings transformed into freezing tundra. With their car buried in snow and [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) trapped in a hypothermic seizure, the group flew on [Dave](Dave.md) toward the village of [Holly Hollow](Holly%20Hollow.md), taking refuge at the Sno'where Like Home Inn as the annual Mid-Winter Jubilee began in the town square.

An announcer introduced the local festive legend of Father Klaus — and to the party's astonishment, the man in holiday garb was [Glenn](Glenn.md), [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md)'s former associate and Head of HR, somehow aged terribly into an old man despite only five years passing. The festivities were violently interrupted by the Culinary Crusaders — hostile sentient food constructs including a sushi samurai and a taco knight. The party and Glenn neutralized them, only to discover the assault was a diversion: five local children had been abducted.

[Holly Hollow](Holly%20Hollow.md)'s portly Mayor contracted the party for 500 gold to retrieve the children. Tracking footprints into the snowy forest, they found a quaint cottage hiding a portal in its fireplace — leading to a murky swamp and a twisted industrial factory where the children were chained to a conveyor belt. There they confronted the legendary fiend **Krampus**, who was morphing captives into gremlin-like creatures. Fighting through formidable immunities, they destroyed the fiend with a massive divine smite from [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) and a high-voltage blast from [Icarus](Icarus.md) — and in death, the monster reverted to its true, familiar form: [Agatha the Hag](Agatha%20the%20Hag.md).

Three surviving children were rescued. [Glenn](Glenn.md) claimed a magical coat from Agatha's remains that shifted into an elegant red garment, while his wolf Walter transformed into a levitating stallion wolf. Assuming the mantle of the true Father Klaus, Glenn departed on a festive sleigh ride. The party collected their 500 gold and rested — until [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) received a promotional flyer for [The White Claw](The%20White%20Claw.md): a deadly invitation to face a maiden's champion in an arena where hearts fail.

## Key Developments

- Reunion with [Glenn](Glenn.md), mysteriously aged; he becomes the true Father Klaus.
- Krampus defeated — revealed as [Agatha the Hag](Agatha%20the%20Hag.md). Two of five children did not survive.
- Earned 500 gold from the Mayor of [Holly Hollow](Holly%20Hollow.md).
- New hook: [The White Claw](The%20White%20Claw.md) arena flyer delivered to [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md).
