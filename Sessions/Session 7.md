# Session 7: Finders & Keepers

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** Captured by the [[Itju-Sha]] in [[Anauroch]], the party meets [[Ouraeus]] — and [[Rik]] decapitates [[Icarus]] in the fighting pit.

## Summary

The party awoke not dead, but washed ashore in the scorching desert of [[Anauroch]], stripped of their gear and surrounded by the [[Itju-Sha]] — the Takers of the Sand — a gang in scrap-metal armor with crude sand vehicles. [[Icarus]]'s Minor Illusion act of defiance ("We will get on the truck!") drew the attention of their leader, the scorpion-man [[Tlincalli]], who flailed, chain-grappled, and venom-paralyzed him before throwing him into a caged truck. The party was transported to the Itju-Sha hideout: a settlement built into the colossal skeleton of a desert leviathan.

In the cage, they met [[Ouraeus]], who identified himself as a [[Medji-Tzentak]] — "Sons of the Keeper" — and claimed it was his destiny to help them escape, but only after they survived the fighting pit. Guards dragged [[Rik]] into the arena against [[Tlincalli]]'s champion, a massive half-orc fresh from an execution. What followed was stunning teamwork: unarmed, Rik baited the goliath; [[Wild Fury]] cast Command to make him drop his weapon; [[Icarus]] landed Hold Person; and Rik decapitated the champion with the warrior's own weapon on a critical hit.

Amused, [[Tlincalli]] threw [[Icarus]] into the pit to face the victorious [[Rik]]. Realizing his earlier Wild Magic surge would *reincarnate* him on death, Icarus knelt and invited the blow. Rik obliged — decapitating his friend and stunning the crowd into silence.

## Key Developments

- Captured by the [[Itju-Sha]]; gear confiscated.
- [[Ouraeus]] and the [[Medji-Tzentak]] introduced.
- [[Rik]] became pit champion; [[Icarus]] died (again) betting on Wild Magic reincarnation.
