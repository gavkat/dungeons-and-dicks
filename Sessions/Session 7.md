# Session 7: Finders & Keepers

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** Captured by the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md) in [Anauroch](Anauroch.md), the party meets [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md) — and [Rik](Rik.md) decapitates [Icarus](Icarus.md) in the fighting pit.

## Summary

The party awoke not dead, but washed ashore in the scorching desert of [Anauroch](Anauroch.md), stripped of their gear and surrounded by the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md) — the Takers of the Sand — a gang in scrap-metal armor with crude sand vehicles. [Icarus](Icarus.md)'s Minor Illusion act of defiance ("We will get on the truck!") drew the attention of their leader, the scorpion-man [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md), who flailed, chain-grappled, and venom-paralyzed him before throwing him into a caged truck. The party was transported to the Itju-Sha hideout: a settlement built into the colossal skeleton of a desert leviathan.

In the cage, they met [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md), who identified himself as a [Medji-Tzentak](Medji-Tzentak.md) — "Sons of the Keeper" — and claimed it was his destiny to help them escape, but only after they survived the fighting pit. Guards dragged [Rik](Rik.md) into the arena against [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md)'s champion, a massive half-orc fresh from an execution. What followed was stunning teamwork: unarmed, Rik baited the goliath; [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) cast Command to make him drop his weapon; [Icarus](Icarus.md) landed Hold Person; and Rik decapitated the champion with the warrior's own weapon on a critical hit.

Amused, [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md) threw [Icarus](Icarus.md) into the pit to face the victorious [Rik](Rik.md). Realizing his earlier Wild Magic surge would *reincarnate* him on death, Icarus knelt and invited the blow. Rik obliged — decapitating his friend and stunning the crowd into silence.

## Key Developments

- Captured by the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md); gear confiscated.
- [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md) and the [Medji-Tzentak](Medji-Tzentak.md) introduced.
- [Rik](Rik.md) became pit champion; [Icarus](Icarus.md) died (again) betting on Wild Magic reincarnation.
