# Session 6: Devoured

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** Farewell to [[Koa]], prophetic dreams at sea, the [[Sovereign Prophet]]'s ultimatum — and the raft swallowed whole.

## Summary

The party confirmed [[Polly]] — who *bamfs* away whenever [[Icarus]] tries to "cuddle it with his teeth" — as the source of the "pieces of six" whispers. Handled gently, she landed on Icarus's shoulder and called him "daddy." [[Koa]] urged them to stay on [[Mintan]], refusing to face the sea and [[The Great Devourer]], but the [[Boner Bros]] planned their escape. [[Dave]] was too "wrecked" to fly, and a PTERODACTYL KOA polymorph plan failed on spell-duration grounds, so they resigned themselves to a raft. They foraged for two days (poisonous fish, more tainted Ankheg meat, and "10 human kebabs" made from the dead mage guards). A now surprisingly eloquent [[Koa]] bid them a tearful farewell.

At sea, the night brought visions: [[Rik]] dreamt of an oasis where water turned to blood; [[Icarus]] dreamt of a kaleidoscopic world and a yellow butterfly. Icarus woke voiceless to find [[Gearsley]] magically held by [[Skum]] as the [[Sovereign Prophet]] boarded, flanked by sahuagin. While the others slept, the Prophet delivered an ultimatum: **Icarus has one lunar cycle to find the thief who stole the amulet and deliver them to him** — this "tithe" would pay his debt. As a final clue, the Prophet advised him to "head northeast." Then massive tentacles destroyed the raft, and the party was consumed by a black hole lined with teeth.

## Key Developments

- [[Skum]] revealed to be serving the [[Sovereign Prophet]].
- The Prophet's ultimatum hangs over [[Icarus]]: deliver the amulet thief ([[Blackjack]], unknowingly) within one lunar cycle.
- Prophetic dreams: blood oasis ([[Rik]]) and the yellow butterfly ([[Icarus]]).
- The party was swallowed by [[The Great Devourer]] — and survived.
