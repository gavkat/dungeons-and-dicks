# Session 6: Devoured

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** Farewell to [Koa](Koa.md), prophetic dreams at sea, the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md)'s ultimatum — and the raft swallowed whole.

## Summary

The party confirmed [Polly](Polly.md) — who *bamfs* away whenever [Icarus](Icarus.md) tries to "cuddle it with his teeth" — as the source of the "pieces of six" whispers. Handled gently, she landed on Icarus's shoulder and called him "daddy." [Koa](Koa.md) urged them to stay on [Mintan](Mintan.md), refusing to face the sea and [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md), but the [Boner Bros](Boner%20Bros.md) planned their escape. [Dave](Dave.md) was too "wrecked" to fly, and a PTERODACTYL KOA polymorph plan failed on spell-duration grounds, so they resigned themselves to a raft. They foraged for two days (poisonous fish, more tainted Ankheg meat, and "10 human kebabs" made from the dead mage guards). A now surprisingly eloquent [Koa](Koa.md) bid them a tearful farewell.

At sea, the night brought visions: [Rik](Rik.md) dreamt of an oasis where water turned to blood; [Icarus](Icarus.md) dreamt of a kaleidoscopic world and a yellow butterfly. Icarus woke voiceless to find [Gearsley](Gearsley.md) magically held by [Skum](Skum.md) as the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md) boarded, flanked by sahuagin. While the others slept, the Prophet delivered an ultimatum: **Icarus has one lunar cycle to find the thief who stole the amulet and deliver them to him** — this "tithe" would pay his debt. As a final clue, the Prophet advised him to "head northeast." Then massive tentacles destroyed the raft, and the party was consumed by a black hole lined with teeth.

## Key Developments

- [Skum](Skum.md) revealed to be serving the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md).
- The Prophet's ultimatum hangs over [Icarus](Icarus.md): deliver the amulet thief ([Blackjack](Blackjack.md), unknowingly) within one lunar cycle.
- Prophetic dreams: blood oasis ([Rik](Rik.md)) and the yellow butterfly ([Icarus](Icarus.md)).
- The party was swallowed by [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md) — and survived.
