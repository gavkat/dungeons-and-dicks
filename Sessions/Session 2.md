# Session 2: Boner Bros

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** Brawls, wrestling, and bonding aboard [The Tempest's Temptation](The%20Tempest's%20Temptation.md) as the ship sails into "the choke."

## Summary

After escaping the chaos at [](Leilon.md#The%20Crow's%20Nest|The%20Crow's%20Nest), the party boarded [The Tempest's Temptation](The%20Tempest's%20Temptation.md) under the eccentric [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md). Fleeing [Leilon](Leilon.md) unearthed deep trauma for [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md), who recalled failing to save civilians from a dragon attack there five years prior.

[Icarus](Icarus.md)'s clumsy attempt to lure [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) with an illusion of gold backfired into a massive crew brawl. Watching [Rik](Rik.md) fight reminded [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) of childhood memories with his father watching the wrestling hero "Bonecrusher" — happy memories that soured into panic. [Rik](Rik.md) came to his aid and the two bonded, dubbing themselves the [Boner Bros](Boner%20Bros.md).

[Icarus](Icarus.md) proposed stealing the Captain's prized macaw ([Polly](Polly.md)), claiming it as a family heirloom, recruiting [Blackjack](Blackjack.md), [Rik](Rik.md) and [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md). [Blackjack](Blackjack.md)'s conditions: they do it his way, and he gets the confiscated [Sending Phone](Sending%20Phone.md) back. The plan drew the ire of First Mate [Krell](Krell.md) and Bosun [Sauriv](Sauriv.md), who challenged [Rik](Rik.md) and [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) to a tag-team wrestling match. [Icarus](Icarus.md) used magic to aid his friends, but his attempt to influence the Captain was countered by the slimy Quartermaster [Skum](Skum.md) — revealing another magic user aboard. The Boner Bros won, but victory triggered another incapacitating flashback for [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md).

Meanwhile a stealthy [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) followed the Captain and [Skum](Skum.md) below deck, overhearing talk of an approaching navigational hazard and the Captain's words: *"we'll make it work, Skum, we always do."* The session closed as the ship sailed into dense fog, approaching [The Choke](The%20Choke.md).

## Key Developments

- [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md)'s trauma: failed to save civilians from a dragon attack on [Leilon](Leilon.md) five years ago.
- The [Boner Bros](Boner%20Bros.md) alliance formed.
- [Skum](Skum.md) revealed as a magic user; he and [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md) are hiding something.
- Plot to steal [Polly](Polly.md) established.
