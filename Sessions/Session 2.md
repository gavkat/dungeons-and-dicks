# Session 2: Boner Bros

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** Brawls, wrestling, and bonding aboard [[The Tempest's Temptation]] as the ship sails into "the choke."

## Summary

After escaping the chaos at [[Leilon#The Crow's Nest|The Crow's Nest]], the party boarded [[The Tempest's Temptation]] under the eccentric [[Captain Siren Shellsword]]. Fleeing [[Leilon]] unearthed deep trauma for [[Wild Fury]], who recalled failing to save civilians from a dragon attack there five years prior.

[[Icarus]]'s clumsy attempt to lure [[Blackjack]] with an illusion of gold backfired into a massive crew brawl. Watching [[Rik]] fight reminded [[Wild Fury]] of childhood memories with his father watching the wrestling hero "Bonecrusher" — happy memories that soured into panic. [[Rik]] came to his aid and the two bonded, dubbing themselves the [[Boner Bros]].

[[Icarus]] proposed stealing the Captain's prized macaw ([[Polly]]), claiming it as a family heirloom, recruiting [[Blackjack]], [[Rik]] and [[Wild Fury]]. [[Blackjack]]'s conditions: they do it his way, and he gets the confiscated [[Sending Phone]] back. The plan drew the ire of First Mate [[Krell]] and Bosun [[Sauriv]], who challenged [[Rik]] and [[Wild Fury]] to a tag-team wrestling match. [[Icarus]] used magic to aid his friends, but his attempt to influence the Captain was countered by the slimy Quartermaster [[Skum]] — revealing another magic user aboard. The Boner Bros won, but victory triggered another incapacitating flashback for [[Wild Fury]].

Meanwhile a stealthy [[Blackjack]] followed the Captain and [[Skum]] below deck, overhearing talk of an approaching navigational hazard and the Captain's words: *"we'll make it work, Skum, we always do."* The session closed as the ship sailed into dense fog, approaching [[The Choke]].

## Key Developments

- [[Wild Fury]]'s trauma: failed to save civilians from a dragon attack on [[Leilon]] five years ago.
- The [[Boner Bros]] alliance formed.
- [[Skum]] revealed as a magic user; he and [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] are hiding something.
- Plot to steal [[Polly]] established.
