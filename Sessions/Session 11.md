# Session 11: Rotten Hearts

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md)'s betrayal, [Tzentak](Tzentak.md)'s rotten heart, a charmed [Rik](Rik.md) — and the desert replaced by frozen tundra.

## Summary

The session began in the "Hallway of the Keeper," where [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) acrobatically sprinted through traps screaming "Jack-Attack!" while [Icarus](Icarus.md) crawled undignifiedly to safety. The party barely survived a crushing boulder trap by deploying the [Immovable Rod](Immovable%20Rod.md) at the last second. Deeper inside, they trapped the hostile genie [Kaza-Rul](Kaza-Rul.md) in an obsidian jar and recovered a [Mysterious Book](Mysterious%20Book.md).

The true nightmare began when [Rik](Rik.md), exploring a hidden cavern alone, found [Tzentak](Tzentak.md)'s beating rotten heart and was instantly charmed, flooded with visions of Tzentak's origins and his deal with [The Dark Six](The%20Dark%20Six.md). [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md), with a snake-charmer's tune, revealed himself as the true [Medji-Tzentak](Medji-Tzentak.md) — confessing he had slaughtered his own village as repentance and meant to revive the Pharaoh — and led the charmed Rik away.

In the final battle, [Rik](Rik.md) turned his blade on the party to protect the heart. [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) fought through his panic to take a final, divine stand. [Icarus](Icarus.md), recovering from a Wild Magic surge that turned him into a potted plant, polymorphed Wild Fury into a Giant Ape. Desperate for firepower, the party released [Kaza-Rul](Kaza-Rul.md) to turn the tide. The Giant Ape pummeled [Tzentak](Tzentak.md); [Gearsley](Gearsley.md) gunned down the Naga ([Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md)); [Rik](Rik.md) dove to shield the heart, but [Icarus](Icarus.md)'s Hold Person froze him, giving [Gearsley](Gearsley.md) the opening to shoot the heart and end Tzentak for good.

The victory triggered a dread-filled Wild Magic surge: an explosion of visions of a city consumed by darkness and evil. The party awoke to find the desert gone, replaced by frozen tundra. *Hell hath frozen over.*

## Key Developments

- [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md) revealed as a betrayer — a naga seeking to revive [Tzentak](Tzentak.md) — and was gunned down by [Gearsley](Gearsley.md).
- [Tzentak](Tzentak.md) destroyed when his heart was shot.
- [Kaza-Rul](Kaza-Rul.md) captured, then released in bargain; the [Mysterious Book](Mysterious%20Book.md) recovered.
- Wild Magic vision: a city consumed by darkness.
- The party mysteriously transported from [Anauroch](Anauroch.md) to a frozen tundra.
