# Session 11: Rotten Hearts

**Tags:** #session

**In short:** [[Ouraeus]]'s betrayal, [[Tzentak]]'s rotten heart, a charmed [[Rik]] — and the desert replaced by frozen tundra.

## Summary

The session began in the "Hallway of the Keeper," where [[Blackjack]] acrobatically sprinted through traps screaming "Jack-Attack!" while [[Icarus]] crawled undignifiedly to safety. The party barely survived a crushing boulder trap by deploying the [[Immovable Rod]] at the last second. Deeper inside, they trapped the hostile genie [[Kaza-Rul]] in an obsidian jar and recovered a [[Mysterious Book]].

The true nightmare began when [[Rik]], exploring a hidden cavern alone, found [[Tzentak]]'s beating rotten heart and was instantly charmed, flooded with visions of Tzentak's origins and his deal with [[The Dark Six]]. [[Ouraeus]], with a snake-charmer's tune, revealed himself as the true [[Medji-Tzentak]] — confessing he had slaughtered his own village as repentance and meant to revive the Pharaoh — and led the charmed Rik away.

In the final battle, [[Rik]] turned his blade on the party to protect the heart. [[Wild Fury]] fought through his panic to take a final, divine stand. [[Icarus]], recovering from a Wild Magic surge that turned him into a potted plant, polymorphed Wild Fury into a Giant Ape. Desperate for firepower, the party released [[Kaza-Rul]] to turn the tide. The Giant Ape pummeled [[Tzentak]]; [[Gearsley]] gunned down the Naga ([[Ouraeus]]); [[Rik]] dove to shield the heart, but [[Icarus]]'s Hold Person froze him, giving [[Gearsley]] the opening to shoot the heart and end Tzentak for good.

The victory triggered a dread-filled Wild Magic surge: an explosion of visions of a city consumed by darkness and evil. The party awoke to find the desert gone, replaced by frozen tundra. *Hell hath frozen over.*

## Key Developments

- [[Ouraeus]] revealed as a betrayer — a naga seeking to revive [[Tzentak]] — and was gunned down by [[Gearsley]].
- [[Tzentak]] destroyed when his heart was shot.
- [[Kaza-Rul]] captured, then released in bargain; the [[Mysterious Book]] recovered.
- Wild Magic vision: a city consumed by darkness.
- The party mysteriously transported from [[Anauroch]] to a frozen tundra.
