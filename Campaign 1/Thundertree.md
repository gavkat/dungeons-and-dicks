# Thundertree

**Tags:** #Location #Campaign1

---

## Overview
A ruined town overrun by giant spiders and shrouded in [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s tragic past. The [[Wolfpack]] came here seeking directions to [[Cragmaw Castle]] and a contact named [[Elder Reidoth|Reidoth]] — and found instead a cult of [[Silvanus]] that had sacrificed the town's own inhabitants to their nature god. For Frederick it was a homecoming of grief: among the cultists stood his old friend [[Elder Marcus]] and his ex-wife [[Sister Fredericka|Fredericka]]. It was also where the party recovered the silver comb owed to [[Agatha the Hag|Agatha]].

## Sub-locations
- **The Herbologist's Shop** — a herbalist's store where giant spiders were fought and the silver comb was found (claimable by all but [[Glenn]]).

## Session History

### Session 9

The cultists' refused bargain — Frederick's allegiance and life sacrificed to [[Silvanus]] — sparked a battle of summoned zombies and a colossal tree monster. Reidoth and Fredericka fell to [[Walter & Kyle|Walter and Kyle]]; Marcus charged to his death; [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s Fist of the Fire Snake felled the tree monster.

### Session 10
