# Wyvern Tor

**Tags:** #Location #Campaign1

---

## Overview
A peak of profound magic, hunting-ground of wyverns and the source of the holy water that fed [[Leilon]]'s Fountain of Fortune. The Vox Spiritus directed the [[Wolfpack]] here as the place where [[Prometheus]] could attune to the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]] — and it became the stage for the great battle against the false goddess and her dragon. Climbing its slopes, [[Wild Fury]] uncovered the origins of the [[Lionshield Coster]] and saved the wyvern egg that would one day hatch into [[Dave]].

## Sub-locations
- **The Mountain Alcove** — a cave guarded by ember skeletons, remnants of past adventurers claimed by wyverns; where [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] rejoined the party.
- **The Hidden Vision-Wall** — a translucent wall only Wild Fury could see; beyond it, a vision of his grandfather, the Lionshield Coster's origin, and the slain wyvern whose egg ([[Dave]]) he claimed.
- **The Summit** — where Prometheus attuned the Spellbook and the battle of the Vox Spiriti was fought.
