# Wand of True Polymorph

**Tags:** #Item #Campaign1

---

## Overview
A **modified** wand of True Polymorph, lying at the heart of [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]]'s schemes from the very first session to the last. In the wrong hands it warped flesh and matter alike — a tool of panic, transformation and, ultimately, godhood.

It first appeared in the Prequel as the weapon of Shinebright's mad apprentice [[Arnolt Noke|Noke]], who used it to conjure a **dragon made of bedsheets, bedposts and linen** — and then, in a last-ditch act, to transform himself into a monstrous **Gibbering Mouther**. [[Ethel Elkhid|Ethel]] turned the wand to gentler use, restoring the cursed-sheep Shinebright to human form. Far later, Shinebright wielded the wand at the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] to complete his terrible self-transformation.

## Session History

### Prequel: The Wild Sheep's Chase
- Noke used the modified wand to summon the bed-dragon, then to turn himself into a Gibbering Mouther; after his defeat, Ethel used it to restore Shinebright to human form.

### Session 25: The Sheep & The Spider
- With the Wand of True Polymorph and the Forge's power, Shinebright grotesquely transformed into a Giant **Drider** — half-human, half-spider — becoming the true Black Spider.
