## Overview
A city of the Sword Coast and the site of the **Saint's Day fair**, where the whole tale began. Amid the festival crowds, fate gathered an unlikely band: [[Ethel Elkhid|Ethel]] the gothic dwarf wizard, [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] the Tortle cleric bellowing praises to [[Silvanus]], [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] the blind monk listening to ranger gossip of [[The Redbrands|the Redbrands]]' return, and [[Dew-Dew Bun]] the fairy bard with her bodyguard [[Switchblade]].

The merriment broke when a frantic sheep — secretly the wizard [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] — bleated a warning, just as [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]] and his beastly entourage crashed the fair in pursuit of his brother [[Guz Nightfoot|Guz]]. The heroes leapt into the fray together, and from that chaos the [[Wolfpack]] was born.
