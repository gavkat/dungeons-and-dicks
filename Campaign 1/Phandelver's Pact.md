# Phandelver's Pact

**Tags:** #Lore #WorldBuilding #Campaign1

---

## Overview
An ancient alliance of legend — the stalwart clans of **dwarves and gnomes**, joined by **human spellcasters** — forged in an age long forgotten, when magic still coursed through the very rock and soil. Together they hewed [[Wave Echo Cave]] from the earth, a mine of unparalleled richness, and within it birthed the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]], a crucible where sorcery was woven into the fabric of creation. In the Forge's shadow, the land of [[Phandalin]] flourished in peace and prosperity.

The Pact's golden age ended when a horde of goblins and orcs surged from the North under the war god [[Maglubiyet]]. In their direst hour the human wizards used the Forge to birth an army of **warforged** — beings of metal and magic — but Maglubiyet's bargain with [[The Dark Six]] dimmed the Forge's light, and when silence finally claimed the battlefield, Wave Echo Cave was lost to the annals of time. [[King Dumathoin]] led the dwarves in that defence, and his line endures in the [[Gundren Rockseeker|Rockseeker]] brothers.

## Known History
- **The Founding** — dwarves, gnomes and humans hewed Wave Echo Cave and built the Forge of Spells; Phandalin prospered above.
- **The War** — Maglubiyet's horde invaded; the wizards birthed the warforged; the Forge dimmed under the Dark Six's influence; the cave was buried and forgotten.
- **The Legacy** — the echoes of the forge still linger as a siren's call; [[Prometheus]], himself a warforged of the Forge, carried the Pact's lore to the Wolfpack.
