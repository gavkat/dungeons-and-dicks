# Lost Mine of Phandelver

**Tags:** #Campaign #Hub #Campaign1

> The first campaign in the world — the chronicle of the [[Wolfpack]]. Its sequel, set five years later, is [[Echoes of Phandelver]].

---

## The Premise

Long ago, dwarves, gnomes, and humans forged the [[Phandelver's Pact]] and built the [[Wave Echo Cave|Forge of Spells]] beneath what is now [[Phandalin]] — until an orc horde under [[Maglubiyet]] and the [[The Dark Six|Dark Six]] buried it all ([[Prologue]]).

Centuries later, a band of strangers met at the [[Daggerford]] fair, broke a wizard's curse, and learned too late that the helpless "sheep" they'd saved was [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]] ([[Prequel - The Wild Sheep's Chase|Prequel]]). Reunited a year on to escort [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]]'s cargo, the newly minted **[[Wolfpack]]** were pulled into a race for the rediscovered [[Wave Echo Cave]] — and a war against the Black Spider for the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]] and the godlike power of the Forge.
