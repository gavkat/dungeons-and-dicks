# Old Owl Well

**Tags:** #Location #Campaign1

---

## Overview
A mysterious, seemingly abandoned well standing in an expansive open field, and the entrance to a dungeon of mind-bending trials. The [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox Spiritus]] teleported the [[Wolfpack]] here after [[Cragmaw Castle]], charging them to retrieve [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|Mordenkainen's spellbook]] and deliver it to her at [[Wyvern Tor]].

To enter, the party had to drink the well's water — a potent magical elixir that muddled thought, memory, time and motor function — and fell unconscious, waking inside the dungeon proper. What followed was a gauntlet of riddles and trials probing each hero's deepest self, culminating at a grand watchtower overlooking the Sword Coast where [[The Bookkeeper]] waited.

## Sub-locations
- **The Well Chamber** — a room centred on the well, with a mirror-bearing statue and an ominous ceiling inscription; Glenn's curiosity nearly drowned him before the statue's puzzle was solved.
- **The Trials** — an endless staircase (escaped by "flipping and reversing" via thieves' cant), a tortured figure freed by melody, a red-painted mirror revealing the ally "Richard", and a Beholder's beauty riddle.
- **The Separated Trials** — abysses where each hero faced a tailored test: the **Spark of Curiosity** ([[Prometheus]]), the **Tides of Balance** ([[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]), the **Roar of Pride** ([[Wild Fury]]), and the **Flame of Passion** ([[Glenn]]).
- **The Watchtower Summit** — where the Bookkeeper bestowed the Spellbook and revealed the Ilefarn Prophecy.

## Session History

### Session 15: Son of a Grick
- The Vox Spiritus of "Tymora" teleported the party here at the end of the session.

### Session 16: Dazed and Confused
- The Wolfpack drank the water and entered the dungeon; the well-chamber drowning puzzle, the endless staircase, the freed figure, the red mirror, "Richard", and the Beholder beauty riddle. They were separated into dark abysses for their next trial.

### Session 17: The Light Beyond
- Each hero passed their tailored trial and earned a gift of self. Reunited, they shattered the elemental guardian construct and answered the Scarlet Great-horned Owl riddle to reach the summit, where the Bookkeeper gave Prometheus the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]], revealed the Ilefarn Prophecy and Ilbryn as the Elven Oracle, unmasked [[Halia]] as his daughter, and gifted elemental rings of resistance. The party set off for [[Leilon]] — and on the road, a Drow posing as Halia poisoned Wild Fury.
