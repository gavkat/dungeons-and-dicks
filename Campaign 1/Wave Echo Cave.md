# Wave Echo Cave

**Tags:** #Location #Campaign1

---

## Overview
The legendary lost mine — a cavern of wonders hewn from the earth in an age long forgotten by the dwarves and gnomes of the [[Phandelver's Pact]]. At its heart lies the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]], the arcane crucible where human spellcasters, in brotherhood with their dwarven and gnomish kin, wove sorcery into the fabric of creation.

Five hundred years ago a horde of orcs and goblins surged from the North under the war-god [[Maglubiyet]], who had struck a bargain with [[The Dark Six]]. In their direst hour the defenders, led by [[King Dumathoin]], used the Forge to birth an army of **warforged** wizards — but the battle was lost, and Wave Echo Cave was buried beneath rubble and ruin, its location forgotten until the Rockseeker brothers rediscovered it.

It became the campaign's climactic dungeon: the destination of the whole quest, where the [[Wolfpack]] descended to stop [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]] from claiming the Forge.

## Sub-locations
- **The Bridge over the Starry Cavern** — a water-slick span hundreds of meters high over crashing waves, beset by skeletal hands, spiders and bat-like creatures.
- **The Hall of Phandelver's Pact** — a shattered Dwarven hall sealed with ancient runes, its centre dominated by the massive skull of [[Maglubiyet]]; the war room here held the jade-frog key.
- **The Smeltery** — sweltering furnace chamber where cultists fed gold to the flames.
- **The Throne Room** — where ten cultists merged into a Beholder and [[King Dumathoin]]'s statue stood guard.
- **The Forge of Spells** — the brass-tracked heart of the mine; site of the ancient Ilefarn *"tri animalus arcanum"* ritual and the final battle.

## Echoes of Phandelver
This is the birthplace of the new industrial age. When [[Prometheus]] chose *Conjunction* and reignited the Forge of Spells, he unleashed long-forgotten magic back into the world — ushering in the era of technology that defines [[Echoes of Phandelver]], where the rediscovered mines pump *"a new flame, a new life force, into civilisation and humanity."*

### Session 25
