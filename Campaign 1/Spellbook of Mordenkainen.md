# Spellbook of Mordenkainen

**Tags:** #Item #Campaign1

---

## Overview
The legendary spellbook of the archmage [[Mordenkainen]], and the great quest object of the campaign. [[Sister Garaele]]'s vision first named it as the one thing that could save the lost mine and stop [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]], setting the [[Wolfpack]] on its trail by way of [[Agatha the Hag|Agatha]], [[Old Owl Well]] and its guardian [[The Bookkeeper]] — who had sworn to watch over the book until [[Prometheus]]'s arrival and declared it Prometheus's destiny.

At [[Wyvern Tor]], Prometheus attuned to the book; it erupted in arcane flames and surged power through his warforged body, and from its heart he could draw a radiant longsword (Mordenkainen's Sword). Its purpose was to reignite the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] — though the false [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox Spiritus]] coveted it for her own ends.
