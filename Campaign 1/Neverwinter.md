**Tags:** #Location #Campaign1

## Overview
The great city from which the [[Wolfpack]] set out on the road proper. After their year apart, the heroes reunited here at the behest of [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] and [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]], who hired them to escort mysterious cargo bound for [[Phandalin]] and tied to the Lost Mines of [[Phandelver]]. Their departure down the High Road was almost immediately interrupted by the ambush that ended with [[Prometheus]]'s reawakening.

Much later the party returned for a brief, well-earned reprieve — a week-long celebratory stopover en route from [[Thundertree]] to [[Conyberry]], where (true to the city's reputation) it snowed.
