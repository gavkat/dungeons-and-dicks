# Frederick Iron-Thunder

**Tags:** #character #npc #pc #Campaign1

## Description
**Tortle/Cleric.** A booming-voiced cleric once devoted to [[Silvanus]], Frederick abandoned his old religion to chase his love, [[Betty]], and to emulate his hero, [[Lightning Steel]]. He wields [[Lightbringer]], the holy sword reforged from [[Iarno Albrek|the Glass Knight]]'s "Refraction."

## Role in Campaign
A founding member of the [[Wolfpack]], Frederick's arc is one of death, rebirth, and reluctant retirement. In the crypts beneath [[Cragmaw Castle]], his idol [[Lightning Steel]] cut him down — and slashed the throat of [[Betty]] before his eyes. In the abyss Frederick met Lathander, who offered revival; his sword "Refraction" transformed into the legendary [[Lightbringer]], and a single sunbeam obliterated the vampire. He resurrected Betty, learned of her pregnancy, and chose family over adventure, **leaving the party in Session 11** and entrusting Lightbringer to [[Wild Fury]] in exchange for a giant Elk steed.

He **returned in Session 20**, cradled by [[Sister Garaele]], swooping into Wyvern Tor to vanquish ember skeletons and reclaim Lightbringer (after some squabbling). He stood with the Wolfpack through the fall of the Black Spider, his blinded Sunbeam guided true by an unseen voice: *"There is always light in the darkness, for you are the Lightbringer."* **Status: Alive — and incarcerated.**

## Echoes of Phandelver
The epilogue finds Frederick's heroics having driven Betty away, leaving him partial custody of Fred Jr. At a playground, the **MageGuard** — a squad of militant wizards — swarm and arrest him for the murder of [[Lightning Steel]]. His fate carries directly into [[Echoes of Phandelver]].

## Session History

### Prequel: The Wild Sheep's Chase
- Introduced at the Daggerford fair as a Silvanus-praising cleric; fell and was revived during the clash at [[Littlefoot]].

### Session 1
- Reunited with the party at [[Neverwinter]]; tense first meeting with [[Prometheus]] ended in trust over a common goal.

### Session 11
- Killed by [[Lightning Steel]], revived by Lathander; "Refraction" became [[Lightbringer]]; destroyed the vampire and resurrected [[Betty]]. Left the party, giving Lightbringer to [[Wild Fury]].

### Session 20
- Returned with [[Sister Garaele]] at [[Wyvern Tor]]; slew the ember skeletons and reclaimed Lightbringer.

### Session 21
- Sensed the avalanche and used his storm-born powers to help the party escape toward [[Wave Echo Cave]].

### Session 22
- Present as [[Prometheus]] received the journal visions of [[Mordenkainen]], [[Szass Tam]] and [[King Dumathoin]].

### Session 24
- Bled by [[Talice]] in disguise; named by the statue of [[King Dumathoin]] as the bearer of Lightbringer, the king's own creation.

### Session 25
- Blinded by the Black Spider's Prismatic Spray, yet guided by an unseen voice his Sunbeam struck true in the final blow.

### Epilogue: 3 Years Later
- Arrested by the MageGuard for the murder of [[Lightning Steel]].
