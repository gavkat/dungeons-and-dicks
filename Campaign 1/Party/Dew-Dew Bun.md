# Dew-Dew Bun

**Tags:** #character #npc #pc #Campaign1

## Description
**Fairy/Bard.** A fairy bard of great renown, gifted a spiritual, enchanting lute whose music carried her fame across oceans, lands and planes. A prequel-era member of the [[Wolfpack]], shadowed everywhere by her bodyguard [[Switchblade]].

## Role in Campaign
Dew-Dew Bun's performance at the Daggerford fair was the spark that drew the future Wolfpack together — interrupted first by a warning-bleating sheep ([[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] in disguise) and then by [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]]'s beastly entourage crashing the crowd. Her fame also made her the obsession of [[Arnolt Noke|Noke]], whose mania over her drove him to summon zombies clad in her merchandise. When the prequel ended and [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] silenced her with a spell and froze the party in time, Dew-Dew Bun's thread was cut. **Status: Whereabouts unknown.**

## Session History

### Prequel: The Wild Sheep's Chase
- Performed at the Daggerford fair under [[Switchblade]]'s protection; the unwitting object of [[Arnolt Noke|Noke]]'s obsession. Silenced by [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] as he froze the party in time; her fate thereafter is unknown.
