# Ethel Elkhid

**Tags:** #character #npc #pc #Campaign1

## Description
**Dwarf/Wizard.** A gothic dwarf wizard who ran away from home young and discovered her magical aptitude only after stealing a mysterious Spellbook from an old man. A prequel-era member of the [[Wolfpack]].

## Role in Campaign
Ethel was part of the original band drawn together at the Daggerford fair. During the clash at [[Littlefoot]] she fell alongside [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]], only to be revived by her companions. After the defeat of [[Arnolt Noke|Noke]], it was Ethel who wielded the [[Wand of True Polymorph]] to restore [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] to human form — unwittingly unmasking the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]] himself. When the party splintered in the awkward aftermath, Ethel went her own way and did not rejoin the campaign proper. **Status: Unknown** (last seen as the party fractured).

## Session History

### Prequel: The Wild Sheep's Chase
- Navigated the Daggerford Saint's Day fair; fought through the ruins of [[Littlefoot]], fell and was revived; used the [[Wand of True Polymorph]] to turn [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] human, exposing his true nature as the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]]. Departed when the party splintered.
