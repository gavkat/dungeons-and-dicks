# Silvanus

**Tags:** #Deity #Campaign1

---

## Overview
A nature god, and the faith [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] once served before he abandoned his religion to pursue his love [[Betty]] and emulate his hero [[Lightning Steel]]. Frederick first appeared at the [[Daggerford]] fair with a booming voice singing Silvanus's praises.

Silvanus is also the dark god of the **Thundertree cult**, led by [[Elder Reidoth|Reidoth]], who held that the god demanded blood. The cult had sacrificed the entire town of [[Thundertree]] to him and sought to add Frederick to the offering — including Frederick's ex-wife [[Sister Fredericka|Fredericka]] and his old friend [[Elder Marcus|Marcus]], gone mad in the god's service.

## Session History

### Prequel: The Wild Sheep's Chase
- Frederick introduced as a Tortle cleric of Silvanus.

### Session 9: May Silvanus Open Wide and Let You In
- At Thundertree, Reidoth, Marcus and Fredericka revealed the sacrifice of the town's inhabitants to Silvanus and offered the party directions to [[Cragmaw Castle]] in exchange for Frederick's allegiance and life. The refusal sparked a ritual that summoned zombies and animated a colossal tree monster; the cultists fell.
