# The Redbrands

**Tags:** #Faction #Campaign1

---

## Overview
An infamous gang of ruffians, silent for years until [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]] revived them to do his dirty work. Operating out of [[Phandalin]], where they oppressed the townsfolk from a hidden lair beneath Tresendar Manor, they were the muscle behind Shinebright's hunt for the Lost Mines. Their nominal leader was the **Glass Knight**, [[Iarno Albrek]], a Tiefling oathbreaker paladin and dissenter of the Lord's Alliance, hired by the Black Spider; he wielded the glass sword **Refraction**.

The gang first crossed the party in the [[Littlefoot]] ruins during the Prequel, where they were briefly led by [[Guz Nightfoot|Guz]]. Their reign of fear over Phandalin — the murder of woodcarver [[The Dendrar Family|Thel Dendrar]] and the imprisonment of his family among it — drove much of the early campaign.

## Notable Members
- [[Iarno Albrek|The Glass Knight]] — Tiefling oathbreaker paladin, the gang's leader; wielded Refraction. Deceased.
- [[Guz Nightfoot|Guz]] — Half-Orc, an early leader; murdered by [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] in the Prequel.
- [[Switchblade]] — a member of the newly-reformed Redbrands before becoming [[Dew-Dew Bun]]'s bodyguard.
- [[Droop]] — a goblin slave kept by the gang.
- The [[Order of the Gauntlet]] — in cahoots with the Redbrands.

## Session History

### Prequel: The Wild Sheep's Chase
- The party first clashed with the Redbrands at the Littlefoot ruins.

### Session 5: Revelations, Revolutions and Radicchio
- The Redbrands' illusions and gas bombs captured the Wolfpack; the Glass Knight interrogated them. A chaotic battle in Phandalin let the party escape into Tresendar Manor and down to the Redbrand lair.

### Session 6: The Thing About Bowties
- In the lair, a captured Redbrand revealed under [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s bowtie monologue that the Glass Knight also served the Black Spider.

### Session 8: You, Me and Droopy
- The Glass Knight was defeated; his sword Refraction passed to Frederick. The gang's grip on Phandalin broke.
