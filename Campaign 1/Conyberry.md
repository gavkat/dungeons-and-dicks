## Overview
The woods that hold the cottage and lair of [[Agatha the Hag|Agatha]], the vain hag who answers a single question in exchange for a silver comb. Drawn here by [[Sister Garaele]]'s vision that Mordenkainen's spellbook could save the lost mine, the [[Wolfpack]] sought out Agatha's whereabouts-knowledge — and paid her toll.

## Session History

### Session 10: It Only Snows in Neverwinter
- Arriving from [[Thundertree]] (by way of a stopover in [[Neverwinter]]), the party reached Agatha's lair seeking word of [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|Mordenkainen's spellbook]]. They bartered the silver comb and received only a cryptic riddle in return; [[Prometheus]]'s impatience nearly drew the hag's wrath before a swift apology spared them.
- On the road out, the Cragmaw tribe ambushed them and seized [[Glenn]].
