# Littlefoot

**Tags:** #Location #Campaign1

---

## Overview
[[Glenn]]'s home village, and the first proper trial of the fledgling [[Wolfpack]]. Following the cursed-sheep [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] out of the [[Daggerford]] fair and through the forest, the party reached Littlefoot's ruins — where Glenn confronted the remnants of his past and the group clashed with [[The Redbrands|the Redbrands]]. [[Ethel Elkhid|Ethel]] and [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] fell here, only to be revived by their companions.

Years later, after a small stint on tour, Glenn found peace and retired to a cozy Littlefoot cottage, enjoying quiet days by the fire with [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]] — until the peace shattered.
