# Order of the Gauntlet

**Tags:** #Faction #Campaign1

---

## Overview
A troupe of thespian-adventurers led by [[Lightning Steel]] (Prince Darren Edermath), the acclaimed folk hero and actor of [[Phandalin]] — secretly a centuries-old vampire prince. Billed as foes of [[The Redbrands|the Redbrands]], the Order were in fact in cahoots with them, and their grand "ambush" of the gang proved a deadly piece of theatre that turned on the [[Wolfpack]]. Every member is now deceased.

## Members
- [[Lightning Steel]] — Prince Darren "Lightning Steel" Edermath, leader; vampire prince. Resurrected as a vampire after his staged death, finally destroyed by [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] and [[Lightbringer]].
- **Lady Esmerelda "Queen of Hearts" Quinnley** — slew Lightning Steel mid-monologue with a "Queen of Hearts" card during the staged ambush.
- **Phibeus "P.T. Bonesman" Tiberus** — begged for mercy in a peculiar manner after the Wolfpack prevailed.
- **Krazzix "Sweet Strings" Trudedor** — attacked the party alongside the resurrected vampire Lightning Steel.
- **Claudius Shakelspears III** — fought the party in the Redbrand lair beside Lady Esmerelda.

## Session History

### Session 4: The Kobold and the Bootyfull
- Lightning Steel met the party at his orchard, voicing his desire for vengeance against the Redbrands for ruining his theatrical endeavours.

### Session 5: Revelations, Revolutions and Radicchio
- The Order joined the Wolfpack's midnight ambush of the Redbrands — but illusions at the Sleeping Giant tavern turned it to disaster; Lightning Steel "died" mid-monologue to the Queen of Hearts card.

### Session 7: Never Meet Your Heroes
- Lady Esmerelda and Shakelspears ambushed the party in the lair; later Sweet Strings, P.T. Bonesman and a now-vampiric, resurrected Lightning Steel assaulted them. The heroes prevailed; Lightning Steel fled and P.T. Bonesman begged for mercy.
