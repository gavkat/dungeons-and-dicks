# The Redback Enclave

**Tags:** #Faction #Campaign1

---

## Overview
The red-cloaked cult of [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]], encountered in the Neverwinter Woods at the threshold of [[Wave Echo Cave]]. Where [[The Redbrands]] were the Spider's ruffian muscle, the Redback Enclave were his true believers — robed cultists chanting his rites and guarding the path to the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]].

The [[Wolfpack]] first met them at the cave entrance under [[Nezznar]]'s command, and later moved through their tunnels in stolen masks and robes. Their chants — *"Nisciens, non probatus, non ductus"* — echoed the ancient Ilefarn ritual the Black Spider sought to mirror, and ten of them merged into a Beholder in the throne room before the Forge.

## Notable Members
- [[Nezznar]] — Drow mage who led the cultists at the cave entrance before vanishing.
- [[Talice]] — Drow shapeshifter and the Spider's henchman.
- Rank-and-file cultists — guards, smelters feeding gold to the furnace, and ritualists who fused into a ten-eyed Beholder.

## Session History

### Session 21: Silence is Violence
- First encountered at Wave Echo Cave: Nezznar led a large group of red-cloaked cultists before disappearing. The party stealthily took out the entrance guards, though one summoned a cave troll.

### Session 22: Frederick and Mordy
- [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] and [[Glenn]] donned cultist garb to infiltrate the tunnels.

### Session 24: Human Resources
- Glenn shoved a cultist into the smeltery's molten pit during his "HR" infiltration; in the throne room, ten cultists completed a dark ritual and merged into a Beholder. The party defeated the horror, with the imposter Talice exposed by the "Meatloaf" codeword.

### Session 25: The Sheep & The Spider
- The remaining cultists chanted the Ilefarn ritual words as the Wolfpack reached the Forge, where the Black Spider made his final stand.
