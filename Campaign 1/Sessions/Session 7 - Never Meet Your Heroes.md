# Session 7: Never Meet Your Heroes

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** A telepathic [[The Nothic|Nothic]] turns ally as the [[Wolfpack]] uncover the legend of [[Lightbringer]] — then a vampiric [[Lightning Steel]] rises from the dead.

## Summary

Delving deeper into the [[The Redbrands|Redbrands']] lair, the [[Wolfpack]] encountered a telepathic [[The Nothic|Nothic]]. After a tense exchange it withdrew into a crevice, leaving the party to face Lady Esmerelda and Shakelspears of the [[Order of the Gauntlet]]. The skirmish sent [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] tumbling into the crevice, where he found a chest, a corpse, and the [[The Nothic|Nothic]] once more. Offered foes as food, the creature turned ally and helped defeat the ambushers.

The group then discovered a lab where [[Glenn]] — drawing on his time as a sales assistant for [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] — recognized failed polymorph potions. From the texts, the party learned of the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]], the sword [[Lightbringer]], and an elven prophecy: *"Efarn Aux Bryn Il,"* translating to *"the one to see beyond the light."*

Exiting, they were assaulted by Sweet Strings, P.T. Bonesman, and a vampiric, resurrected [[Lightning Steel]]. Despite a fierce fight and [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s fall, the heroes prevailed — though [[Lightning Steel]] fled, and P.T. Bonesman begged for mercy in a peculiar manner.

## Key Developments

- A telepathic [[The Nothic|Nothic]] became a reluctant ally.
- The party learned of the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]], the sword [[Lightbringer]], and the prophecy *"Efarn Aux Bryn Il."*
- [[Lightning Steel]] returned — as a vampire — and fled the fight.
