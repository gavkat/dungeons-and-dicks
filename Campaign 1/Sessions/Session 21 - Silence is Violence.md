# Session 21: Silence is Violence

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** The Vox of Beshaba makes one last grab for the Spellbook, the party flees an avalanche, and they finally reach Wave Echo Cave — only to find death waiting.

## Summary

Atop [[Wyvern Tor]], the [[Wolfpack]] felt a brief moment of peace. Before the descent, [[Sister Garaele]] revealed that, while in her Harpy form, she had cast a spell on [[Switchblade|the dragon]] that exposed its deepest desire — the silhouette of a fairy. [[Prometheus]], now attuned to the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|Spellbook]], felt arcane power surge through his Warforged body. The triumph was short-lived: the ground cracked open and the spectral form of the [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox Spiritus]] rose with a piercing wail — *"GIVE ME MY SPELLBOOK!"*

[[Glenn]] leapt to save Prometheus, anchoring himself to the ground and holding on as the Vox tried to drag the wizard away. Prometheus plunged his hand into the heart of the Spellbook, drawing forth a radiant longsword, and drove the blade into the spirit's face — banishing her in a burst of radiant light. Then [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] sensed a colossal avalanche; with a flash of lightning he warned the party. Prometheus polymorphed [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] into a Quetzalcoatlus and [[Halia]] wild-shaped into a giant eagle, and together they soared clear of the deadly slide.

Safe on the ground, the party took a long rest before the final leg toward [[Wave Echo Cave]]. At the entrance they spotted a band of red-cloaked [[The Redback Enclave|cultists]] led by the enigmatic [[Nezznar]], who vanished into thin air, leaving two guards behind. The Wolfpack stealthily took them out — but not before one cried for help, summoning a monstrous cave troll from the shadows. They defeated the beast, then interrogated a frantic [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] about the cave. In a desperate panic, Gundren ran inside, only to find his brother [[Nundro Rockseeker|Nundro]] dead, and his older brother [[Tharden Rockseeker|Tharden]] missing.

## Key Developments

- [[Sister Garaele]] revealed her Harpy spell had shown [[Switchblade]]'s deepest desire: a fairy's silhouette.
- The [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox of Beshaba]] returned for the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|Spellbook]]; [[Glenn]] saved [[Prometheus]], who banished her with a radiant sword drawn from the book.
- [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] warned of an avalanche; the party escaped by air via [[Prometheus]] and [[Halia]]'s transformations.
- The Wolfpack reached [[Wave Echo Cave]], where [[Nezznar]] and his [[The Redback Enclave|cultists]] guarded the entrance.
- They slew a cave troll, then [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] discovered [[Nundro Rockseeker|Nundro]] dead and [[Tharden Rockseeker|Tharden]] missing.
