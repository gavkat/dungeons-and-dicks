# Session 3: Meine Neuen Wolfsfreunde

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** Two new wolf friends, a dead second-in-command, and a bugbear ambushed in his own lair.

## Summary

The heroes ventured headfirst into [[Cragmaw Hideout]] to save [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] and [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]]. They bypassed wolves and faced a goblin ambush, where a flood swept away [[Prometheus]]. In an unlikely twist, [[Glenn]] tamed the wolves, [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] and [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]], and the group pressed on to find [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] held captive among goblins.

A fierce battle ensued. [[Yeemik]], the goblin second-in-command, tried to negotiate but ended up dead after a psionic dagger exchange with [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]. The group discovered treasures, including some belonging to "the [[Lionshield Coster]]."

[[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] revealed critical information: the Rockseeker brothers had found the lost [[Wave Echo Cave]], [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] had been taken to [[Cragmaw Castle]], and [[Klarg]] — the bugbear leader — was elsewhere in the cave, working for "the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]]," who wanted Gundren and the map to the cave's secret location. Sildar also named a contact in [[Phandalin]], [[Iarno Albrek]], who might help.

Using *speak with animals*, [[Glenn]] communicated with [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] and [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]], who deceived Klarg's pet dire wolf, Ripper. The party stealthily infiltrated [[Klarg]]'s lair and ambushed him and his minions, securing a hard-fought victory.

## Key Developments

- [[Glenn]] tamed the wolves [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]] and [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]].
- [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] killed [[Yeemik]] in a psionic dagger exchange after failed negotiations.
- Found treasure belonging to the [[Lionshield Coster]].
- [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] revealed the Rockseekers found [[Wave Echo Cave]], that [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] was taken to [[Cragmaw Castle]], and that [[Klarg]] works for the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]] — who wants Gundren and the map.
- Sildar named [[Iarno Albrek]] as a contact in [[Phandalin]].
- The wolves tricked Klarg's dire wolf Ripper; the party ambushed and killed [[Klarg]].
