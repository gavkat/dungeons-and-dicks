# Session 23: The Lying Lich and the Warforged

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** The party crosses a starry bridge into the birthplace of the Phandelver's Pact, learns the Ilefarn's cruelty, and watches Szass Tam murder and reanimate Kyle.

## Summary

The [[Wolfpack]]'s odyssey through [[Wave Echo Cave]] continued across a bridge in a vast, starry cavern, hundreds of meters above crashing waves and slick with cascading water. Skeletal hands burst from the bridge, spiders crept from the shadows, and bat-like creatures swarmed to push the party into the abyss. [[Prometheus]] transformed into a Quetzalcoatlus guardian to save his friends while [[Halia]], as a giant eagle, secured her own passage, and together they reached the far side.

There they found a door carved with ancient Dwarven runes, marking the hallowed halls as the birthplace of the [[Phandelver's Pact]]. Inside lay a shattered cavern — the remnants of a once-grand Dwarven hall — with a massive skull of the sinister deity [[Maglubiyet]] at its center. A grand door stood magically sealed, so they descended to a war room, where a grand carved table held a jade-outlined lily pad. Recalling a jade frog statuette, they placed it on the pad; the frog opened its mouth and revealed a hidden key. Meanwhile, gazing out the war-room windows, [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] fell into an Oracle vision: a lively city of High Elves, where a regal [[The Ilefarn|Ilefarn]] noble watched a royal guard escort a family of rabbitfolk before him. The harengon pleaded to "the light ones" for help — but in a shocking twist, the Ilefarn refused, instead seizing the child for *'the Six'* and ordering the guards to dispose of the *'Filthy Animal'* parents. The vision shifted to the Battle for Wave Echo Cave, then to the present and the fallen's remains.

As the vision faded, [[Szass Tam]] materialized and demanded the party submit to [[The Dark Six|the Dark Six]], confessing he had bargained with the deities after [[Mordenkainen]] refused their offers. When the party pressed him on his motives, he turned to the window, raised his hands, and commanded thousands of corpses to rise. [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]] and [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] growled, and Kyle charged — until Szass Tam spoke a single word, *'Down,'* unleashing Power Word Kill and ending Kyle's life instantly. The party fought back but were quickly overpowered. Before departing, the lich reanimated [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] as his pet and whispered *"Beshaba"* to [[Prometheus]], leaving the Wolfpack on the edge of despair.

## Key Developments

- The party crossed the bridge over the starry cavern, aided by [[Prometheus]] and [[Halia]]'s transformations.
- They found the runed door marking the birthplace of the [[Phandelver's Pact]] and a hall containing [[Maglubiyet]]'s god-skull.
- A jade frog statuette on a lily pad yielded a hidden key in the war room.
- [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s Oracle vision revealed the [[The Ilefarn|Ilefarn]] seizing a harengon child for *'the Six'* and condemning its parents.
- [[Szass Tam]] raised thousands of corpses and killed [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] with Power Word Kill, then reanimated him as his pet.
- Szass Tam whispered *"Beshaba"* to [[Prometheus]] before leaving.
