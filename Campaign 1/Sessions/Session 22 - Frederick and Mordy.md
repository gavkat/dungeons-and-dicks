# Session 22: Frederick and Mordy

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** Disguised as cultists, the party delves the tunnels of Wave Echo Cave, glimpses a 500-year-old war through Mordenkainen's eyes, and meets the necromancer Szass Tam.

## Summary

As [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] mourned over the lifeless form of his brother [[Nundro Rockseeker|Nundro]], he wove tales of how he, his brothers, and [[Prometheus]] had first discovered the fabled mines. Meanwhile, [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] and [[Glenn]] donned the garb of the [[The Redback Enclave|Redback cultists]] — and as the masks cloaked their faces, an unexpected surge of exhilaration washed over them.

The adventurers found a slender passage that opened into a labyrinth of tunnels. Within the maze they fought jelly monsters, swarms of skeletal foes, venomous mushrooms, and cave-spawn crawlers, overcoming each through unity and resilience. In a forgotten library, they unearthed a journal that, when opened, whisked Prometheus into visions of a fateful interaction between the formidable [[Mordenkainen]], a sorcerer known as [[Szass Tam]], and the dwarven king [[King Dumathoin|Dumathoin]]. These glimpses hinted at the intricate roles many had played in the lead-up to the cataclysmic war against the Orcs that took place in these mines 500 years ago.

Later, the party crossed paths with a necromancer who revealed himself as [[Szass Tam]]. Claiming to have awaited their arrival for ages, he left them with a foreboding warning that shrouded their minds in more questions than answers. Having navigated the treacherous cave and its perilous foes, the party now stood at the edge of a shadowy cavern — its waters flowing ominously, mysterious voices taunting them, a foreboding bridge beckoning them forward.

## Key Developments

- [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] recounted how he, his brothers, and [[Prometheus]] discovered the mines; [[Nundro Rockseeker|Nundro]]'s body was confirmed dead.

- The party battled through tunnels of oozes, skeletons, and mushrooms.
- A library journal showed [[Prometheus]] a vision of [[Mordenkainen]], [[Szass Tam]], and [[King Dumathoin]] before the war of 500 years ago.
- The necromancer [[Szass Tam]] appeared, claiming he'd long awaited them, and left an ominous warning.
- The party reached the edge of a shadowy cavern with a foreboding bridge.
