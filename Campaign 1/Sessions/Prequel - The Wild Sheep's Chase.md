# Prequel: The Wild Sheep's Chase

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** A talking sheep, a bedsheet dragon, and a wizard who turned out to be the puppeteer behind everything.

## Summary

In the whimsical realm of the Sword Coast, a tale of unlikely heroes began at the [[Daggerford]] Saint's Day fair. There [[Ethel Elkhid|Ethel]], a gothic dwarf wizard, crossed paths with Elder Frederick, a Tortle cleric who sang praises to [[Silvanus]]. In a tavern, [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]], a blind monk, overheard the city rangers whisper of the return of [[The Redbrands]], silent for years and now led by the mysterious "Master Noke." As the festivities peaked, [[Dew-Dew Bun]], a fairy bard, strummed her lute under the protection of her bodyguard [[Switchblade]] — until a sheep crashed the performance, desperate to deliver a warning, and a Half-orc and his beastly entourage stormed the crowd.

Fate drew the adventurers together. [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]], a Half-orc monster slayer, confronted his brother [[Guz Nightfoot|Guz]], while [[Glenn]], a "totally normal dude," revealed unexpected prowess in battle. Victorious, they learned the sheep was the wizard [[Finethir Shinebright|Finethir Shinebright]], who begged them to break his curse and defeat his rogue apprentice, [[Arnolt Noke|Noke]]. The journey led through the forest to the ruins of [[Littlefoot]], where Glenn faced his past and the party clashed with the Redbrands. Ethel and Frederick fell and were revived; Shinebright imparted wisdom and magical gifts before nightmares haunted the party's rest.

The greatest challenge awaited at Shinebright's former tower, where Noke — a fanatical admirer of Dew-Dew Bun — summoned zombies clad in her merchandise. [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] slew [[Guz Nightfoot|Guz]] despite [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]]'s attempts to save him. Noke emerged atop a dragon stitched from bedsheets, bedposts and linen — a creation of panic and the modified [[Wand of True Polymorph]]. After the bed-dragon fell, Noke turned the wand on himself, becoming a monstrous Gibbering Mouther, only to be undone by the party's united might (and Glenn's cock).

In the aftermath, [[Ethel Elkhid|Ethel]] used the [[Wand of True Polymorph]] to restore Shinebright to human form. But as he cast a spell, his true nature surfaced: he was the puppeteer behind the Redbrands' resurgence and the architect of the party's woes. He silenced [[Dew-Dew Bun]], revealed [[Glenn]]'s origins as an experiment, exposed [[Switchblade]] as a former Redbrand involved in the destruction of Ilbryn's people, and vanished — leaving the party frozen in time.

## Key Developments

- The party first assembled at the [[Daggerford]] Saint's Day fair: [[Ethel Elkhid|Ethel]], Frederick, [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]], [[Dew-Dew Bun]], [[Glenn]], and [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]].
- A cursed sheep revealed itself as the wizard [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]], who enlisted them against his apprentice [[Arnolt Noke|Noke]].
- [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] killed [[Guz Nightfoot|Guz]], [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]]'s brother and Redbrand leader.
- [[Arnolt Noke|Noke]] was defeated after turning himself into a Gibbering Mouther with the [[Wand of True Polymorph]].
- Restored to human form, [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] revealed himself as the mastermind — the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]] — silenced [[Dew-Dew Bun]], exposed [[Glenn]] as an experiment and [[Switchblade]] as a former Redbrand, then froze the party in time.
