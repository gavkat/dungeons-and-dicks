# Session 18: Leilon Calling

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** A poisoned [[Wild Fury]] limps back, a codeword is born, and the party reaches [[Leilon]] to revive [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] and recruit new blood.

## Summary

The adventure opened with [[Wild Fury]] stumbling through the forest in his poisoned state, calling out before collapsing at the campsite. As he recovered, his account raised suspicions about [[Halia]] — until teleportation tracks on the ground convinced the party she wasn't the shapeshifter after all. To prevent future confusion, they agreed on a codeword: *"Meatloaf."*

Reaching the picturesque town of [[Leilon]], they were met at the gates by the charming captain of the town guard, [[The Thornes|Eammon Thorne]], who explained the rules and directed them to his father, [[The Thornes|Alaric]], and the Fountain of Fortune. Alaric used the fountain's restorative waters to revive [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] from his vegetative state, explaining that the fountain — created by [[Tymora]] and the holy-water runoff from [[Wyvern Tor]] — could not be transported, holding its properties only about five minutes once removed. Told of [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]]'s fate, a grieving Gundren drowned his sorrows at the tavern, with [[Glenn]] joining him.

Meanwhile [[Prometheus]], Wild Fury, and [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] explored the town seeking recruits, eventually convincing Headmaster [[Eldrin Willowbrook|Willowbrook]] to let two teachers — [[Garrick Dreldan]] and [[Tolgun Aer'thi|Tolgun Aer'Thi]] — join them. The now-drunk Glenn won over the tavern with his bardic ways, earning the adoring attention of a local Lizardfolk, [[Sylvinia Salazar|Silvinia]]. The party were asked to find a missing dog and browsed the shops, dealing with merchants like [[Glimmerspark Tumpleton|Glimmerspark]] but struggling to strike good bargains. Throughout, Ilbryn was troubled by ominous visions that left him with more questions than answers.

## Key Developments

- [[Wild Fury]] recovers from [[Talice]]'s poison; the party adopts the codeword *"Meatloaf."*
- [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] revived at [[Leilon]]'s Fountain of Fortune by [[The Thornes|Father Alaric Thorne]], then learns of [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]]'s death.
- New recruits: [[Garrick Dreldan]] and [[Tolgun Aer'thi]], released by Headmaster [[Eldrin Willowbrook|Willowbrook]].
- [[Sylvinia Salazar|Silvinia]] becomes smitten with [[Glenn]]; a missing-dog quest is taken up.
- [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] plagued by ominous visions.
