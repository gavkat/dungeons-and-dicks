# Session 5: Revelations, Revolutions and Radicchio

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** A midnight ambush turned trap — a hero dead mid-monologue, the party captured, and a desperate escape into Tresendar Manor.

## Summary

The [[Wolfpack]] visited Alderleaf farm to speak with Quelline and her son, Carp, learning of a secret passage near Tresendar Manor, a secret password sounding like "illefarm," and a contact at [[Thundertree]] named [[Elder Reidoth|Reidoth]]. Investigating the vicinity of the manor, they evaded [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] and planned a midnight ambush with [[Lightning Steel]]'s [[Order of the Gauntlet]]. At the Stonehill Inn, [[Halia]] flirted with [[Glenn]], leading to an intimate invitation.

The ambush night turned sour as illusions tricked the party at the Sleeping Giant tavern, resulting in [[Lightning Steel]]'s death mid-monologue by a "Queen of Hearts" card and the party's capture by gas bombs. [[Prometheus]], unaffected by the gas, held strong and cast a *fireball* at the Redbrands, but was quickly outnumbered. Interrogated by [[Iarno Albrek|Iarno, the Glass Knight]], for information on the Lost Mines' cargo, they remained defiant.

Facing execution in [[Phandalin]], a rat freed [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]], sparking a chaotic battle with town guards, Redbrands, townsfolk and allies like [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]], [[Halia]] (in Owlbear form), [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]], [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] and a Harpy [[Sister Garaele]]. Using the chaos to their advantage, the Wolfpack escaped to Tresendar Manor, finding it filled with elvish gold, and descended into its basement.

## Key Developments

- Learned from Quelline and Carp of a secret passage near Tresendar Manor, the password "illefarm," and the [[Thundertree]] contact [[Elder Reidoth|Reidoth]].
- The planned ambush at the Sleeping Giant tavern was an illusion-trap; [[Lightning Steel]] was killed mid-monologue and the party gassed and captured.
- [[Iarno Albrek|The Glass Knight]] interrogated them about the Lost Mines' cargo; they stayed defiant.
- A rat freed [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]], triggering a chaotic battle with allies including [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]], [[Halia]], [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]], [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] and [[Sister Garaele]].
- The [[Wolfpack]] escaped into gold-filled Tresendar Manor and descended to its basement.
