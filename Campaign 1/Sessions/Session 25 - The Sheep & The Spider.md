# Session 25: The Sheep & The Spider

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** At the Forge of Spells, Shinebright becomes the true Black Spider, Gundren dies to save his brother's soul, and Prometheus reignites the Forge to birth a new age.

## Summary

In the heart of the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]], the [[Wolfpack]] faced their destiny. A brass clockwork track led them to the shimmering Forge, a source of raw arcane power, and there [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s visions surfaced once more — memories of the [[The Ilefarn|Ilefarn]] nation's dark ritual, performed in this very place thousands of years ago. The *"tri animalus arcanum"* saw their leaders, *'the six'*, sacrifice an *"unknowing"* harengon, an *"unproven"* grung, and an *"unguided"* aarakocra in a bid for godhood, chanting *Nisciens, non probatus, non ductus* — words now echoed by the onlooking cultists. The vision closed as the six elves completed their ritual, birthing the Elven Oracle prophecy, becoming six dark gods, and leaving the rest of the Ilefarn cursed into [[The Nothic|Nothics]].

The party's path was blocked by [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]], reinforced by his [[Nezznar|drow]] allies, menacing [[The Forge Warforged|warforged combatants]], and a shockingly corrupted [[Tharden Rockseeker|Tharden]] — [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]]'s own brother. Shinebright wove a chilling tale of manipulation before making for the levitating platform. [[Halia]] transformed into a Giant Ape, Gundren charged at his estranged brother, and the battle erupted. The Wolfpack used the bridge to outmaneuver the warforged, and their old ally [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]] — summoned by the horn he'd gifted them — joined the fray. [[Prometheus]] and Shinebright locked in a magical duel of counterspells and raw evocation. A colossal golden warforged titan descended, but Halia's Giant Ape, despite a devastating blow that knocked her unconscious, toppled it off the bridge. Even so, aided by [[Nezznar]] and [[Talice]], Shinebright reached the Forge.

There he unveiled his horrifying ambition: he had collected the blood of [[Glenn]], [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]], and [[Wild Fury]] to mirror the Ilefarn ritual — not for legacy, but for his own eternal life. With the [[Wand of True Polymorph]] and the Forge's power, he grotesquely transformed into a Giant Drider, half-human and half-spider, finally becoming the true [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]]. The Wolfpack fought with desperate courage, felling Talice and Nezznar, and [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] tackled [[Tharden Rockseeker|Tharden]] off the bridge, sacrificing himself to end his brother's dark path. As the Forge began to crumble and lava flowed, the Black Spider unleashed a devastating Prismatic Spray: Wild Fury was scorched, Glenn electrified, Ilbryn began to petrify, and Frederick was blinded.

With the Black Spider momentarily vulnerable, the party seized their chance. Prometheus's Mordenkainen's Sword lashed out, Glenn delivered a brutal *"turkey slap"* with his axes, Ilbryn channeled fiery energy into a punch even as stone crept up his legs, and Wild Fury clawed furiously. Frederick, blinded and disoriented, fumbled with his Sunbeam — until a warm hand and a resonant voice guided him: *"There is always light in the darkness, for you are the Lightbringer."* His Sunbeam lanced forward with pinpoint accuracy, and with the combined onslaught — BOOM! — the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]] crumpled, his reign obliterated.

But the Forge, in overdrive, threatened to consume them. Remembering his ultimate goal of Project Prometheus, [[Prometheus]] faced a choice: Disjunction, which would inert his warforged form but halt the magic, or Conjunction, to surge the Forge with chaotic arcane energy. Defiantly, he chose Conjunction and, muttering *"Beshaba"*, saved the Forge with the help of the pearl of power. The prophecy was fulfilled. The [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] roared to life, unleashing long-forgotten magic back into the world, and Prometheus — the wizard who dared steal divine fire — ushered in a new era of technology. Yet with the power came a stark reminder: once unleashed, power cannot easily be contained. What future awaits this new world? One shaped by the [[Echoes of Phandelver]]...

## Key Developments

- [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s vision revealed the [[The Ilefarn|Ilefarn]]'s *"tri animalus arcanum"* ritual that made *'the six'* into dark gods and the rest into [[The Nothic|Nothics]].
- [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]], his [[Nezznar|drow]], the [[The Forge Warforged|warforged]], and a corrupted [[Tharden Rockseeker|Tharden]] blocked the Forge.
- [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]] returned, summoned by the horn; [[Halia]]'s Giant Ape toppled the warforged titan.
- Shinebright used the blood of [[Glenn]], [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]], and [[Wild Fury]] with the [[Wand of True Polymorph]] to become a Giant Drider — the true [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]].
- [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] sacrificed himself, taking [[Tharden Rockseeker|Tharden]] off the bridge.
- The party survived the Black Spider's Prismatic Spray and destroyed him, guided by the [[Lightbringer]]'s voice.
- [[Prometheus]] chose Conjunction and reignited the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]], birthing a new age.
