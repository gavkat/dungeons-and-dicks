# Session 6: The Thing About Bowties

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** A bowtie monologue, a golden door vision, a halted mummy, and a family rescued from the crypts.

## Summary

In the depths of the [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]]' lair, the [[Wolfpack]] triumphed over hidden foes and uncovered the [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]]'s hidden escape pack. Frederick, angered by the death of his hero [[Lightning Steel]], intimidated a captive Redbrand with a bowtie monologue, terrifying him into uncovering hideout secrets and revealing that the [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]] is also working for the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]].

After unaliving the informant, the team donned Redbrand cloaks and navigated secret passages and traps to reach the Crypts. There, [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s touch on a golden door whisked him to a vision-filled plane before he faced undead adversaries. Frederick's *turn undead* and [[Prometheus]]'s fiery insights turned the tide against the skeletons and a mummy, which was ultimately halted at [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s mention of "Ilefarn," the name of an ancient elven nation.

Victorious in the prison cells, they rescued [[The Dendrar Family|Thel Dendrar's family]], who offered an heirloom from [[Thundertree]] as thanks.

## Key Developments

- Found the [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]]'s hidden escape pack.
- Frederick's bowtie monologue terrified a captive Redbrand into revealing hideout secrets — and that the [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]] also serves the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]].
- The party disguised in Redbrand cloaks and reached the Crypts.
- A golden door gave [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] a vision; the word "Ilefarn" halted a mummy — the name of the ancient elven nation [[The Ilefarn]].
- Rescued [[The Dendrar Family|Thel Dendrar's family]], who gifted an heirloom from [[Thundertree]].
