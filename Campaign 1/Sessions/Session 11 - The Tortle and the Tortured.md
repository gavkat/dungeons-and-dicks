# Session 11: The Tortle and the Tortured

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] dies, meets Lathander, reforges "Refraction" into [[Lightbringer]] to slay [[Lightning Steel]] — then leaves the party for family.

## Summary

After their harrowing encounter with the [[Cragmaw Hideout|Cragmaw]] tribe, the [[Wolfpack]] deliberated and accepted the offer from the vampire [[Lightning Steel]]. Under his shroud, they infiltrated [[Cragmaw Castle]], freeing the tabaxi paladin [[Wild Fury|Wild Fury III]], heir to the [[Lionshield Coster]] — the covert guild ensuring Faerûn's political order. [[Lightning Steel]]'s true plan unfolded in his crypts: to commandeer [[Wave Echo Cave]] and resurrect a vampire empire using the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]].

The [[Wolfpack]] resisted, and as [[Lightning Steel]] held [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s beloved [[Betty]] hostage, an epic battle ensued, the vampire summoning spectral forces. The party fought valiantly — [[Wild Fury|Wild Fury III]] empowered by an amulet, [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]] and [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] taking care of ghosts and ghouls (with [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]] aging 20 years in the process) — rallying [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s Spirit Guardian, [[Wild Fury]]'s radiant claws, [[Prometheus]]'s Witch's Bolt, and [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s fiery Snake strikes against the vampire.

But the vampire's wrath struck [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] down, and in his final breaths he watched the vampire slash the throat of his beloved. In the abyss, [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] encountered Lathander, who offered revival and salvation. His sword, Refraction, transformed into the legendary [[Lightbringer]], empowering [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] to obliterate [[Lightning Steel]] with a sunbeam. He then resurrected [[Betty]], who disclosed her pregnancy. Choosing family over adventure, [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] left the party, entrusting [[Lightbringer]] to [[Wild Fury]] in return for a giant Elk steed — a poignant farewell.

## Key Developments

- The party freed [[Wild Fury|Wild Fury III]] and learned of the [[Lionshield Coster]].
- [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] died, met Lathander, reforged "Refraction" into [[Lightbringer]], and destroyed [[Lightning Steel]].
- [[Betty]] revealed her pregnancy; [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] left the party, giving [[Lightbringer]] to [[Wild Fury]].
