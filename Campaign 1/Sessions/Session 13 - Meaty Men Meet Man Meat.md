# Session 13: Meaty Men Meet Man Meat

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** Sneaking through [[Cragmaw Castle]]'s second level, the [[Wolfpack]] brawl through a kitchen chef and goblin feast — and win over the Goblinettes.

## Summary

The session began as the [[Wolfpack]] entered the second level of [[Cragmaw Castle]]. Traversing the dark halls, [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] brilliantly cast Pass Without Trace, letting the team move swiftly and stealthily. [[Prometheus]] perceived laughter and music from a dining hall, and clanging and barking from a kitchen further down. Peering into the dining hall, they saw a group of goblins being entertained by scantily clad Goblinettes; in the kitchen, a large, burly chef worked alongside two Mastiffs caged nearby.

The team plotted to tie both doors shut simultaneously. The trick worked on the dining-room door but failed on the kitchen door, alerting the caged dogs. Using his animal charm and a piece of red meat, [[Glenn]] subdued the dogs, and the team set up an ambush on the chef. They struck together, breaking into a fight as the chef released his dogs.

The [[Wolfpack]] landed devastating blows but took nasty haymakers in return — [[Glenn]] was thrown across the kitchen. The commotion drew the goblins next door into the fray as the Goblinettes tried to flee. [[Prometheus]] terrified the goblins with a deadly poison spray that made their heads explode. As the fight peaked, the goblin boss ordered the Goblinettes to join in while he tried to flee — but after seeing [[Glenn]]'s salami, they turned on the boss instead. The [[Wolfpack]] took turns aiding the Goblinettes in their revenge, eventually succeeding. After a harsh rejection from the Goblinettes, the party took a long rest in the bunkers across the hall, anticipating a long day ahead.

## Key Developments

- [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s Pass Without Trace carried the party stealthily through the second level.
- [[Glenn]] charmed the Mastiffs; the chef and goblin feast erupted into a brawl, [[Prometheus]] wiping out goblins with poison spray.
- The Goblinettes turned on the goblin boss; the [[Wolfpack]] took a long rest.
