# Epilogue: 3 Years Later

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

## Summary

Three years on, the Wolfpack's triumph over the Black Spider had faded into the quiet — and not-so-quiet — lives that followed.

For [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]], victory soured into troubled domesticity. His ceaseless recounting of heroic deeds finally drove the voluptuous [[Betty]] away, leaving him partial custody of Fred Jr. every second weekend. It was during one such weekend at a playground that peace shattered: the MageGuard, a squad of militant wizards, swarmed him and placed him under arrest — the charge, the murder of [[Lightning Steel]].

Leaving the Lost Mines behind, [[Wild Fury]] embraced his destiny and assumed leadership of the [[Lionshield Coster]] in his father's absence, while finding a new paternal role raising his loyal wyvern [[Dave]]. But all that changed when [[Halia]], now a respected member of the Coster herself, delivered a revelation that shattered his world: his father was still alive.

[[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] conquered his alcohol addiction, transforming his past into a new beginning by opening his own bar. But one fateful night, an Oracle vision shattered his peace — a terrifying tableau of [[The Dark Six]] engaged in a sinister ritual, images of raw elements and stark landscapes flashing across his mind: ocean, arctic tundra, desert. Then, with chilling synchronicity, the Dark Six turned, their voices resonating as one: *"The Dark Six are coming."*

After a small stint on tour, [[Glenn]] found peace and retired to a cozy [[Littlefoot]] cottage, enjoying tranquil days by the fire with [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]]. Yet peace proved fleeting. One day a sharp knock echoed; Walter growled as Glenn opened the door to a familiar face — [[Droop]]. But this Droop had black eyes, foamed bile, and rasped a sinister *"Howdy Glenn,"* before lunging to attack.

And [[Prometheus]], his world-saving spell complete, found himself ripped from reality and thrust into a chillingly mysterious dimension. There, as [[Mordenkainen]], he hung tethered and bound, his very soul a siphon for the gnawing negative energy pulsing from the dead eye of an undead Beholder. Looming over him, a cruel smile twisting his lips, was [[Szass Tam]]. *"I made a promise I wouldn't kill you,"* the lich rasped, *"so I'll just have to make you suffer."*

## Key Developments

- [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] lost [[Betty]] and was arrested by the MageGuard for the murder of [[Lightning Steel]].
- [[Wild Fury]] led the [[Lionshield Coster]] and raised [[Dave]]; [[Halia]] revealed his father is still alive.
- [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] beat his addiction and opened a bar, then received an Oracle vision: *"The Dark Six are coming."*
- [[Glenn]] retired to [[Littlefoot]] with [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]], only to be attacked by a black-eyed [[Droop]].
- [[Prometheus]], as [[Mordenkainen]], was left tethered and tortured by [[Szass Tam]] in another dimension.
