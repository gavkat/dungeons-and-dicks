# Session 24: Human Resources

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** Glenn plays HR rep in the smeltery, "Meatloaf" exposes a shapeshifter, the party slays a Beholder, and a stone king names Prometheus as Mordenkainen reborn.

## Summary

Under the unsettling gaze of [[Szass Tam]]'s undead beyond the war-room window, the [[Wolfpack]] pressed on toward the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]]. Oppressive heat from an adjacent chamber drew their attention, and [[Glenn]] — still in his cultist disguise — went ahead to scout. Inside the sweltering smeltery he found two cultists feeding gold into the furnace, but an overwhelming persuasive charm took hold and he marched in with a hearty greeting. Outside, a duplicate Glenn appeared, trying to mislead the party. The real Glenn improvised as a Human Resources representative questioning workplace safety, ending the farce by shoving a halfling cultist into the molten pit; when the second retaliated with a flaming skull, Glenn swiftly dispatched both.

Emerging from the chaos, Glenn found his companions had followed his treacherous double to the throne room, where ten cultists completed a dark ritual, merging into a ten-eyed Beholder. The true Glenn burst in warning of the imposter, and chaos erupted as the party struggled to tell friend from foe — until Glenn recalled their codeword: *"Meatloaf."* The imposter was unmasked as [[Talice]], the Drow shapeshifter, who vanished after drawing [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s blood. The battle against the Beholder was desperate: [[Wild Fury]] smote it under cover of [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s darkness — cracking his [[Dave|dragon egg]] in the effort — while [[Prometheus]] was petrified, Frederick paralysed, and Glenn narrowly dodged a disintegration beam. Through sheer resilience, they vanquished the horror.

Their relief was short-lived. As they moved to leave, a colossal statue of [[King Dumathoin]] came to life, blocking their path with shocking accusations. It denounced Ilbryn as an abomination and identified [[Prometheus]] as [[Mordenkainen]] reincarnated. The king expressed profound shame, recognizing [[Lightbringer]] — now carried by Frederick — as his own creation from a bygone era. Finally, the stone king declared that only those of his lineage could proceed. Silence hung in the air until [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] stepped forward to claim his heritage; acknowledging his descendant, the statue rumbled aside and granted passage.
