# Session 20: Vox

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** The Wolfpack reaches Wyvern Tor, an old friend returns, and the "goddess" guiding them is unmasked as an avatar of Beshaba.

## Summary

Still reeling from the [[Leilon|red dragon's attack]], the [[Wolfpack]] lingered to console the maimed and injured townsfolk, who gifted them valuable items — including gauntlets forged from dragon scales. [[Wild Fury]] wrestled with a strange weakness sapping his paladin strength as the party rested, then set off for [[Wyvern Tor]] so [[Prometheus]] could attune to the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|Spellbook]]. Their ascent led to an alcove whose quiet was shattered by *ember skeletons* — remnants of past adventurers claimed by wyverns. [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]], cradled by [[Sister Garaele]], swooped in and vanquished the undead.

Reunited with their long-absent friend, the party promptly squabbled over who should carry [[Lightbringer]]. Wild Fury's keen eyes caught a translucent wall the others couldn't see; passing through it, he vanished into a vision of his grandfather that revealed the origins of the [[Lionshield Coster]]. Guided by this tale of sacrifice, he found a defeated wyvern and an egg nestled within, protected by his grandfather's armor and sword. Embracing his destiny, Wild Fury donned the armor, took up the blade, and vowed to safeguard [[Dave|the egg]].

At the summit, Prometheus cast a modified Globe of Invulnerability and began his attunement — until [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|The Vox Spiritus]] demanded the book. She revealed she had tricked the party all along: she was no servant of [[Tymora]] but the evil Vox of [[Beshaba]]. A fierce battle erupted against the Vox and her pet dragon. Garaele's mind invasion paralyzed the beast, but the enraged Vox struck Garaele down. Then [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s Oracle sight revealed the dragon's weakness — a slice along its throat — and the truth dawned: this was [[Switchblade]]. The dragon roared mockingly, *"Switch Switch, Bitch."*

Divine intervention saw Garaele rise again, a radiant warrior in Tymora's visage. As the battle of sisters raged in the sky, the party exploited the dragon's weakness and [[Glenn]] severed its head. The Vox resurrected Switchblade one last time to break Prometheus's globe, but Ilbryn's fatal blow ended the dragon, and Wild Fury misty-stepped to the Vox to smite the final blow. Prometheus completed his attunement; the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|Spellbook]] erupted in arcane flames, the storm clouds parted, and he stood with the sacred flame in his grasp — a beacon destined to ignite the fires of rebellion.

## Key Developments

- Grieving townsfolk gifted the party items, including dragon-scale gauntlets; [[Wild Fury]]'s paladin strength was mysteriously weakening.
- At [[Wyvern Tor]], a vision of Wild Fury's grandfather revealed the origins of the [[Lionshield Coster]]; Wild Fury claimed his grandfather's armor, sword, and a wyvern [[Dave|egg]].
- [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] returned, carried by [[Sister Garaele]].
- The guiding "goddess" was revealed as the [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba]], an avatar of [[Beshaba]]; her pet dragon was [[Switchblade]].
- [[Sister Garaele]] was struck down, then rose as a Vox of [[Tymora]]; the dragon was beheaded by [[Glenn]] and finished by [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]].
- [[Prometheus]] completed his attunement to the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|Spellbook]] and drew forth its sacred flame.
