# Session 9: May Silvanus Open Wide and Let You In

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** In ruined [[Thundertree]], [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] confronts his past as the [[Silvanus]] cult tries to feed the town to their god.

## Summary

After dismissing [[Droop]], the [[Wolfpack]] leaned on their knowledge of the land and [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s celestial visions to make their way to [[Thundertree]]. Their search in a herbologist's shop was interrupted by a skirmish with giant spiders, which led to the discovery of the silver comb — something all but [[Glenn]] could hold.

Outside, they met [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s past in the form of [[Elder Reidoth|Elder Reidoth]], [[Elder Marcus]], and his ex-wife [[Sister Fredericka|Fredericka]], who disclosed a sinister sacrifice to [[Silvanus]] — the inhabitants of the town. The elders proposed a trade: directions to [[Cragmaw Castle]] in exchange for [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Fred]]'s allegiance and sacrifice to [[Silvanus]].

The party's refusal sparked a fierce confrontation. The elders' ritual summoned zombies and animated a colossal tree monster. Amid the chaos, [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]] and [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] vanquished [[Elder Reidoth|Reidoth]] and [[Sister Fredericka|Fredericka]] — to [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Fred]]'s disapproval — while [[Elder Marcus]] met his end in a deluded charge toward [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s Spirit Guardians. The battle climaxed with [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s fiery Fist of the Fire Snake defeating the Tree Monster.

## Key Developments

- The silver comb was recovered (holdable by all but [[Glenn]]).
- The [[Silvanus]] cult — [[Elder Reidoth|Reidoth]], [[Elder Marcus|Marcus]], and [[Sister Fredericka|Fredericka]] — was destroyed; [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] faced his old life.
- The tree monster and summoned zombies were defeated.
