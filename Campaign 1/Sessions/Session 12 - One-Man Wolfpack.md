# Session 12: One-Man Wolfpack

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] is broken by [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]], [[Glenn]] transforms mid-fall, and the [[Wolfpack]] battle mimics to recover [[Wild Fury]]'s armor.

## Summary

The tale begins with [[Glenn]], barely conscious, dragged through the dark forest by the [[Cragmaw Hideout|Cragmaw]] tribe, the malevolent grin of [[King Grol]] seared into his mind. He awoke chained beside a bloodied [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren Rockseeker]] in the depths of [[Cragmaw Castle]]. [[King Grol]] and the familiar half-orc [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]] demanded the location of [[Wave Echo Cave]], but [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]]'s defiance was unyielding — and with a sinister command, both captives were knocked unconscious.

Meanwhile, the rest of the [[Wolfpack]], fresh from their victory over [[Lightning Steel]], stealthily navigated the castle's crypts and emerged into the dilapidated entrance hall. [[Glenn]] regained consciousness chained by a grand window. A portal shimmered into existence, and out stepped [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] and two Drow — one familiar from the [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] hideout. The male Drow drew blood from [[Glenn]]'s cheek while [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] tried to coerce [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] into revealing the mine's location. Defiant to the end, [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] spat in [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]]'s face and told [[Glenn]] to *"find the Elven oracle!"* Wiping the spit away with a handkerchief embroidered with a black spider, [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] cast a spell that contorted and broke [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]]'s body, extracting the information. [[King Grol]] then kicked [[Glenn]] out of the window, threatening to kill [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]] and [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] if he saw them again.

As [[Glenn]] plummeted, rage surged through him and he transformed into his full bestial form just before impact. The [[Wolfpack]] rushed to his aid through whizzing arrows. Pressing on, the party ascended the crumbling grand staircase, triggering a trap that alerted goblin assassins; after a fierce battle they prevailed. Guided by radiant magic to a storeroom, they found a treasure chest that morphed into a Mimic — as did the door behind them. Surrounded, they fought valiantly, defeating the mimics and recovering [[Wild Fury]]'s armor and weapons before ascending to the next level.

## Key Developments

- [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] broke [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]]'s body to extract the mine's location; [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] charged [[Glenn]] to *"find the Elven oracle."*
- [[Glenn]] transformed into his full bestial form mid-fall and reunited with the party.
- The [[Wolfpack]] defeated goblin assassins and mimics, recovering [[Wild Fury]]'s armor and weapons.
