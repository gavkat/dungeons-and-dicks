# Session 19: Fire and Flesh

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** A red dragon razes [[Leilon]], killing teachers and the [[The Thornes|Thornes]] alike, and leaves [[Wild Fury]] a [[Glimmerspark Tumpleton|Glimmerspark]]-forged Javelin of Lightning.

## Summary

[[Wild Fury]], [[Prometheus]], [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]], and [[Halia]] kept exploring [[Leilon]]'s shops, bartering at length with Lila Greenleaf at the Apothefairy. Meanwhile [[Glenn]] put a drunken [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] to bed and had an intimate encounter with [[Sylvinia Salazar|Silvinia]] — who revealed she was the daughter of Glenn's former lover, [[The Rembrandts|Pierre of the Rembrandts]]. As the group prepared to investigate the butchery for Tamás's lost dog, the sky darkened and a severed dog's head fell from above. Then a massive red dragon appeared and tore into the peaceful town.

The dragon landed first on the Adventuring Academy, collapsing pillars and walls; despite Ilbryn's efforts, he could not save the children from the debris. It set the tavern ablaze and trapped its occupants — Ilbryn's morningstar throw opened the doors, but only sparked a panicked stampede. Glenn, on the tavern's upper level, leapt from a window, choosing to rescue Gundren over Silvinia. Attempts to fight the beast went poorly: Professor [[Tolgun Aer'thi|Tolgun]] flew in to help and was swatted away by the dragon's tail, dying instantly. The Academy fell — [[Garrick Dreldan]] staggered out burnt and dismembered but alive, while Headmaster [[Eldrin Willowbrook|Willowbrook]] was crushed mid-spell saving schoolchildren. Wild Fury watched as the dragon's tail smashed toward the Fountain of Fortune; [[The Thornes|Father Alaric]] and his son [[The Thornes|Captain Eammon Thorne]] threw themselves in to protect it, and the fountain's magical water turned red with the [[The Thornes|Thornes]]' blood.

Ilbryn lashed the dragon with water whips while Wild Fury struck at its eyes, provoking fire breath on the town guard that Glenn and Ilbryn narrowly escaped. In desperation the party called on Lila for help; as the dragon destroyed her Apothefairy tent, she threw a "lightning in a bottle" to Wild Fury, who passed it to [[Glimmerspark Tumpleton|Glimmerspark]]. Satisfied, the dragon told Ilbryn they would meet again and flew off with Tamás as a final prey. In the aftermath, Glimmerspark presented Wild Fury with a newly forged Javelin of Lightning, leaving the party to face the wreckage.

## Key Developments

- A **red dragon** destroys [[Leilon]] — the Academy, the tavern, and much of the town.
- [[Tolgun Aer'thi|Tolgun]] killed; Headmaster [[Eldrin Willowbrook|Willowbrook]] crushed; [[Garrick Dreldan]] survives, maimed.
- The [[The Thornes|Thornes]] die protecting the Fountain of Fortune, bloodying its waters.
- [[Sylvinia Salazar|Silvinia]] revealed as the daughter of [[The Rembrandts|Pierre]].
- The dragon promises Ilbryn a reunion and flies off with Tamás.
- [[Glimmerspark Tumpleton|Glimmerspark]] forges [[Wild Fury]] a Javelin of Lightning.
