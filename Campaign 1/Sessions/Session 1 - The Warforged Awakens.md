# Session 1: The Warforged Awakens

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** A year apart, a reunion, an ambush — and a Warforged bursting from a carriage, reborn.

## Summary

As [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]]'s spell faded, an awkward tension filled the air. [[Dew-Dew Bun]] whispered as the party splintered, leaving behind unresolved tensions and bitter exchanges. During their year apart, Frederick sought heroism after heartbreak, [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] delved into the shadows seeking revenge, and [[Glenn]] embraced a rockstar-widow lifestyle.

After the hiatus, they reunited upon receiving a request from [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]], along with [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]], to transport mysterious cargo linked to the Lost Mines of [[Phandelver]]. Setting off from [[Neverwinter]], their journey was abruptly interrupted by an ambush.

Unbeknownst to the adventurers, a divine encounter was taking place: a godly figure offered [[Prometheus]] the Warforged a chance at life through reanimation. With terms agreed, the session ended with Prometheus's mystical rebirth as he burst through the carriage.

## Key Developments

- The party reunited after a year apart, hired by [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] and [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] to haul cargo tied to the Lost Mines of [[Phandelver]].
- They set off from [[Neverwinter]] and were ambushed en route.
- A "godly figure" (later the [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba]]) offered [[Prometheus]] the Warforged life through reanimation.
- [[Prometheus]] agreed and was reborn, bursting through the carriage.
