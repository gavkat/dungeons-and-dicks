# Session 16: Dazed and Confused

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** The [[Wolfpack]] drinks from the well at [[Old Owl Well]] and wakes into a mind-bending dungeon of riddles, mirrors, and a beauty-judging Beholder.

## Summary

Arriving in an expansive open field, the team studied a mysterious, seemingly abandoned well. The [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox Spiritus]] told them that once they retrieved the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|spellbook]], they should bring it to her at [[Wyvern Tor]] — a site of profound magic — where she could attune to it, then departed. After many failed attempts at [[Agatha the Hag|Agatha]]'s riddle, [[Wild Fury]] drank from the well and fainted within a minute. The party deliberated, investigated, and ultimately all took a sip, falling unconscious together.

They woke with their minds and sense of time warped by a potent elixir, muddling thought, memory, and motor function. They found themselves in an ominous room: the well at its heart, a statue holding a mirror across from them, and a foreboding inscription on the ceiling. Driven by curiosity, [[Glenn]] gripped a floating gauntlet in the well, which restrained him and triggered a rush of water. As Glenn struggled against the rising tide, the team deciphered the statue-hand puzzle, freeing him and opening the way forward.

The [[Wolfpack]] faced a series of challenges and riddles. They ascended (and free-fell down) a seemingly endless staircase, solving it through thieves' cant by *"flipping and reversing"* their direction. They freed a tortured figure through a musical melody. [[Prometheus]], with artistic flair, painted the mirror red to reveal a hidden passage and a new ally named Richard, and finally the party proved that beauty is in the eye of the Beholder. They then advanced, seemingly separated into dark abysses where their next trial awaited.

## Key Developments

- The party drinks the well water at [[Old Owl Well]] and enters a magical trial dungeon.
- Minds and perception altered; motor function and memory muddled.
- Puzzles solved: the gauntlet/statue trap, the endless staircase ("flipping and reversing"), the freed musical figure, the red-painted mirror, and a Beholder's beauty riddle.
- A new ally, "Richard," is uncovered.
- The [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox Spiritus]] names [[Wyvern Tor]] as the spellbook's attunement site.
