# Session 10: It Only Snows in Neverwinter

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** A vampire's offer, a hag's riddle, and a [[Cragmaw Hideout|Cragmaw]] ambush that seizes [[Glenn]].

## Summary

In the rubble of [[Thundertree]], [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s heartache was laid bare through a letter from his departed ex-wife, moving the group to tears. They journeyed from [[Thundertree]] to [[Conyberry]], with a quick (one-week) celebratory stopover in [[Neverwinter]]. Driven by the mysteries of [[Agatha the Hag|Agatha]]'s cottage and haunted by dreams of past deeds, [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s nightmares replayed the grim demise of [[Switchblade]], now twisted by a shadowy figure's intervention.

While attending to nature's call, [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s serenity was interrupted as [[Lightning Steel]] emerged from the shadows with a proposition: his guidance to [[Cragmaw Castle]] in exchange for an "invitation" into [[Wave Echo Cave]]. His tale was one of ambition turned to tragedy — a quest for eternal youth granted by [[Beshaba]] that twisted his fate from prince to vampire, leading to the slaughter of his kin, centuries of solitude, and a castle overrun by unruly goblinoids.

At [[Agatha the Hag|Agatha]]'s lair, the party, seeking the whereabouts of [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|Mordenkainen's spellbook]], bartered with the silver comb — only to receive a cryptic riddle in response. [[Prometheus]]'s impatience sparked [[Agatha the Hag|Agatha]]'s wrath, but a swift apology quelled the rising storm and the party was spared. Yet the road held its own dangers: the [[Cragmaw Hideout|Cragmaw]] tribe ambushed them, seizing [[Glenn]] amid the fray. As they regrouped, [[Lightning Steel]] reappeared, his proposition now a beacon in their darkest hour — the vampire's chilling question echoing in their ears: *"So, do you want to take me up on that offer?"*

## Key Developments

- [[Lightning Steel]] revealed his vampire backstory and offered passage to [[Cragmaw Castle]] / [[Wave Echo Cave]].
- [[Agatha the Hag|Agatha]] gave a cryptic riddle in exchange for the silver comb.
- The [[Cragmaw Hideout|Cragmaw]] tribe ambushed the party and captured [[Glenn]].
