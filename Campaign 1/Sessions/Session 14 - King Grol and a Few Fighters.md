# Session 14: King Grol and a Few Fighters

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** A throne-room brawl ends with [[King Grol]] dead, [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]] standing down, and a freed owlbear named Owlie McBear stealing the show.

## Summary

After a long rest in the goblin quarters, the [[Wolfpack]] was haunted by vivid dreams — grandfathers battling Wyverns, an armillary sphere atop a floating island, a cryptic riddle about mending and bending, and [[Glenn]] enjoying the best sleep of his life. A sharp knock woke them. While Glenn and [[Wild Fury]] devised an escape through the window, [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] and [[Prometheus]] quietly dispatched the goblin guard. Heading upstairs, the crew faced a beast rumbling behind one door and an eerie silence behind a grand one.

Choosing the grand door, they entered the king's throne room, where [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]] and [[King Grol]] waited beside a collapsed [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]]. Tlaloc offered safety if they groveled and kissed his feet. Refusing, the party turned to flee — but in the chaos Prometheus and [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]] were trapped on the wrong side, facing an army of orcs, hobgoblins, and bugbears. King Grol, defying Tlaloc, charged Prometheus, who held him in place with a spell and tried to talk Tlaloc down. Intrigued, Tlaloc leapt into the ceiling, and the battle began. Glenn and [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]], realizing the beastly grumbles were cries for help, freed an owlbear from her chains, named her Owlie McBear, and she stormed in with ferocious might.

The battle was intense. Owlie McBear fell after taking out numerous orcs, and Grol broke free of Prometheus's *Hold Person*, choke-slamming the warforged into the ground and beating him unconscious with his great axe. Turning to Glenn — "You and the dogs are next!" — Grol met his end as Glenn, Walter, and Kyle annihilated him together. Wild Fury revived Prometheus, who rose and torched a number of orcs with his burning hands. When Tlaloc emerged threatening Ilbryn, Ilbryn convinced him and the remaining goblinoids to stand down as the battle concluded.

## Key Developments

- [[King Grol]] killed by [[Glenn]], [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]] and [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]].
- The freed owlbear "Owlie McBear" turned the tide, then fell after taking out many orcs.
- [[Prometheus]] knocked unconscious by Grol, then revived by [[Wild Fury]].
- [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]] hinted at an alliance; [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] talked him and his goblinoids into standing down.
- [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] found collapsed in the throne room.
