# Prologue

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** In an age long forgotten, the Phandelver's Pact built a cavern of wonders — and lost it to war.

## Summary

In an age long forgotten, when magic still coursed through rock and soil, the stalwart clans of dwarves and gnomes forged an alliance of legend — the [[Phandelver's Pact]] — and hewed from the earth the mine of unparalleled richness known as [[Wave Echo Cave]]. Within it was born the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]], a crucible where human spellcasters, in brotherhood with their dwarven and gnomish kin, wove the very essence of sorcery into creation. The land of [[Phandalin]] flourished in the shadow of this subterranean marvel, and peace and prosperity reigned.

But darkness coveted the light. A horde of goblins and orcs surged from the North, led by the vile war god [[Maglubiyet]]. As the cavern trembled with arcane fury, the defenders stood firm. In their direst hour, the human wizards used the Forge of Spells to birth an army of warforged wizards — beings of metal and magic, mirrors of their creators' resolve.

The battle became legend. The forge's light dimmed under the shadow of Maglubiyet's bargain with [[The Dark Six]], deities of dread who sought to extinguish the warforged's newfound spark. When silence finally claimed the battlefield, Wave Echo Cave was lost to the annals of time, its secrets buried beneath rubble and ruin — its legacy a whisper on the winds of history.

## Key Developments

- The dwarves and gnomes formed the [[Phandelver's Pact]] and built [[Wave Echo Cave]], home to the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]].
- A horde of goblins and orcs under [[Maglubiyet]] invaded from the North.
- The human wizards forged an army of warforged wizards from the Forge of Spells to defend the cave.
- [[Maglubiyet]] bargained with [[The Dark Six]] to extinguish the warforged.
- Wave Echo Cave was lost to time, its secrets buried under rubble.
