# Session 15: Son of a Grick

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** The party makes peace with [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]], survives a library puzzle and a pair of Gricks, and meets the celestial who brought [[Prometheus]] back to life.

## Summary

After the battle with the Cragmaw tribe, the [[Wolfpack]] made amends with [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]], who let them loot [[King Grol]]'s body, take a vegetative [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]], and gave them a horn to summon him in a time of need, promising safe passage through the rest of [[Cragmaw Castle]]. They pressed on to the castle's final storey, finding a desecrated temple shrine and a library.

While [[Prometheus]] and [[Glenn]] investigated the library, [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] and [[Wild Fury]] felt watched. As Prometheus read a spellscroll — *"what can bend may break, but what can mend will never be broken"* — the library flung itself into a chaotic whirlwind, shooting Glenn out of the room and Prometheus into a void where a single wrong step caused psychic pain. As Prometheus wrestled with the puzzle, the rest of the gang were set upon by a baby Grick, and then its enormous mother. After a fierce battle — Wild Fury smiting the baby, Glenn felling the mother with [[Walter & Kyle|Walter and Kyle]]'s help but taking heavy damage — the party reunited.

Now holding a scroll of mending, Prometheus repaired an arcane symbol in the shrine, summoning a beautiful, angelic celestial. Claiming to be the [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox Spiritus]] of the goddess [[Tymora]], she revealed she was the one who had brought Prometheus back to life and granted his powers. As the tethering between this realm and the gods, she spoke of the importance of retrieving the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|spellbook of Mordenkainen]] so she could attune to it and stop the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]]. She gave Prometheus a magical pearl of power, healed the group of all ailments, and transported them to their next destination: [[Old Owl Well]].

## Key Developments

- Peace made with [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]]; the party gains a summoning horn, safe passage, and the loot of [[King Grol]].
- A vegetative [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] taken along.
- Library "mend/bend" puzzle and a baby-and-mother Grick fight survived.
- A celestial claiming to be the [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox Spiritus of Tymora]] appears — she reanimated [[Prometheus]] and wants the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]].
- Prometheus receives a pearl of power; the party is teleported to [[Old Owl Well]].
