# Session 17: The Light Beyond

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** Personal trials yield the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]], [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] is revealed as the Elven Oracle — and a poisoned blade ends the session in betrayal.

## Summary

Separated by design, the [[Wolfpack]] faced trials tailored to their deepest vulnerabilities. [[Prometheus]], lured by explosive temptation, drew on his curiosity to earn *"The Spark of Curiosity."* [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] was besieged by a shadowy, hauntingly familiar assassin and triumphed through disciplined defense, earning *"The Tides of Balance."* [[Wild Fury]] confronted his father in a clash of wills, channeling his Smite to claim *"The Roar of Pride."* [[Glenn]] faced the spectral echoes of past lovers and, by rekindling forgotten flames, awakened *"The Flame of Passion."*

Reunited, the party faced a guardian whose chest held a shifting gem; realizing its color signaled an elemental weakness, they combined their new powers to shatter the construct. A final memory riddle involving the Scarlet Great-horned Owl opened a door to the summit of a grand watchtower overlooking the Sword Coast. There waited the [[The Bookkeeper|Bookkeeper]], who claimed to have awaited them for years and bestowed the legendary [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]] upon Prometheus, declaring it his destiny.

The Bookkeeper revealed the Ilefarn Prophecy — *"Efarn Euox Bryn Il,"* sounding like "Elven Oracle," meaning *"the one to see beyond the light."* In a twist of fate, Ilbryn was unveiled as the Elven Oracle, and through intense visions he discerned the true location of the [[Phandelver|Lost Mines of Phandelver]]. The Scarlet Owl transformed into [[Halia]], revealing herself as the Bookkeeper's daughter. Cautioning the party that the [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox Spiritus]] might be malevolent, the Bookkeeper gifted them elemental rings of resistance forged from the guardian and its gems. Setting off for [[Leilon]] in search of a fountain of greater restoration to heal [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]], the party hit a dark turn: as Wild Fury kissed Halia, she was revealed as a familiar [[Talice|Drow]] impostor, who struck him with a venomous blade and left him gasping for breath.

## Key Developments

- Four personal trials earn the Spark of Curiosity, Tides of Balance, Roar of Pride, and Flame of Passion.
- The [[The Bookkeeper|Bookkeeper]] gives [[Prometheus]] the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]].
- The Ilefarn Prophecy revealed; [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] is the **Elven Oracle** and pinpoints the [[Phandelver|Lost Mines]].
- [[Halia]] revealed as the Bookkeeper's daughter; the party warned about the [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox Spiritus]].
- The party gains elemental rings of resistance and sets off for [[Leilon]].
- [[Talice]], a Drow shapeshifter posing as Halia, poisons [[Wild Fury]] and leaves him for dead.
