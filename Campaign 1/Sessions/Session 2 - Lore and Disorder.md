# Session 2: Lore and Disorder

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** Goblins beaten, a kidnapping uncovered, and ancient war carvings that unlocked the Warforged's memory.

## Summary

The party emerged victorious against the gang of goblins and wolves, uncovering clues of a kidnapping that led to "[[Cragmaw Hideout]]." A tense moment of interrogation between Fred and [[Prometheus]] ended in trust over a common goal.

After evading traps and scouting stealthily, the adventurers encountered and bypassed two negligent goblin guards before discovering a cave adorned with ancient war carvings. There, [[Prometheus]] shared the lore of the [[Phandelver's Pact]] and the storied past of [[Wave Echo Cave]].

The adventurers now stand ready to explore the cave's depths and unravel its secrets.

## Key Developments

- Defeated the goblin-and-wolf ambushers and found clues pointing to [[Cragmaw Hideout]] and a kidnapping.
- Fred and [[Prometheus]] reached an uneasy trust over their shared goal.
- The party bypassed two negligent goblin guards and found a cave of ancient war carvings.
- [[Prometheus]] recounted the lore of the [[Phandelver's Pact]] and [[Wave Echo Cave]].
