# Session 8: You, Me and Droopy

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** The [[Wolfpack]] defeat the [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]], learn the true [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] is dead, and leave [[Phandalin]] behind.

## Summary

The [[The Nothic|Nothic]] charged into battle against a bugbear, an imp, "Mayor Harbin Wester," and the formidable [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]]. The clash revealed the [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]]'s power source — his sword "Refraction." [[Glenn]] was temporarily downed, but rallied with a second wind as [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]] and [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] leapt into action. The party gained the upper hand, though at the cost of their [[The Nothic|Nothic]] ally.

The [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]]'s attempt to break [[Glenn]] by forcing him to choose between [[Walter & Kyle|Walter]] and [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] failed. The fake "Mayor Wester" fled, only for a shapeshifting Drow masquerading as [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar Halwinter]] to appear and then escape. The party disarmed and defeated the [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]], securing an enigmatic note from the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]] and the sword "Refraction" — claimable only by [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]. Chained in the next room, they found the real Harbin Wester and a goblin named [[Droop]].

As the dust settled, it was revealed that the true [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] had lost his life in the battle of [[Phandalin]]. Mourning the loss, the party donned new wolf armor and departed [[Phandalin]]. Disregarding [[Droop]]'s guidance and dismissing him, they ran into [[The Rembrandts|the Rembrandts]] once again, who sought to purchase "the robot man." [[Prometheus]] decisively ended the kobolds with a fiery explosion, closing the session with a bang.

## Key Developments

- The [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]] was defeated; "Refraction" claimed for [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]], plus a note from the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]].
- The [[The Nothic|Nothic]] died in the fight; the real Harbin Wester and [[Droop]] were found chained.
- The true [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] was revealed dead; [[Prometheus]] incinerated [[The Rembrandts|the Rembrandts]].
