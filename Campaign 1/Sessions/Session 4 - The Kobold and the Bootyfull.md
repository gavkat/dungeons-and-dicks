# Session 4: The Kobold and the Bootyfull

**Tags:** #session #Campaign1

**In short:** A Redbrand-haunted town, a stolen wine heist, a spellbook vision, and a hag who wants a silver comb.

## Summary

Departing [[Cragmaw Hideout]], the adventurers set out for [[Phandalin]]. Along the route they were briefly entertained by the "[[The Rembrandts|Rembrandts]]," a troupe of kobold artists. In town they met Barthen, who was fascinated by [[Prometheus]] and warned of [[The Redbrands]]' activities. Toblin, the innkeeper, provided shelter and lamented the theft of his wine by the [[The Rembrandts|Rembrandts]] — wine intended to lift the town's spirits from Redbrand oppression. Trilena, Toblin's wife, recounted [[The Dendrar Family|Thel Dendrar]] the woodcarver's tragic dealings with the Redbrands and his family's disappearance.

The group also met [[Lightning Steel]], who expressed his desire for vengeance against the Redbrands for ruining his theatrical endeavours. [[Sister Garaele]], at the Shrine of Luck, shared a vision that the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|spellbook of Mordenkainen]] could save the lost mine, and suggested that [[Agatha the Hag|Agatha]] the Hag might assist in exchange for a silver comb. She also spoke of the sisterly rivalry between the ancient Elves-turned-Gods, [[Tymora]] (Lady Luck) and [[Beshaba]] (Lady Mischief).

[[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] was led by clues to rendezvous with the enigmatic [[Halia]], who revealed a secret Redbrand base and advised seeking out Quelline Alderleaf for more information. [[Halia]] also hinted that the [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]] might not be as noble as he claims.

## Key Developments

- Arrived in [[Phandalin]]; the [[The Rembrandts|Rembrandts]] entertained the party and stole Toblin's wine.
- Met Barthen, Toblin and Trilena; learned of [[The Dendrar Family|Thel Dendrar]]'s fate and his family's disappearance under [[The Redbrands]].
- Met [[Lightning Steel]], who wants vengeance on the Redbrands.
- [[Sister Garaele]] revealed the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]] could save the mine and that [[Agatha the Hag|Agatha]] might help for a silver comb; she recounted the rivalry of [[Tymora]] and [[Beshaba]].
- [[Halia]] gave [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] a Redbrand base, pointed to Quelline Alderleaf, and hinted the [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]] is not as noble as he seems.
