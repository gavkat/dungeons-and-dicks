# Phandalin

**Tags:** #Location #Campaign1

---

## Overview
A frontier town near the lost mines (not to be confused with the mines themselves), once flourishing in the shadow of [[Wave Echo Cave]] and now oppressed by [[The Redbrands]] — a gang of ruffians revived by [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]]. It was the [[Wolfpack]]'s base of operations through the early campaign, home to allies like [[Sister Garaele]], [[Halia]] and [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]], and the staging ground for the battle that broke the Redbrands' grip.

## Sub-locations
- **Tresendar Manor** — the ruined manor above the Redbrand hideout; its basement, filled with elvish gold, hid the gang's lair and crypts.
- **The Stonehill Inn** & **the Sleeping Giant** — town taverns; the latter the site of the botched midnight ambush.
- **The Shrine of Luck** — [[Sister Garaele]]'s shrine to [[Tymora]].
- **The Edermath Orchard** — where the party first met [[Lightning Steel]].

## Session History

### Session 4
- Arrived in town; met Barthen, Toblin and Trilena; learned of the Dendrars' fate; met [[Lightning Steel]], [[Sister Garaele]] and [[Halia]]; first hints of the Glass Knight.

### Session 5
- Scouted Tresendar Manor; the Sleeping Giant ambush went sour, Lightning Steel "died", and the party was captured. After a chaotic escape sparked by a rat freeing [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]], they fled into Tresendar Manor's basement.

### Session 6
- Cleared the Redbrand lair and crypts beneath the manor; rescued [[The Dendrar Family]].

### Session 7
- Battled the [[Order of the Gauntlet]] and the [[The Nothic|Nothic]] in the depths.

### Session 8
- Defeated [[Iarno Albrek|the Glass Knight]]; learned the true [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] had died in the battle of Phandalin; the party departed.
