# Maglubiyet

**Tags:** #Deity #Campaign1

---

## Overview
The vile **orc and goblin war god** of the Prologue, his countenance a twisted visage of war and wrath. In an age long forgotten he led a horde of goblins and orcs from the North against the defenders of [[Wave Echo Cave]], breaking the peace of [[Phandelver's Pact]]. It was Maglubiyet's **bargain with [[The Dark Six]]** that dimmed the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] and doomed the warforged army the human wizards had birthed, ending in the cave's burial and the loss of the mine.

His remains endure: deep within Wave Echo Cave, in the shattered halls that were the birthplace of the Pact, lies the **massive skull of Maglubiyet** — a god's skull at the centre of a once-grand Dwarven hall.
