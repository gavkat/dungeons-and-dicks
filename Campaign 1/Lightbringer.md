# Lightbringer

**Tags:** #Item #Campaign1

---

## Overview
A holy sunblade — and one of the campaign's great reforged legacies. It began as **Refraction**, the glass sword of the Glass Knight [[Iarno Albrek]], the very source of his power, claimable only by [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] after the Glass Knight's defeat.

When Frederick fell to [[Lightning Steel]] and met **Lathander** in the abyss, the god offered revival — and transformed Refraction into the legendary **Lightbringer**, empowering Frederick to obliterate the vampire with a sunbeam. Choosing family over adventure, Frederick later entrusted the blade to [[Wild Fury]], who has carried it since. In the depths of [[Wave Echo Cave]], the living statue of [[King Dumathoin]] recognized Lightbringer as **his own creation from a bygone era** — a relic of [[Phandelver's Pact]] returned at last to the place it was forged.

## Session History

### Session 8: You, Me and Droopy
- The party defeated the Glass Knight and secured his glass sword "Refraction", claimable only by Frederick.

### Session 11: The Tortle and the Tortured
- In the abyss before Lathander, Refraction became Lightbringer; Frederick destroyed Lightning Steel with a sunbeam, then gave the blade to Wild Fury on leaving the party.

### Session 20: Vox
- The reunited party squabbled over possession of Lightbringer when Frederick returned.

### Session 24: Human Resources
- King Dumathoin's statue recognized Lightbringer, the blade now carried by Frederick, as his own ancient creation.

### Session 25: The Sheep & The Spider
- Guided by an unseen voice — *"There is always light in the darkness, for you are the Lightbringer"* — Frederick's Sunbeam struck the Black Spider true in the final assault.
