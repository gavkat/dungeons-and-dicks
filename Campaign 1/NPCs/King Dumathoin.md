# King Dumathoin

**Tags:** #character #npc #WorldBuilding #Campaign1

## Description
The **Dwarf** king of the [[Phandelver's Pact]] era — ruler of the dwarves who, in brotherhood with gnomes and human spellcasters, hewed [[Wave Echo Cave]] and birthed the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]]. He is the ancestor of the Rockseekers.

## Role in Campaign
Dumathoin led the charge defending the lost mines against the horde of orcs and goblins five hundred years ago — the cataclysmic war that doomed Wave Echo Cave. He first surfaced to the [[Wolfpack]] in [[Prometheus]]'s journal visions, glimpsed alongside [[Mordenkainen]] and [[Szass Tam]] in the days before the war.

Deep within the cave, his colossal living statue rose to bar the party's path, testing them. The stone king denounced [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] as an abomination, named [[Prometheus]] as **Mordenkainen reincarnated**, and recognized [[Lightbringer]] — now carried by [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] — as his own creation from a bygone age. He declared that only those of his lineage could proceed, and rumbled aside only when [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] stepped forward to claim his Rockseeker heritage.
