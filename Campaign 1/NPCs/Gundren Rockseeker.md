# Gundren Rockseeker

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Campaign1 #Deceased

## Description
**Dwarf Miner.** Gundren Rockseeker and his brothers, [[Tharden Rockseeker|Tharden]] and [[Nundro Rockseeker|Nundro]], located the lost mines of [[Phandelver]]. A descendant of [[King Dumathoin]], the dwarf king of [[Phandelver's Pact|Phandelver's Pact]] era. It was Gundren who hired the [[Wolfpack]].

## Role in Campaign
Gundren is the quest that starts it all: together with [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] he hired the party to escort a supply wagon to [[Phandalin]] before being captured by the Cragmaw tribe. Held in [[Cragmaw Castle]], he defied [[Finethir Shinebright|Shinebright]] to the last, spitting in his face and telling [[Glenn]] to *"find the Elven oracle!"* before Shinebright magically contorted his body to wrench out the location of [[Wave Echo Cave]]. Left vegetative, he was carried by the party for much of the journey and finally revived at [[Leilon]]'s Fountain of Fortune (Session 18).

His arc closes in his ancestral mine. The statue of [[King Dumathoin]] would grant passage only to his lineage — and Gundren stepped forward to claim his heritage. In the Forge battle he charged his corrupted brother [[Tharden Rockseeker|Tharden]] and, in a final struggle, tackled him off the bridge, **sacrificing himself** to end Tharden's dark path. **Status: Dead** (Session 25).
