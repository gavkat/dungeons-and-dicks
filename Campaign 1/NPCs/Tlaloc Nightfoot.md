# Tlaloc Nightfoot

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Campaign1

## Description
**Half-Orc/Ranger.** Raised as a monster-hunter in an Orc tribe, Tlaloc and his brother [[Guz Nightfoot|Guz]] once did whatever they had to in order to survive. When Guz vanished, Tlaloc dedicated his life to finding him.

## Role in Campaign
Tlaloc's loyalties swing across the whole campaign — prequel ally, then antagonist, then ally once more. He first appeared at the Daggerford fair as a half-orc monster-slayer, crashing the crowd in pursuit of his brother [[Guz Nightfoot|Guz]]; he tried and failed to save Guz from [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]. After Guz's death he joined the Cragmaw tribe and sided with the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]], standing beside [[King Grol]] as an interrogator and demanding the party grovel.

When the tide turned against Grol, Tlaloc hinted at a possible alliance, and after the king's death [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] convinced him to stand down. The party made amends: Tlaloc let them loot Grol, take the vegetative [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]], and gifted them **a summoning horn** to call him in a time of need. He honored that promise in the finale — summoned by the horn to fight at the Forge. **Status: Alive.**
