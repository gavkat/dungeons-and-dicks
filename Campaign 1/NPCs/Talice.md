# Talice

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Campaign1

## Description
A **Drow/Hunter**, a shapeshifter and henchman of [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]]. Her signature weapon is deception itself: she repeatedly impersonates the party's allies to sow confusion and strike from within.

## Role in Campaign
First glimpsed in the Redbrand Hideout masquerading as the late [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar Halwinter]] before slipping away. She later impersonated [[Halia]] to lure and poison [[Wild Fury]] with a venomous blade, leaving him gasping in the forest. This near-fatal trick prompted the party to adopt the codeword *"Meatloaf"* to verify one another's identities.

That codeword proved her undoing. At the smeltery throne room, after a duplicate [[Glenn]] led the party astray, the real Glenn invoked *"Meatloaf"* — exposing Talice the Drow, who drew [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s blood and vanished. **Status: Dead.** She was finally felled by the Wolfpack at the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] alongside [[Nezznar]] (Session 25).
