# Elder Reidoth

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Thri-kreen** cultist and the cult leader of the followers of [[Silvanus]] at [[Thundertree]]. (Carp Alderleaf had named him as a contact for the party.)

## Role in Campaign
Reidoth led the Silvanus cult that had already sacrificed the people of [[Thundertree]] to their god. When the [[Wolfpack]] arrived, he and his fellow elders [[Elder Marcus|Marcus]] and [[Sister Fredericka|Fredericka]] proposed a trade: directions to [[Cragmaw Castle]] in exchange for [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s allegiance and his life as a sacrifice. The refusal triggered a fierce confrontation, the cult's ritual summoning zombies and animating a colossal tree monster.

**Status: Dead.** Cut down by [[Walter & Kyle|Walter and Kyle]] amid the chaos of the battle.

## Session History

### Session 9
- Offered [[Cragmaw Castle]]'s location for Frederick's sacrifice; when refused, raised undead and a tree monster. Killed by [[Walter & Kyle|Walter and Kyle]].
