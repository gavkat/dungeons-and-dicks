# Sister Garaele

**Tags:** #character #npc #QuestGiver #Ally #Campaign1

## Description
An **Elf/Cleric** of [[Tymora]], Lady Luck, tending the Shrine of Luck in [[Phandalin]] — and a secret **Harpy**. After her seeming death she returned, briefly, as the **Vox Spiritus of Tymora**, the goddess's material avatar.

## Role in Campaign
Garaele set the central quest in motion: a vision told her that [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen|Mordenkainen's spellbook]] could save the lost mine, and she sent the [[Wolfpack]] to barter a silver comb with [[Agatha the Hag|Agatha]] in exchange for the spellbook's whereabouts. She also taught them of the old rivalry between the Elves-turned-Gods [[Tymora]] (Lady Luck) and [[Beshaba]] (Lady Mischief) — the sisters whose feud would shape the campaign's climax.

Her Harpy form revealed itself in the chaotic battle of [[Phandalin]], where she fought alongside the party against the [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]]. Much later, cradling the returned [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]], she joined the final ascent of [[Wyvern Tor]] — and there she used a Harpy spell to expose the dragon's deepest desire (the silhouette of a fairy).

### Vox Spiritus of Tymora
When the false [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox]] struck Garaele down in a fit of rage, divine intervention raised her again — not as the Elf cleric, but as a radiant warrior wearing **Tymora's visage**, the Vox Spiritus of Tymora. In the sky above [[Wyvern Tor]] she waged the battle of sisters against the **Vox of Beshaba**, buying the [[Wolfpack]] the opening to slay [[Switchblade]] and seize victory.
