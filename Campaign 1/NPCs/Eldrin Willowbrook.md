# Eldrin Willowbrook

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
"Headmaster Willowbrook" — a quirky **Human/Wizard** and the head of the Leilon Adventuring Academy. A man who *loves a game*, he presided over the school's halls of young would-be adventurers in [[Leilon]].

## Role in Campaign
When the [[Wolfpack]] came to [[Leilon]] seeking recruits, it was Willowbrook they had to win over — convincing the headmaster to let two of his teachers, [[Garrick Dreldan]] and [[Tolgun Aer'thi|Tolgun Aer'Thi]], join the party's quest.

**Status: Dead.** When a red dragon (secretly [[Switchblade]]) razed Leilon, the Adventuring Academy was among the first to fall. Willowbrook was crushed mid-spell, casting a protection enchantment to shield a handful of schoolchildren from the collapsing building.

## Session History

### Session 18
- Persuaded by the party to release Garrick Dreldan and Tolgun Aer'thi from teaching duties to join the quest.

### Session 19
