# Betty

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Campaign1

## Description
A **Tiefling** commoner — the beloved of [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] and the mother of his child, Fred Jr.

## Role in Campaign
Betty was the lever [[Lightning Steel]] used against [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]. The vampire prince held her hostage in his crypts beneath [[Cragmaw Castle]] as he revealed his plan to seize [[Wave Echo Cave]] and raise a vampire empire from the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]].

In the battle that followed, Lightning Steel struck Frederick down and slashed Betty's throat before his eyes. But Frederick, revived by Lathander and wielding the newly reforged [[Lightbringer]], obliterated the vampire — then resurrected Betty, who revealed she was pregnant. Choosing family over adventure, Frederick left the [[Wolfpack]] to be with her.

By the **epilogue**, domesticity had soured: Frederick's endless retellings of his heroics drove Betty away, leaving him with custody of Fred Jr. every second weekend.
