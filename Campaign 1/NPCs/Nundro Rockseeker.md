# Nundro Rockseeker

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Dwarf**, brother to [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] and Tharden. With his siblings, he located the lost mines of [[Wave Echo Cave]].

## Role in Campaign
**Status: Dead.** Murdered by his own brother [[Tharden Rockseeker|Tharden]], who betrayed the family to the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]]. [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] found Nundro's body upon reaching [[Wave Echo Cave]], Tharden gone missing.

### Session 21
