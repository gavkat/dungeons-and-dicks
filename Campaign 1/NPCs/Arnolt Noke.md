# Arnolt Noke

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Human/Wizard**, apprentice to [[Finethir Shinebright]]. His all-consuming obsession with the fairy bard [[Dew-Dew Bun]] curdled into mania and betrayal, making him the prequel's apparent antagonist — the supposed "rogue apprentice" the disguised Shinebright begged the party to stop.

## Role in Campaign
The villain of [[Prequel - The Wild Sheep's Chase|the prequel]], styled by the city rangers as the mysterious "Master Noke" behind the silent [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]]. A fanatical admirer of Dew-Dew Bun, he summoned zombies clad in her merchandise and, when cornered in the tower, rode a dragon stitched from bedsheets, bedposts and linen — a panic-fuelled creation of the modified [[Wand of True Polymorph]].

**Status: Dead.** After his bed-dragon fell, Noke turned the wand on himself, becoming a monstrous Gibbering Mouther, and was defeated by the united might of the adventurers. He served as the unwitting decoy that let his master Shinebright stay hidden as a harmless sheep.
