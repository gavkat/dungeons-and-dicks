# Tolgun Aer'thi

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Companion #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
"Professor Tolgun Aer'thi" — an **Aasimar** teacher of history and lore at the Leilon Adventuring Academy, and a skilled archer besides.

## Role in Campaign
One of two Academy teachers the [[Wolfpack]] recruited in [[Leilon]] from [[Eldrin Willowbrook|Headmaster Willowbrook]], alongside [[Garrick Dreldan]]. His tenure with the party was tragically brief.

**Status: Dead.** When the red dragon (secretly [[Switchblade]]) attacked Leilon, Tolgun flew in to help fight it — only to be swatted away by the dragon's tail, dying instantly on impact with a building.

## Session History

### Session 18
