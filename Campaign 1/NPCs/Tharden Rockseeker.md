# Tharden Rockseeker

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Dwarf/Fighter**, eldest of the Rockseeker brothers — sibling to [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] and [[Nundro Rockseeker|Nundro]]. Together the three brothers located the lost mines of [[Phandelver]]. But Tharden betrayed his family, throwing in his lot with [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]].

## Role in Campaign
The traitor in the Rockseeker line. After helping discover [[Wave Echo Cave]], Tharden murdered Nundro and aided the Black Spider in reaching the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]]. When Gundren entered the cave he found Nundro dead and Tharden missing — the betrayal not yet understood.

At the Forge, Tharden stood among the Black Spider's forces, shockingly corrupted, and Gundren charged at his estranged brother. **Status: Dead.** In a final, fierce struggle Gundren tackled Tharden off the bridge, sacrificing himself to end his brother's dark path (Session 25).
