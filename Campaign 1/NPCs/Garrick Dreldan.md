# Garrick Dreldan

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Companion #Campaign1

## Description
"Master Garrick Dreldan" — a **Human/Fighter**, former adventurer turned teacher of combat and strategy at the Leilon Adventuring Academy in [[Leilon]].

## Role in Campaign
One of the two Academy teachers the [[Wolfpack]] recruited from [[Eldrin Willowbrook|Headmaster Willowbrook]], alongside [[Tolgun Aer'thi]]. Where Tolgun did not survive the dragon's arrival, Garrick did — barely. When the Adventuring Academy fell, he stumbled out of the wreckage *burnt and dismembered, but alive*.

**Status:** Survivor of the Leilon dragon attack.

## Session History

### Session 18
- Recruited to the party from the Leilon Adventuring Academy.

### Session 19
- Survived the collapse of the Academy during the dragon attack, emerging burnt but alive.
