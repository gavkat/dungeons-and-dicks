# Sister Fredericka

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Tortle** cultist of [[Silvanus]], and the ex-wife of [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]. A piece of his abandoned past, waiting at [[Thundertree]].

## Role in Campaign
At the ruined town of [[Thundertree]], the [[Wolfpack]] ran into [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s history made flesh: [[Elder Reidoth|Reidoth]], [[Elder Marcus|Marcus]], and Fredericka. She disclosed the cult's sinister plan — to sacrifice the town's inhabitants to [[Silvanus]] — and the elders offered Cragmaw Castle's location in trade for Frederick's allegiance and his own sacrifice. The party refused, and battle broke out.

**Status: Dead.** [[Walter & Kyle|Walter and Kyle]] vanquished Fredericka (and Reidoth) during the ritual fight, to Frederick's dismay. In the rubble afterward, a letter from his departed ex-wife moved the whole group to tears.
