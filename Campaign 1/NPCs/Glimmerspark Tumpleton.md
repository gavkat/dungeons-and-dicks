# Glimmerspark Tumpleton

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Campaign1

## Description
A **Gnome/Artificer** — a skilled tinkerer and blacksmith, and the owner of *"The Tinkerer's Toolbox"* in [[Leilon]].

## Role in Campaign
The [[Wolfpack]] first met Glimmerspark while browsing Leilon's shops, though they struggled to strike a favorable deal with him. He proved his worth during the dragon attack: as Lila Greenleaf's *"lightning in a bottle"* was passed from [[Wild Fury]] into his hands, Glimmerspark used it to forge a **Javelin of Lightning**, which he presented to Wild Fury in the aftermath.

## Echoes of Phandelver
Glimmerspark is the implied namesake and founder of [[Glimmerspark Industries]] — the technology company that rises in [[Echoes of Phandelver]], five years on, building the prototype [[Sending Phone]] and carrying the gnome artificer's spark of invention into the new industrial age.
