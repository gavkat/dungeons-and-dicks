# Yeemik

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Goblin**, second-in-command of the Cragmaw tribe at the [[Cragmaw Hideout]].

## Role in Campaign
With his bugbear boss [[Klarg]] elsewhere in the cave, Yeemik held [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] captive among his goblins. When the [[Wolfpack]] came to free their friend, he tried to negotiate his way out of trouble.

**Status: Dead.** A psionic dagger exchange with [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] ended the bargaining permanently.

## Session History

### Session 3
- Attempted to negotiate over the captive [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]]; died after a psionic dagger exchange with [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]].
