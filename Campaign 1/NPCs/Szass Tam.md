# Szass Tam

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Campaign1

## Description
A **Human/Wizard** turned **Lich**. He was [[Mordenkainen]]'s best friend (and, perhaps, lover). Where Mordenkainen refused [[The Dark Six]]' offers, Szass Tam accepted them — and was granted the eternal, undead life that made him a lich.

## Role in Campaign
A creature of dark bargains, haunting the depths of [[Wave Echo Cave]]. Prometheus first glimpsed him in a journal-vision of the war 500 years past, scheming alongside Mordenkainen and [[King Dumathoin]]. He later materialized in the flesh, claiming to have awaited the party for ages and demanding they submit to the Dark Six, confessing he took the deities' deal after Mordenkainen refused.

At the starry-cavern bridge he raised thousands of corpses, and when [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle]] charged him he answered with a single word — *"Down"* — a **Power Word Kill** that ended Kyle instantly. He reanimated Kyle as his pet and whispered *"Beshaba"* to [[Prometheus]] before departing (Session 23).

In the [[Epilogue|epilogue]], Szass Tam carries straight into [[Echoes of Phandelver]]: he keeps Prometheus — as Mordenkainen — tethered in a mysterious dimension, his soul a siphon for the negative energy of an undead Beholder, tormenting rather than killing him: *"I made a promise I wouldn't kill you, so I'll just have to make you suffer."*
