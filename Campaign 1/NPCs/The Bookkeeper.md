# The Bookkeeper

**Tags:** #character #npc #QuestGiver #Ally #Campaign1

## Description
An otherworldly **Vedalken/Sorcerer**, revealed to be the father of [[Halia]] — sworn to guard the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]] until [[Prometheus]]'s arrival.

## Role in Campaign
At the summit of the Old Owl watchtower above [[Old Owl Well]], the Bookkeeper awaited the [[Wolfpack]] — claiming to have expected them for years. He bestowed the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]] upon [[Prometheus]], declaring it his destiny.

He then unveiled the Ilefarn Prophecy, *"Efarn Euox Bryn Il"* — phonetically "Elven Oracle," meaning *the one to see beyond the light* — and revealed [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] himself as the **Elven Oracle**. When the Scarlet Owl that had guided their final trial transformed into [[Halia]], he named her his daughter.

Crucially, the Bookkeeper warned the party about the **Vox Spiritus**, hinting at its potentially malevolent nature — a caution that proved true when it was unmasked as the [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba]]. He gifted them elemental rings of resistance forged from the Guardian construct and the shattered gems.
