# The Nothic

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A telepathic **Nothic** lurking in the crevices of the [[The Redbrands|Redbrand]] lair beneath Tresendar Manor. In the campaign's deeper lore, the Nothics are what remained of the cursed [[The Ilefarn|Ilefarn]] nation after their leaders became [[The Dark Six]].

## Role in Campaign
After a tense telepathic exchange, the creature withdrew into its crevice — but proved an unlikely ally. When [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] tumbled into the crevice and found it guarding a chest and a corpse, the party fed it their foes; the Nothic, satisfied, charged into battle on their side against the [[Order of the Gauntlet]] ambushers and later a bugbear, an imp, the false "Mayor Wester," and [[Iarno Albrek|the Glass Knight]].

**Status: Dead.** The [[Wolfpack]] gained the upper hand in that final clash, but at the cost of their Nothic ally.
