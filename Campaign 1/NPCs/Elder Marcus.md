# Elder Marcus

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Vulpin** cultist of [[Silvanus]] — and once an old friend of [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]], now gone mad after sacrificing the town of [[Thundertree]] to his god.

## Role in Campaign
One of the three Silvanus elders the [[Wolfpack]] confronted at [[Thundertree]], alongside [[Elder Reidoth|Reidoth]] and [[Sister Fredericka|Fredericka]]. When the party refused the cult's bargain — Cragmaw Castle's location for [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s sacrifice — Marcus joined the ritual battle.

**Status: Dead.** He met his end in a deluded charge straight into [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s Spirit Guardians.
