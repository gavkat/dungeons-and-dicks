# The Thornes

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A father and son who served [[Leilon]] together:
- **Captain Eammon Thorne** (**Human/Fighter**) — the charming captain of the town guard, who met the [[Wolfpack]] at the gates and laid out the town's rules.
- **Father Alaric Thorne** (**Human/Commoner**) — Eammon's father, devoted priest at the Temple of [[Tymora]] and keeper of the Fountain of Fortune.

## Role in Campaign
The Thornes were the party's first friendly faces in [[Leilon]]. Eammon welcomed them through the gates and sent them on to his father. Alaric used the restorative waters of the Fountain of Fortune — created by [[Tymora]] from the holy runoff of [[Wyvern Tor]] — to revive [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] from his vegetative state. He explained that the fountain's magic could not be moved; the water lost its potency within about five minutes of leaving the basin.

**Status: Dead.** When the red dragon attacked, its tail came crashing down upon the Fountain of Fortune. Both Thornes leapt in to protect it — and the once-magical water turned red with their blood.
