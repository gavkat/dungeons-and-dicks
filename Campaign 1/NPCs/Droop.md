# Droop

**Tags:** #character #npc #Mystery #Campaign1

## Description
A simple **Goblin** commoner, once a slave of [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]], found chained in their lair alongside the real Harbin Wester.

## Role in Campaign
After the [[Wolfpack]] defeated [[Iarno Albrek|the Glass Knight]], they discovered Droop and the true Mayor Wester in the next room. Droop offered the party his guidance and companionship — and was flatly rejected, dismissed as they departed [[Phandalin]].

That cruelty did not go unrepaid. Years later, in the **epilogue**, a black-eyed Droop appeared at [[Glenn]]'s cottage in [[Littlefoot]]: eyes pitch black, foaming bile, rasping a sinister *"Howdy Glenn"* before lunging to attack. The blackened eyes mark him as one of [[The Tethered]] — the dark plague threading into [[Echoes of Phandelver]], the campaign to come.
