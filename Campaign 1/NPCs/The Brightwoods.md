# The Brightwoods

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Satyr** family of [[Leilon]]:
- **Merrick** & **Cassie Brightwood** — run *"The Tipsy Trot"*, a local tavern and inn.
- **Tamás Brightwood** — their son, a student at the Leilon Adventuring Academy, whose recently-lost dog became a quest hook for the [[Wolfpack]].

## Role in Campaign
A small, ordinary family caught in the tide of the party's visit to [[Leilon]]. Young Tamás's missing dog set the party off toward the town butchery in search of it — an errand that ended in horror when a dog's head fell from the sky moments before the red dragon descended.

**Status:** Cassie and Tamás were both killed in the dragon's attack on Leilon. As the dragon departed, it carried Tamás off as a final prey. Merrick survived.
