# King Grol

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
An **Orc/Barbarian**, King of the Cragmaw tribe. A cruel, brawny tyrant ruling from [[Cragmaw Castle]], working for [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]].

## Role in Campaign
Grol held [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]], [[Wild Fury]] and [[Glenn]] captive for the Black Spider, demanding from Gundren the location of [[Wave Echo Cave]]. When Gundren refused, Grol had him and Glenn knocked out, later kicking Glenn from a high window (sparking Glenn's transformation into his bestial form) and threatening to kill [[Walter & Kyle|Walter and Kyle]] on sight.

**Status: Dead.** In his throne room, Grol defied [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]]'s orders, choke-slammed [[Prometheus]] unconscious, then turned on Glenn — only to be annihilated by Glenn, Walter and Kyle in a triumphant joint assault (Session 14). His death and Tlaloc's stand-down let the party loot him, take the vegetative Gundren, and receive Tlaloc's summoning horn.
