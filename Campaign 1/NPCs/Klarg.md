# Klarg

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Bugbear**, leader of the Cragmaw tribe stationed at the [[Cragmaw Hideout]]. Kept a pet dire wolf named Ripper.

## Role in Campaign
Klarg worked for the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]], who wanted [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] and the map to the secret location of [[Wave Echo Cave]]. While he lurked elsewhere in the cave, his lieutenant [[Yeemik]] held [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] prisoner.

**Status: Dead.** After [[Glenn]] used *speak with animals* to turn the tamed wolves [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle and Walter]] against Ripper, the [[Wolfpack]] stealthily infiltrated his lair and ambushed him and his minions.

## Session History

### Session 3
- Ambushed and killed in his lair after [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle and Walter]] deceived his dire wolf Ripper.
