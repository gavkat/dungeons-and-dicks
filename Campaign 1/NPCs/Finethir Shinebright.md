# Finethir Shinebright

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Human/Wizard**, and the campaign's true big bad — better known as **The Black Spider**. He first appeared not as a villain but as a helpless, innocent *sheep*, a polymorphed wizard begging the party to break his curse and stop his "rogue apprentice" [[Arnolt Noke|Noke]]. The disguise was the long con of a master manipulator; his calling card was a handkerchief embroidered with a black spider.

## Role in Campaign
The puppeteer behind nearly everything that befell the [[Wolfpack]]. Shinebright was the architect of the revived [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]], the orchestrator of the party's woes, and the hand that hired the [[Iarno Albrek|Glass Knight]], commanded the [[Talice|drow]] [[Nezznar|henchmen]], and corrupted [[Tharden Rockseeker|Tharden]]. His goal: reach the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] in [[Wave Echo Cave]] and use the modified [[Wand of True Polymorph]] to claim eternal life.

At the [[Prequel - The Wild Sheep's Chase|prequel]] he revealed his true colours — silencing [[Dew-Dew Bun]], exposing [[Glenn]] as an experiment, naming [[Switchblade]] as a Redbrand complicit in the massacre of [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s people, and vanishing while freezing the party in time. He extracted the location of the lost mines from [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] by breaking his body with a spell, then raced the Wolfpack to the Forge.

At the Forge, mirroring the ancient [[The Ilefarn|Ilefarn]] *"tri animalus arcanum"* ritual, he used the blood of Glenn, [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] and [[Wild Fury]] and the wand to transform himself into a **Giant Drider** — a half-human, half-spider abomination, finally becoming the true Black Spider. **Status: Dead.** Destroyed by the Wolfpack's unified assault after his last-ditch Prismatic Spray (Session 25). His defeat cleared the way for [[Prometheus]] to reignite the Forge and birth the new industrial age of [[Echoes of Phandelver]].
