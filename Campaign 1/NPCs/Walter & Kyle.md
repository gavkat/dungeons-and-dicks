# Walter & Kyle

**Tags:** #character #npc #Companion #Campaign1

## Description
The two wolves of the [[Cragmaw Hideout]], tamed by [[Glenn]] and adopted as the party's beloved animal companions. The glossary sums them up best: *the best boys.* [[Glenn]] became their surrogate father.

## Role in Campaign
[[Glenn]] tamed Kyle and Walter during the hideout rescue in Session 3, and using *speak with animals* enlisted them to deceive [[Klarg]]'s dire wolf, Ripper. Thereafter the pair fought through the whole campaign — handling ghosts and ghouls under [[Lightning Steel]] (Walter aged 20 years in the process), helping bring down the Mother Grick, joining [[Glenn]] to annihilate [[King Grol]], and growling down a lich.

In Session 23, beneath [[Wave Echo Cave]], Kyle charged [[Szass Tam]] and was instantly slain by a single word — *"Down"* — the lich's **Power Word Kill**. Before departing, Szass Tam reanimated Kyle as his own undead pet. **Kyle's Status: Dead** (reanimated as Szass Tam's thrall). Walter survived.

## Echoes of Phandelver
In the epilogue, Walter retires with [[Glenn]] to a cozy [[Littlefoot]] cottage, days by the fire — until [[Droop]], black-eyed and rasping, comes knocking. Walter (and his growl) continues at Glenn's side into [[Echoes of Phandelver]].
