# Sylvinia Salazar

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
"Silvinia" — a **Lizardfolk** commoner of [[Leilon]], and [[Glenn]]'s biggest fan. She is the daughter of Pierre, frontman of [[The Rembrandts]] and a former lover of Glenn's.

## Role in Campaign
Silvinia fell for [[Glenn]] over a night of his drunken bardic entertainment at the local tavern, showering him with loving attention. The two shared an intimate encounter, after which she revealed her parentage — that she was Pierre's daughter, tying her to Glenn's old flame among [[The Rembrandts]].

**Status: Dead.** When the red dragon set the tavern ablaze, [[Glenn]] — on the upper level — chose to leap from the window saving [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] over Silvinia. She perished in the fire.

## Session History
