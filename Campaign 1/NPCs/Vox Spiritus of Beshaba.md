# Vox Spiritus of Beshaba

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
The "goddess" who reanimated [[Prometheus]] — first appearing as a beautiful, angelic celestial claiming to be the **Vox Spiritus of [[Tymora]]**, a force of good. In truth she was the **Vox** (material avatar) of [[Beshaba]], Lady Mischief and a member of [[The Dark Six]]. The [[The Bookkeeper|Bookkeeper]] cautioned the party about her potentially malevolent nature long before her mask slipped.

## Role in Campaign
She granted Prometheus his second life and his powers, then guided the party toward [[Mordenkainen]]'s spellbook — ostensibly so she could attune to it and stop [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]]. Her demand to take the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]] at [[Wyvern Tor]] exposed the lie: she revealed herself as the evil Vox of Beshaba and fought the party alongside her pet dragon, later revealed to be the reincarnated [[Switchblade]].

**Status: Dead.** After her dragon was beheaded by [[Glenn]] and finished by [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]], [[Wild Fury]] misty-stepped to her and smote the final blow. A spectral remnant of Beshaba's Vox rose once more wailing *"GIVE ME MY SPELLBOOK!"*, but Prometheus banished her with a radiant longsword drawn from the heart of the Spellbook (Session 21). Her battle against her sister's avatar, the Vox Spiritus of [[Tymora]] ([[Sister Garaele]]), tied the campaign's climax to the war of the Dark Six that echoes into [[Echoes of Phandelver]].
