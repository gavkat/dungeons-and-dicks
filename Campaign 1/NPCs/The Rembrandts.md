# The Rembrandts

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A troupe of **Kobold** artists, fronted by Pierre. Better at performing than at honest work — and oddly persistent in the [[Wolfpack]]'s path.

## Role in Campaign
First encountered on the road outside [[Phandalin]], where they briefly entertained the party. They had been hired by the innkeeper Toblin to lift the town's spirits under [[The Redbrands|Redbrand]] oppression, but instead stole Phandalin's local supply of wine. They later turned up again seeking to *purchase "the robot man"* — [[Prometheus]].

Pierre was, it emerged, an old lover of [[Glenn]]; his daughter [[Sylvinia Salazar|Silvinia]] would surface in [[Leilon]] much later.

**Status: Dead.** [[Prometheus]] decisively ended the kobolds with a fiery explosion.
