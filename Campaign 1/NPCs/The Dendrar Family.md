# The Dendrar Family

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Human** family of commoners from [[Phandalin]]: **Thel** the woodcarver, his wife **Mirna**, and their children **Nars** and **Nelsa**.

## Role in Campaign
The Dendrars became a cautionary tale of life under [[The Redbrands|Redbrand]] rule. The party first heard of them from Trilena at the inn: Thel had stood up to the Redbrands, and his family had since vanished. The truth was grimmer — Thel had been murdered, and Mirna and the children dragged off to the cells beneath Tresendar Manor.

**Status (Thel): Dead.** Murdered by the [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]] for his defiance.

The [[Wolfpack]] found Mirna, Nars and Nelsa alive in the Redbrand prison and freed them. In gratitude, Mirna revealed the location of a family heirloom hidden at [[Thundertree]] as their reward.
