# The Forge Warforged

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Campaign1

## Description
Four **Warforged** combatants that [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]] deployed at the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] to slow the [[Wolfpack]] in their final confrontation. Grim echoes of [[Prometheus]]'s own kind, each was built around a class:

- **Wrecker** — **Warforged/Barbarian**.
- **Crucible** — **Warforged/Cleric**.
- **Bastion** — **Warforged/Paladin**.
- **Trace** — **Warforged/Monk**.

## Role in Campaign
Menacing minions positioned to delay the party's assault at the Forge. The Wolfpack defeated them by using the bridge to their advantage — aided by the return of [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]], summoned via the horn he had gifted them. A colossal golden **warforged titan** then descended, but [[Halia]], as a Giant Ape, toppled it off the bridge at the cost of falling unconscious.
