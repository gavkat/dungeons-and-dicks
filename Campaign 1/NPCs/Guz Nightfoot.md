# Guz Nightfoot

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Half-Orc/Barbarian**, brother to [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]] and leader of the resurged [[The Redbrands|Redbrands]]. Raised alongside Tlaloc as survivors who did whatever they had to; his mysterious disappearance was the very thing that sent Tlaloc on a lifelong hunt to find him.

## Role in Campaign
The Half-orc who crashed through the [[Daggerford]] fair with a beastly entourage in the [[Prequel - The Wild Sheep's Chase|prequel]], drawing the future [[Wolfpack]] into the fight. Tlaloc confronted his own brother across the battlefield.

**Status: Dead.** Slain by [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] despite Tlaloc's desperate attempts to save him — a death that drove Tlaloc to join the Cragmaw tribe and side with [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]].
