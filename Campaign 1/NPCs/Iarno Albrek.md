# Iarno Albrek

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
"The Glass Knight" — a **Tiefling/Paladin**, an oathbreaker and dissenter of the Lord's Alliance. Originally named to the party by [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]] as a contact in [[Phandalin]] who might help, he was in truth the leader of [[The Redbrands]], hired by [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]]. His power flowed from his glass sword, *Refraction*.

## Role in Campaign
The Black Spider's enforcer in Phandalin. The Glass Knight interrogated the captured Wolfpack about the Lost Mines' cargo, and a terrified Redbrand later confessed under [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s bowtie monologue that the Glass Knight also served the Black Spider. He kept a hidden escape pack and ruled through a fake "Mayor Harbin Wester."

**Status: Dead.** Defeated by the party, who disarmed him, claimed his sword *Refraction*, and seized an enigmatic note from the Black Spider (Session 8). *Refraction* — claimable only by Frederick — was later reforged by Lathander into [[Lightbringer]].
