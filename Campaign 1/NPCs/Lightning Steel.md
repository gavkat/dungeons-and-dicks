# Lightning Steel

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
Prince Darren Edermath — a centuries-old **Vampire** prince, leader of the [[Order of the Gauntlet]] and an acclaimed Folk Hero and thespian. He presented himself as a heroic adventurer wronged by the Redbrands, but beneath the showmanship lay a vampire who had once made a deal with [[Beshaba]] for eternal youth — a bargain that twisted him from prince to monster and led him to slaughter his own kin.

## Role in Campaign
[[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]]'s personal hero. Lightning Steel courted the [[Wolfpack]]'s aid against [[The Redbrands]], then "died" mid-monologue at the Sleeping Giant tavern to a "Queen of Hearts" card — only to return as a vampire. He offered the party guidance into [[Cragmaw Castle]] and an "invitation" to [[Wave Echo Cave]], revealing his true plan: commandeer the cave and use the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] to resurrect a vampire empire.

**Status: Dead.** When the party turned on him he held Frederick's beloved [[Betty]] hostage and struck Frederick down, slashing Betty's throat. In death's abyss Frederick met Lathander, who reforged his glass sword *[[Lightbringer|Refraction]]* into [[Lightbringer]]; revived, Frederick obliterated Lightning Steel with a sunbeam (Session 11). Three years later, in the [[Epilogue|epilogue]], Frederick is arrested by the [[Mage Guard|MageGuard]] for this very murder — a thread that carries into [[Echoes of Phandelver]].
