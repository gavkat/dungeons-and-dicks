# Nezznar

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #Campaign1

## Description
A **Drow/Mage** in the service of [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]]. Little is known of him beyond his allegiance — an enigmatic spellcaster who commanded the [[The Redback Enclave|Redback cultists]].

## Role in Campaign
First seen leading a large group of red-cloaked cultists at the entrance to [[Wave Echo Cave]], where he vanished into thin air before the party's eyes, leaving two guards to summon a cave troll. He resurfaced at the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] among the Black Spider's allies, helping Shinebright reach the Forge.

**Status: Dead.** Felled by the Wolfpack at the Forge alongside [[Talice]] (Session 25).

## Session History

### Session 12
- Identified as one of the drow accompanying Shinebright into Cragmaw Castle.

### Session 21
- Led the Redback cultists at the mouth of Wave Echo Cave, then teleported away.

### Session 25
- Aided Shinebright in reaching the Forge; killed by the Wolfpack.
