# Cragmaw Castle

**Tags:** #Location #Campaign1

---

## Overview
The ruined fortress of [[King Grol]] and the seat of the Cragmaw tribe — a sprawling, multi-level dungeon where [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]], [[Glenn]] and [[Wild Fury]] were all held captive for [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]]. The [[Wolfpack]] fought through its crumbling halls level by level: rescuing the imprisoned tabaxi paladin Wild Fury III, surviving mimics, goblin feasts and a Grick puzzle, and ultimately confronting King Grol himself. It was here that Shinebright tortured the cave's location from Gundren, and here that [[Tlaloc Nightfoot|Tlaloc]] turned from foe back toward ally.

## Sub-locations
- **The Crypts** — Lightning Steel's domain, where the vampire revealed his vampire-empire plan.
- **The Grand Staircase & Storeroom** — trap-laden ascent home to a nest of mimics, where Wild Fury's armor and weapons were recovered.
- **The Dining Hall & Kitchen** — second level; the goblin feast, the Goblinettes, and the brawl with the chef and his mastiffs.
- **The Throne Room** — King Grol's seat, where the climactic battle and Owlie McBear's rampage took place.
- **The Temple Shrine & Library** — the final storey; the Grick puzzle and the mending shrine where the Vox Spiritus first appeared.
