# Cragmaw Hideout

**Tags:** #Location #Campaign1

---

## Overview
A goblin cave of the Cragmaw tribe, and the [[Wolfpack]]'s first proper dungeon. Clues from the ambush on the High Road led the party here in search of the kidnapped [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] and [[Sildar Halwinter|Sildar]]. Within its damp tunnels — past lazy guards, a flood-trap and a chamber of ancient war carvings — the party found Sildar held captive and learned the first threads of the [[Finethir Shinebright|Black Spider]]'s plot. It was also where [[Glenn]] tamed the wolves [[Walter & Kyle|Kyle and Walter]].

## Sub-locations
- **The War-Carving Cave** — a chamber adorned with ancient carvings, where [[Prometheus]] recounted the lore of the [[Phandelver's Pact]] and [[Wave Echo Cave]].
- **Klarg's Lair** — the den of [[Klarg]] the bugbear and his dire wolf Ripper, deep in the hideout.
