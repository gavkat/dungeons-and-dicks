## Overview
A dreaded passage on the [Sea of Swords](Sea%20of%20Swords.md): a wall of unnatural fog, a claustrophobic channel of jagged black cliffs, submerged rocks, and a graveyard of ships. Haunted by a spectral galleon of ghost pirates.
