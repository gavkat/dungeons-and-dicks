# The Choke

**Tags:** #Location

---

## Overview
A dreaded passage on the [[Sea of Swords]]: a wall of unnatural fog, a claustrophobic channel of jagged black cliffs, submerged rocks, and a graveyard of ships. Haunted by a spectral galleon of ghost pirates.
