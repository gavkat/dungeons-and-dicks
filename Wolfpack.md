# The Wolfpack

**Tags:** #Faction #Lore #WorldBuilding

---

## Overview
The heroes of the previous age. Five years ago they defeated the **Black Spider** and his followers at the Forge of Spells in [[Phandelver]], ushering in the new era of technology and prosperity.

*"The Wolfpack saved Phandelver, but at what cost?"* — the campaign's opening question. The reactivated Forge is now linked to [[The Tethered]].

## Known History
- Battle at the Forge of Spells; the Black Spider defeated (five years before [[Session 1]]).
