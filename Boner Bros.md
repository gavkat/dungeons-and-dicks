# The Boner Bros

**Tags:** #Faction #Party

---

## Overview
The party. Founded as the bond between [Rik](Rik.md) and [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) ([Session 2](Session%202.md)) over the wrestling hero "Bonecrusher," and grown into the collective name for the whole adventuring crew.

## Members
- [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) — divine warrior; missing father; wyvern [Dave](Dave.md).
- [Icarus](Icarus.md) — wild-magic noble; twice dead, currently a pink-haired half-orc; butler [Gearsley](Gearsley.md).
- [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) — thief turned **Shepherd** of [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md); bears the [Shard of the Great Devourer](Shard%20of%20the%20Great%20Devourer.md).
- [Rik](Rik.md) — pit champion seeking a cure for [Marisya](Marisya.md); wields the [Flame Tongue Whip](Flame%20Tongue%20Whip.md).

## Companions
- [Gearsley](Gearsley.md) — butler, driver, dead-raiser, heart-shooter.
- [Dave](Dave.md) — wyvern.
- [Polly](Polly.md) — teleporting parrot; calls Icarus "daddy."

## Known History
- Formed aboard [The Tempest's Temptation](The%20Tempest's%20Temptation.md); survived the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md), the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md), and the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md); currently stranded in the frozen tundra near [Holly Hollow](Holly%20Hollow.md).
