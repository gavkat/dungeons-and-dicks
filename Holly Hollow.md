# Holly Hollow

**Tags:** #Location #Village

---

## Overview
A festive village in the frozen tundra, host of the annual **Mid-Winter Jubilee** and keeper of the legend of **Father Klaus** — a mantle now truly held by [Glenn](Glenn.md).

## Sub-locations
- **Sno'where Like Home Inn** — the party's refuge.
- **Town square** — site of the Jubilee and the Culinary Crusaders attack.
- **The cottage portal** — a fireplace portal in the snowy forest leading to [Agatha the Hag](Agatha%20the%20Hag.md)'s swamp factory.

## Notable Residents
- The portly **Mayor**, who paid 500 gold for the children's rescue.
- [Glenn](Glenn.md) and his wolf Walter.

## Session History

### Session 12
