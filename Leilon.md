# Leilon

**Tags:** #Location #City

---

## Overview
The port city where the campaign began. Five years ago it suffered a dragon attack — the source of [[Wild Fury]]'s deepest trauma, having failed to save civilians there.

## Sub-locations
- **The Crow's Nest** — the tavern where [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] recruited the party into the [[Bottom Feeders]], and where pirates with blackened eyes first attacked ([[Session 1]]).

## Session History

### Session 1
- The party's origins converged here; the Crow's Nest massacre.

### Session 2
- The party fled by sea; departure stirred [[Wild Fury]]'s memories of the dragon attack.
