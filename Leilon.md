# Leilon

**Tags:** #Location #City

---

## Overview
The port city where the campaign began. Five years ago it suffered a dragon attack — the source of [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md)'s deepest trauma, having failed to save civilians there.

## Sub-locations
- **The Crow's Nest** — the tavern where [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md) recruited the party into the [Bottom Feeders](Bottom%20Feeders.md), and where pirates with blackened eyes first attacked ([Session 1](Session%201.md)).

## Session History

### Session 1
- The party's origins converged here; the Crow's Nest massacre.

### Session 2
- The party fled by sea; departure stirred [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md)'s memories of the dragon attack.
