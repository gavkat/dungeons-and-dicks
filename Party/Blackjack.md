# Jack "Blackjack" Blackwood

**Tags:** #character #pc #BottomFeeders #Shepherd

### Character Concept

You are **Jack "Blackjack" Blackwood**, an opportunist, thief, and showman — now the unwilling **Shepherd** of [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md).

**Core Traits:**
- A flair for the dramatic: staged car accidents, trap-gauntlet sprints screaming "Jack-Attack!", and tall tales about a "sea girlfriend" who is definitely, totally real.
- Bears the [Shard of the Great Devourer](Shard%20of%20the%20Great%20Devourer.md), which beats in time with his heart. Where others rotted, he became a vessel.
- Powers gained beneath the sea: the Fathomless Plunge and spectral tentacles ([Session 9](Session%209.md)).
- The [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md) hunts the amulet's thief — him — and [Icarus](Icarus.md) has been ordered to deliver that thief.

## Session History

### Session 1
- Faked being hit by [Gearsley](Gearsley.md)'s car to steal [Icarus](Icarus.md)'s [Sending Phone](Sending%20Phone.md). Joined the [Bottom Feeders](Bottom%20Feeders.md).

### Session 2
- Agreed to the [Polly](Polly.md) heist on his own terms (and the return of the phone). Shadowed [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md) and [Skum](Skum.md) below deck, overhearing their plotting.

### Session 3
- Overheard the Captain plotting a "reward." After the fight with the dominated [Sauriv](Sauriv.md), the cursed amulet fell into his possession.

### Session 4
- Rescued from the massacre by the sea elves [Vespera](Vespera.md) and [Kaelen](Kaelen.md).

### Session 9
- In a bioluminescent sanctuary, learned the amulet is the [Shard of the Great Devourer](Shard%20of%20the%20Great%20Devourer.md) and that he is the **Shepherd** — counterpart to the Prophet, [Kaelen](Kaelen.md) and [Vespera](Vespera.md)'s brother Valyrian. Trained with the sea elves; dreamt of inhabiting the Devourer's body. Failed spectacularly to woo [Vespera](Vespera.md). Returned via Fathomless Plunge to impale [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md) with a spectral tentacle.

### Session 10
- Claimed the [Ring of the Rotten](Ring%20of%20the%20Rotten.md); threw [Rik](Rik.md) the [Flame Tongue Whip](Flame%20Tongue%20Whip.md); vaporized the last mummy with Booming Blade.

### Session 11
- Sprinted the Hallway of the Keeper's traps ("Jack-Attack!").

### Session 12
- Suffered a hypothermic seizure in the sudden tundra; recovered at [Holly Hollow](Holly%20Hollow.md).
