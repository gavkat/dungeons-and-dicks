# Jack "Blackjack" Blackwood

**Tags:** #character #pc #BottomFeeders #Shepherd

### Character Concept

You are **Jack "Blackjack" Blackwood**, an opportunist, thief, and showman — now the unwilling **Shepherd** of [[The Great Devourer]].

**Core Traits:**
- A flair for the dramatic: staged car accidents, trap-gauntlet sprints screaming "Jack-Attack!", and tall tales about a "sea girlfriend" who is definitely, totally real.
- Bears the [[Shard of the Great Devourer]], which beats in time with his heart. Where others rotted, he became a vessel.
- Powers gained beneath the sea: the Fathomless Plunge and spectral tentacles ([[Session 9]]).
- The [[Sovereign Prophet]] hunts the amulet's thief — him — and [[Icarus]] has been ordered to deliver that thief.

## Session History

### Session 1
- Faked being hit by [[Gearsley]]'s car to steal [[Icarus]]'s [[Sending Phone]]. Joined the [[Bottom Feeders]].

### Session 2
- Agreed to the [[Polly]] heist on his own terms (and the return of the phone). Shadowed [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] and [[Skum]] below deck, overhearing their plotting.

### Session 3
- Overheard the Captain plotting a "reward." After the fight with the dominated [[Sauriv]], the cursed amulet fell into his possession.

### Session 4
- Rescued from the massacre by the sea elves [[Vespera]] and [[Kaelen]].

### Session 9
- In a bioluminescent sanctuary, learned the amulet is the [[Shard of the Great Devourer]] and that he is the **Shepherd** — counterpart to the Prophet, [[Kaelen]] and [[Vespera]]'s brother Valyrian. Trained with the sea elves; dreamt of inhabiting the Devourer's body. Failed spectacularly to woo [[Vespera]]. Returned via Fathomless Plunge to impale [[Tlincalli]] with a spectral tentacle.

### Session 10
- Claimed the [[Ring of the Rotten]]; threw [[Rik]] the [[Flame Tongue Whip]]; vaporized the last mummy with Booming Blade.

### Session 11
- Sprinted the Hallway of the Keeper's traps ("Jack-Attack!").

### Session 12
- Suffered a hypothermic seizure in the sudden tundra; recovered at [[Holly Hollow]].

### Session 13
- Still comatose, witnessed an apocalyptic warning from the [[Sovereign Prophet]] — the vision that woke him. Once conscious, he set the party on the road north to [[Hartsvale]].
