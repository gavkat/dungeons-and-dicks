# Wild Fury

**Tags:** #character #pc #BottomFeeders #BonerBros

### Character Concept

You are **Wild Fury**, a divine warrior haunted by failure, searching for his missing father.

**Core Traits:**
- Channels divine power from his ancestors — devastating smites ([[Session 10]], [[Session 12]]) and spells like Command and Suggestion.
- Bonded to his wyvern companion [[Dave]].
- Suffers panic attacks and flashbacks rooted in his failure to save civilians from a dragon attack on [[Leilon]] five years ago.
- One half of the [[Boner Bros]] with [[Rik]].

**Personal Quest:**
- Find his missing father, last seen near the [[Ice Spires]] (lead from [[Halia]], [[Session 1]]). Also described as hunting a missing guildmate ([[Session 5]]).
- Carries a prototype [[Sending Phone]] from [[Halia]], who told him of [[The Tethered]].

## Session History

### Session 1
- Met [[Halia]]; learned of [[The Tethered]] and received a [[Sending Phone]] and the [[Ice Spires]] lead about his father. Increasingly isolated and manic, tracking the trail with [[Dave]].

### Session 2
- Fleeing [[Leilon]] triggered trauma from the dragon attack five years ago. Bonded with [[Rik]] over wrestling hero "Bonecrusher"; formed the [[Boner Bros]]. Won the tag-team match against [[Krell]] and [[Sauriv]], then collapsed into a flashback.

### Session 3
- With [[Blackjack]], discovered [[Rik]] hanging in his room and fought the dominated [[Sauriv]].

### Session 4
- Escaped the doomed [[The Tempest's Temptation]] on [[Dave]]'s back.

### Session 5
- Shared his quest for a missing guildmate. Refused to surrender [[Dave]] to the [[Mage Guard]]; escaped to [[Mintan]]; suffered personalized hallucinations from tainted Ankheg meat.

### Session 7
- Cast Command in the fighting pit to help [[Rik]] defeat [[Tlincalli]]'s champion.

### Session 8
- Cleared the escape path from the [[Itju-Sha]] camp with a magical suggestion. Demanded proof of identity from the reborn [[Icarus]].

### Session 10
- Channeled his ancestors to smite the mummies of the [[Tomb of Tzentak]]; knocked unconscious in the trophy-room battle.

### Session 11
- Fought through panic for a final divine stand against [[Tzentak]]; polymorphed by [[Icarus]] into a Giant Ape and pummeled the Pharaoh.

### Session 12
- Reunited with [[Glenn]], his old guild's Head of HR. Destroyed Krampus ([[Agatha the Hag]]) with a massive divine smite. Received the ominous [[The White Claw]] flyer.
