# Wild Fury

**Tags:** #character #pc #BottomFeeders #BonerBros

### Character Concept

You are **Wild Fury**, a divine warrior haunted by failure, searching for his missing father.

**Core Traits:**
- Channels divine power from his ancestors — devastating smites ([Session 10](Session%2010.md), [Session 12](Session%2012.md)) and spells like Command and Suggestion.
- Bonded to his wyvern companion [Dave](Dave.md).
- Suffers panic attacks and flashbacks rooted in his failure to save civilians from a dragon attack on [Leilon](Leilon.md) five years ago.
- One half of the [Boner Bros](Boner%20Bros.md) with [Rik](Rik.md).

**Personal Quest:**
- Find his missing father, last seen near the [Ice Spires](Ice%20Spires.md) (lead from [Halia](Halia.md), [Session 1](Session%201.md)). Also described as hunting a missing guildmate ([Session 5](Session%205.md)).
- Carries a prototype [Sending Phone](Sending%20Phone.md) from [Halia](Halia.md), who told him of [The Tethered](The%20Tethered.md).

## Session History

### Session 1
- Met [Halia](Halia.md); learned of [The Tethered](The%20Tethered.md) and received a [Sending Phone](Sending%20Phone.md) and the [Ice Spires](Ice%20Spires.md) lead about his father. Increasingly isolated and manic, tracking the trail with [Dave](Dave.md).

### Session 2
- Fleeing [Leilon](Leilon.md) triggered trauma from the dragon attack five years ago. Bonded with [Rik](Rik.md) over wrestling hero "Bonecrusher"; formed the [Boner Bros](Boner%20Bros.md). Won the tag-team match against [Krell](Krell.md) and [Sauriv](Sauriv.md), then collapsed into a flashback.

### Session 3
- With [Blackjack](Blackjack.md), discovered [Rik](Rik.md) hanging in his room and fought the dominated [Sauriv](Sauriv.md).

### Session 4
- Escaped the doomed [The Tempest's Temptation](The%20Tempest's%20Temptation.md) on [Dave](Dave.md)'s back.

### Session 5
- Shared his quest for a missing guildmate. Refused to surrender [Dave](Dave.md) to the [Mage Guard](Mage%20Guard.md); escaped to [Mintan](Mintan.md); suffered personalized hallucinations from tainted Ankheg meat.

### Session 7
- Cast Command in the fighting pit to help [Rik](Rik.md) defeat [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md)'s champion.

### Session 8
- Cleared the escape path from the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md) camp with a magical suggestion. Demanded proof of identity from the reborn [Icarus](Icarus.md).

### Session 10
- Channeled his ancestors to smite the mummies of the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md); knocked unconscious in the trophy-room battle.

### Session 11
- Fought through panic for a final divine stand against [Tzentak](Tzentak.md); polymorphed by [Icarus](Icarus.md) into a Giant Ape and pummeled the Pharaoh.

### Session 12
- Reunited with [Glenn](Glenn.md), his old guild's Head of HR. Destroyed Krampus ([Agatha the Hag](Agatha%20the%20Hag.md)) with a massive divine smite. Received the ominous [The White Claw](The%20White%20Claw.md) flyer.
