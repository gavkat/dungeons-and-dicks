# Lord Icarus Valerius Corvus III

**Tags:** #character #pc #BottomFeeders #BonerBros #WildMagic

### Character Concept

You are **Lord Icarus Valerius Corvus III**, a flamboyant noble sorcerer on a "rehabilitation" mission — rehabilitation, specifically, from *eating birds*. Served loyally (and lethally protected) by his butler [Gearsley](Gearsley.md).

**Core Traits:**
- Wild Magic sorcerer: surges have reincarnated him, turned him into a potted plant, and blasted the party with apocalyptic visions.
- Spells seen: Minor Illusion, Hold Person, Polymorph, Fireball, high-voltage lightning.
- Has died **twice** ([Session 1](Session%201.md), [Session 7](Session%207.md)) and come back both times.
- Currently a hulking half-orc with shocking pink hair (reincarnated, [Session 8](Session%208.md)); originally rode the opulent "Aurora Sovereign" car.
- [Polly](Polly.md) the teleporting parrot calls him "daddy."

**Burdens:**
- The [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md)'s ultimatum ([Session 6](Session%206.md)): one lunar cycle to deliver the amulet thief — unknowingly his own friend [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) — or his debt stands.
- Family expectations, delivered via a restricted [Sending Phone](Sending%20Phone.md) from his father.

## Session History

### Session 1
- Received a restricted [Sending Phone](Sending%20Phone.md) from his father; [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) stole it via a staged car accident. Died in the Crow's Nest battle, glimpsed a black void, and was revived by [Gearsley](Gearsley.md).

### Session 2
- Gold-illusion prank sparked a crew brawl. Plotted the theft of [Polly](Polly.md); his magical influence on the Captain was countered by [Skum](Skum.md).

### Session 3
- Mysteriously vanished; found caged, compelled by an unseen voice to stay.

### Session 4
- Escaped his cage (cited as evidence against the party). Tried and failed to bargain with the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md).

### Session 5
- Confessed his "rehabilitation" is about eating birds.

### Session 6
- Dreamt of a kaleidoscopic world and a yellow butterfly. Woke voiceless to receive the Prophet's ultimatum: deliver the amulet thief within one lunar cycle. Told to "head northeast."

### Session 7
- Paralyzed and caged by [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md). Helped Rik win the pit fight with Hold Person, then — counting on a Wild Magic reincarnation — knelt and invited [Rik](Rik.md) to decapitate him.

### Session 8
- Reborn in the refuse heap as a pink-haired half-orc after declaring himself an "Immortal God" in the void. Incinerated "The Harpoon" with Fireball during the escape.

### Session 10
- Claimed the [Wand of Secrets](Wand%20of%20Secrets.md) from a dark void; time warped around him, leaving him exhausted. Knocked unconscious by mummies.

### Session 11
- Turned into a potted plant by a Wild Magic surge, recovered, polymorphed [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) into a Giant Ape, and froze the charmed [Rik](Rik.md) with Hold Person. His closing surge showed visions of a city consumed by darkness.

### Session 12
- Helped destroy Krampus with a high-voltage blast.
