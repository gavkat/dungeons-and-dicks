# Lord Icarus Valerius Corvus III

**Tags:** #character #pc #BottomFeeders #BonerBros #WildMagic

### Character Concept

You are **Lord Icarus Valerius Corvus III**, a flamboyant noble sorcerer on a "rehabilitation" mission — rehabilitation, specifically, from *eating birds*. Served loyally (and lethally protected) by his butler [[Gearsley]].

**Core Traits:**
- Wild Magic sorcerer: surges have reincarnated him, turned him into a potted plant, and blasted the party with apocalyptic visions.
- Spells seen: Minor Illusion, Hold Person, Polymorph, Fireball, high-voltage lightning.
- Has died **twice** ([[Session 1]], [[Session 7]]) and come back both times.
- Currently a hulking half-orc with shocking pink hair (reincarnated, [[Session 8]]); originally rode the opulent "Aurora Sovereign" car.
- [[Polly]] the teleporting parrot calls him "daddy."

**Burdens:**
- The [[Sovereign Prophet]]'s ultimatum ([[Session 6]]): one lunar cycle to deliver the amulet thief — unknowingly his own friend [[Blackjack]] — or his debt stands.
- Family expectations, delivered via a restricted [[Sending Phone]] from his father.

## Session History

### Session 1
- Received a restricted [[Sending Phone]] from his father; [[Blackjack]] stole it via a staged car accident. Died in the Crow's Nest battle, glimpsed a black void, and was revived by [[Gearsley]].

### Session 2
- Gold-illusion prank sparked a crew brawl. Plotted the theft of [[Polly]]; his magical influence on the Captain was countered by [[Skum]].

### Session 3
- Mysteriously vanished; found caged, compelled by an unseen voice to stay.

### Session 4
- Escaped his cage (cited as evidence against the party). Tried and failed to bargain with the [[Sovereign Prophet]].

### Session 5
- Confessed his "rehabilitation" is about eating birds.

### Session 6
- Dreamt of a kaleidoscopic world and a yellow butterfly. Woke voiceless to receive the Prophet's ultimatum: deliver the amulet thief within one lunar cycle. Told to "head northeast."

### Session 7
- Paralyzed and caged by [[Tlincalli]]. Helped Rik win the pit fight with Hold Person, then — counting on a Wild Magic reincarnation — knelt and invited [[Rik]] to decapitate him.

### Session 8
- Reborn in the refuse heap as a pink-haired half-orc after declaring himself an "Immortal God" in the void. Incinerated "The Harpoon" with Fireball during the escape.

### Session 10
- Claimed the [[Wand of Secrets]] from a dark void; time warped around him, leaving him exhausted. Knocked unconscious by mummies.

### Session 11
- Turned into a potted plant by a Wild Magic surge, recovered, polymorphed [[Wild Fury]] into a Giant Ape, and froze the charmed [[Rik]] with Hold Person. His closing surge showed visions of a city consumed by darkness.

### Session 12
- Helped destroy Krampus with a high-voltage blast.

### Session 13
- Polymorphed into a Giant Snow Owl to scout a path through the whiteout — but his avian appetite got the better of him; he lost his bearings and became **stranded alone** in the wastes, separated from the party.
