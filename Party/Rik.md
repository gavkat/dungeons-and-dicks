# Rik "The Bone Crusher" Magnus

**Tags:** #character #pc #BottomFeeders #BonerBros

### Character Concept

You are **Rik "The Bone Crusher" Magnus**, a wrestler-warrior fighting to save his cursed wife [Marisya](Marisya.md).

**Core Traits:**
- Named for the wrestling hero "Bonecrusher" that [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) and his father idolized.
- One half of the [Boner Bros](Boner%20Bros.md) with [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md).
- Pit-fighting Champion of the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md) arena ([Session 7](Session%207.md)–[Session 8](Session%208.md)).
- Wields the [Flame Tongue Whip](Flame%20Tongue%20Whip.md) taken from the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md).

**Personal Quest:**
- Cure [Marisya](Marisya.md). The lead — from [Riptide](Riptide.md) — pointed to the desert of [Anauroch](Anauroch.md) and the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md).

## Session History

### Session 1
- Learned of a potential cure for [Marisya](Marisya.md) in [Anauroch](Anauroch.md) from [Riptide](Riptide.md). Joined the [Bottom Feeders](Bottom%20Feeders.md).

### Session 2
- Helped [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) through a panic attack; formed the [Boner Bros](Boner%20Bros.md). Won the tag-team wrestling match against [Krell](Krell.md) and [Sauriv](Sauriv.md).

### Session 3
- Heard [Riptide](Riptide.md)'s confession about [The Traveler](The%20Traveler.md) and the cursed amulet. Was found hanging dead in his room — yet lived (unexplained).

### Session 4
- Accused by [Krell](Krell.md) of the murders. Escaped on [Dave](Dave.md).

### Session 6
- Dreamt of an oasis where the water turned to blood.

### Session 7
- Thrown into the fighting pit; with the party's hidden help, decapitated [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md)'s champion with the man's own weapon. Then, invited, decapitated [Icarus](Icarus.md).

### Session 8
- Celebrated as Champion in [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md)'s den; retrieved during the escape under cover of Fireball.

### Session 10
- Carved through the tomb's mummies with the [Flame Tongue Whip](Flame%20Tongue%20Whip.md).

### Session 11
- Charmed by [Tzentak](Tzentak.md)'s rotten heart; flooded with visions of Tzentak's deal with [The Dark Six](The%20Dark%20Six.md); turned his blade on the party before being frozen by Hold Person. Freed when the heart was destroyed.
