# Rik "The Bone Crusher" Magnus

**Tags:** #character #pc #BottomFeeders #BonerBros

### Character Concept

You are **Rik "The Bone Crusher" Magnus**, a wrestler-warrior fighting to save his cursed wife [[Marisya]].

**Core Traits:**
- Named for the wrestling hero "Bonecrusher" that [[Wild Fury]] and his father idolized.
- One half of the [[Boner Bros]] with [[Wild Fury]].
- Pit-fighting Champion of the [[Itju-Sha]] arena ([[Session 7]]–[[Session 8]]).
- Wields the [[Flame Tongue Whip]] taken from the [[Tomb of Tzentak]].

**Personal Quest:**
- Cure [[Marisya]]. The lead — from [[Riptide]] — pointed to the desert of [[Anauroch]] and the [[Tomb of Tzentak]].

## Session History

### Session 1
- Learned of a potential cure for [[Marisya]] in [[Anauroch]] from [[Riptide]]. Joined the [[Bottom Feeders]].

### Session 2
- Helped [[Wild Fury]] through a panic attack; formed the [[Boner Bros]]. Won the tag-team wrestling match against [[Krell]] and [[Sauriv]].

### Session 3
- Heard [[Riptide]]'s confession about [[The Traveler]] and the cursed amulet. Was found hanging dead in his room — yet lived (unexplained).

### Session 4
- Accused by [[Krell]] of the murders. Escaped on [[Dave]].

### Session 6
- Dreamt of an oasis where the water turned to blood.

### Session 7
- Thrown into the fighting pit; with the party's hidden help, decapitated [[Tlincalli]]'s champion with the man's own weapon. Then, invited, decapitated [[Icarus]].

### Session 8
- Celebrated as Champion in [[Tlincalli]]'s den; retrieved during the escape under cover of Fireball.

### Session 10
- Carved through the tomb's mummies with the [[Flame Tongue Whip]].

### Session 11
- Charmed by [[Tzentak]]'s rotten heart; flooded with visions of Tzentak's deal with [[The Dark Six]]; turned his blade on the party before being frozen by Hold Person. Freed when the heart was destroyed.

### Session 13
- Briefly **possessed by the oracle [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]** during the colliding arcane storm, becoming the mouthpiece for the warning that [[Tzentak]] was a [[Vox of the Dark Six|Vox]] and that five more Voxes now hunt the party.
