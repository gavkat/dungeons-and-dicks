# Itju-Sha

**Tags:** #Faction

---

## Overview
"The Takers of the Sand" — a desert gang of [[Anauroch]] in scrap-metal armor, riding crude sand vehicles, ruled (until recently) by the scorpion-man [[Tlincalli]] from a hideout built into a colossal desert-leviathan skeleton. They run fighting pits and burn what they cannot take.

## Notable Members
- [[Tlincalli]] — leader (slain, [[Session 9]]).
- "The Harpoon" and "The Pig" — pursuit vehicles (destroyed, [[Session 8]]).

## Known History
- Captured the party ([[Session 7]]); lost their champion to [[Rik]].
- Lost two war vehicles in the party's escape ([[Session 8]]).
- Burned [[Ouraeus]]'s oasis; ambush at the oasis broken and Tlincalli slain ([[Session 9]]). Current leadership unknown.
