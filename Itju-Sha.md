# Itju-Sha

**Tags:** #Faction

---

## Overview
"The Takers of the Sand" — a desert gang of [Anauroch](Anauroch.md) in scrap-metal armor, riding crude sand vehicles, ruled (until recently) by the scorpion-man [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md) from a hideout built into a colossal desert-leviathan skeleton. They run fighting pits and burn what they cannot take.

## Notable Members
- [Tlincalli](Tlincalli.md) — leader (slain, [Session 9](Session%209.md)).
- "The Harpoon" and "The Pig" — pursuit vehicles (destroyed, [Session 8](Session%208.md)).

## Known History
- Captured the party ([Session 7](Session%207.md)); lost their champion to [Rik](Rik.md).
- Lost two war vehicles in the party's escape ([Session 8](Session%208.md)).
- Burned [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md)'s oasis; ambush at the oasis broken and Tlincalli slain ([Session 9](Session%209.md)). Current leadership unknown.
