# Phandelver

**Tags:** #Location #Lore #WorldBuilding

---

## Overview
The rediscovered mines at the heart of the new age — "pumping a new flame, a new life force, into civilisation and humanity" under the stewardship of Prometheus, secretly [Mordenkainen](Mordenkainen.md). Five years ago, the battle here against the Black Spider (won by the [Wolfpack](Wolfpack.md)) ushered in the era of technology, industrialization, and warforged.

## The Forge of Spells
The ancient forge whose **reactivation** marks the turning point of the age — and the onset of [The Tethered](The%20Tethered.md) plague. *"Whispers... of people changing... of eyes turning black... The Wolfpack saved Phandelver, but at what cost?"*
