# The Dark Six

**Tags:** #Lore #WorldBuilding #Pantheon

---

## Overview
Six malevolent gods. [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md) is one of them; the pharaoh [Tzentak](Tzentak.md) owed his power to a deal with them; the whispers *"pieces of six"* carried by [Polly](Polly.md) may name them.

## Known Members
- [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md) — god of the hungering sea.
- Possibly [The Traveler](The%20Traveler.md) — the shrouded dealer of cursed gifts.
- Four others, unknown.
