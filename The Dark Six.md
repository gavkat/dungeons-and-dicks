# The Dark Six

**Tags:** #Lore #WorldBuilding #Pantheon

---

## Overview
Six malevolent gods. [[The Great Devourer]] is one of them; the pharaoh [[Tzentak]] owed his power to a deal with them; the whispers *"pieces of six"* carried by [[Polly]] may name them.

## Known Members
- [[The Great Devourer]] — god of the hungering sea.
- Possibly [[The Traveler]] — the shrouded dealer of cursed gifts.
- Four others, unknown.
