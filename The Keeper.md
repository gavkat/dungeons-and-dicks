# The Keeper

**Tags:** #Lore #WorldBuilding

---

## Overview
A deity (or power) honored at the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md) — its followers interred there, its name carried by the [Medji-Tzentak](Medji-Tzentak.md) ("Sons of the Keeper") and the tomb's "Hallway of the Keeper." The Keeper's exact relationship to the pharaoh [Tzentak](Tzentak.md) and [The Dark Six](The%20Dark%20Six.md) is unresolved.
