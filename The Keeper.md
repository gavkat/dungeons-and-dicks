# The Keeper

**Tags:** #Lore #WorldBuilding

---

## Overview
A deity (or power) honored at the [[Tomb of Tzentak]] — its followers interred there, its name carried by the [[Medji-Tzentak]] ("Sons of the Keeper") and the tomb's "Hallway of the Keeper." The Keeper's exact relationship to the pharaoh [[Tzentak]] and [[The Dark Six]] is unresolved.
