# Shard of the Great Devourer

**Tags:** #Item #Cursed #DarkSix

---

## Overview
An obsidian amulet of [[The Great Devourer]]. It rots ordinary bearers into necrotic husks — black rot consumed [[Riptide]] — but in [[Blackjack]] it found a vessel: it beats in synchronicity with his heart, making him the **Shepherd**.

## Chain of Custody
1. [[The Traveler]] → [[Riptide]] (dark pact, before [[Session 1]]).
2. [[Riptide]] → taken amid the events around [[Sauriv]]'s domination ([[Session 3]]).
3. [[Blackjack]] — current bearer ([[Session 3]]–present).

## The Hunt
The [[Sovereign Prophet]] demands the thief be delivered to him — and gave [[Icarus]] one lunar cycle to do it ([[Session 6]]). A matching amulet corrupted the Prophet himself, once a prince ([[Session 5]]).
