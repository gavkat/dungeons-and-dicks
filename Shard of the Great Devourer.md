# Shard of the Great Devourer

**Tags:** #Item #Cursed #DarkSix

---

## Overview
An obsidian amulet of [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md). It rots ordinary bearers into necrotic husks — black rot consumed [Riptide](Riptide.md) — but in [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) it found a vessel: it beats in synchronicity with his heart, making him the **Shepherd**.

## Chain of Custody
1. [The Traveler](The%20Traveler.md) → [Riptide](Riptide.md) (dark pact, before [Session 1](Session%201.md)).
2. [Riptide](Riptide.md) → taken amid the events around [Sauriv](Sauriv.md)'s domination ([Session 3](Session%203.md)).
3. [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) — current bearer ([Session 3](Session%203.md)–present).

## The Hunt
The [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md) demands the thief be delivered to him — and gave [Icarus](Icarus.md) one lunar cycle to do it ([Session 6](Session%206.md)). A matching amulet corrupted the Prophet himself, once a prince ([Session 5](Session%205.md)).
