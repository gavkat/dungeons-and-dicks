# The Great Devourer

**Tags:** #Lore #DarkSix #Antagonist

---

## Overview
A god of [[The Dark Six]] — a hungering power of the deep, manifest (or served) at sea by the monster [[Sha'Argan]] and heralded by the [[Sovereign Prophet]]. It marks ships and souls as "meals" and demands sacrifice by the score.

## Instruments
- [[Sovereign Prophet]] — Valyrian, its herald.
- [[Sha'Argan]] — its maw at sea.
- [[Shard of the Great Devourer]] — the obsidian amulet that corrupts its bearers... or, in [[Blackjack]]'s case, makes a **Shepherd**.

## The Prophet and the Shepherd
[[Kaelen]] framed the duality: Valyrian is the beast's Prophet; Jack, vessel of the Shard, is its Shepherd. Jack has dreamt of inhabiting the Devourer's gargantuan body and bending it to his will ([[Session 9]]).
