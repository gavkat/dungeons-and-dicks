# The Great Devourer

**Tags:** #Lore #DarkSix #Antagonist

---

## Overview
A god of [The Dark Six](The%20Dark%20Six.md) — a hungering power of the deep, manifest (or served) at sea by the monster [Sha'Argan](Sha'Argan.md) and heralded by the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md). It marks ships and souls as "meals" and demands sacrifice by the score.

## Instruments
- [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md) — Valyrian, its herald.
- [Sha'Argan](Sha'Argan.md) — its maw at sea.
- [Shard of the Great Devourer](Shard%20of%20the%20Great%20Devourer.md) — the obsidian amulet that corrupts its bearers... or, in [Blackjack](Blackjack.md)'s case, makes a **Shepherd**.

## The Prophet and the Shepherd
[Kaelen](Kaelen.md) framed the duality: Valyrian is the beast's Prophet; Jack, vessel of the Shard, is its Shepherd. Jack has dreamt of inhabiting the Devourer's gargantuan body and bending it to his will ([Session 9](Session%209.md)).
