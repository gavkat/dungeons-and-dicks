## The Premise

Five years have passed since the battle at the Forge of Spells, where the [Wolfpack](Wolfpack.md) defeated the Black Spider. A new age of technology, industrialization, and prosperity has dawned — warforged and common folk building side by side, powered by the rediscovered mines of [Phandelver](Phandelver.md) and the patronage of "Prometheus," secretly [Mordenkainen](Mordenkainen.md).

But beneath the surface: rumors, whispers — people changing, eyes turning black, [The Tethered](The%20Tethered.md) falling ill since the Forge reawakened. *The Wolfpack saved Phandelver — but at what cost?*

## The Story So Far

- **Act I — The Sea** ([Session 1](Session%201.md)–[Session 4](Session%204.md)): Recruited into the [Bottom Feeders](Bottom%20Feeders.md) at [Leilon](Leilon.md); ghost ships in [The Choke](The%20Choke.md); murders aboard [The Tempest's Temptation](The%20Tempest's%20Temptation.md); the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md) revealed and [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md) devoured.
- **Interlude — Castaways** ([Session 5](Session%205.md)–[Session 6](Session%206.md)): The [Mage Guard](Mage%20Guard.md), the island of [Mintan](Mintan.md), [Koa](Koa.md)'s revelations, the Prophet's ultimatum — and being swallowed whole.
- **Act II — The Desert** ([Session 7](Session%207.md)–[Session 11](Session%2011.md)): Captured by the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md); pit fights and a Fury-Road escape; [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) reborn as Shepherd; the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md); [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md)'s betrayal and [Tzentak](Tzentak.md)'s destruction.
- **Act III — The Frost** ([Session 12](Session%2012.md)–): The desert replaced by tundra; [Holly Hollow](Holly%20Hollow.md)'s Mid-Winter Jubilee; [Agatha the Hag](Agatha%20the%20Hag.md) slain; [Glenn](Glenn.md) crowned Father Klaus; the [The White Claw](The%20White%20Claw.md) flyer arrives.
