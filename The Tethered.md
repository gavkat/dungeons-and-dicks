# The Tethered

**Tags:** #Lore #Mystery

---

## Overview
People falling mysteriously ill since the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] was reactivated — the dark undercurrent of the new age. Likely connected to the whispers of people changing and *eyes turning black* — as seen in the pirates at [[Leilon#The Crow's Nest|The Crow's Nest]] ([[Session 1]]) and possibly the dominated [[Sauriv]] ([[Session 3]]).
