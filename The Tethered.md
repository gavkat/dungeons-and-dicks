# The Tethered

**Tags:** #Lore #Mystery

---

## Overview
People falling mysteriously ill since the [](Phandelver.md#The%20Forge%20of%20Spells|Forge%20of%20Spells) was reactivated — the dark undercurrent of the new age. Likely connected to the whispers of people changing and *eyes turning black* — as seen in the pirates at [](Leilon.md#The%20Crow's%20Nest|The%20Crow's%20Nest) ([Session 1](Session%201.md)) and possibly the dominated [Sauriv](Sauriv.md) ([Session 3](Session%203.md)).
