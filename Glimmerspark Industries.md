# Glimmerspark Industries

**Tags:** #Faction #WorldBuilding

---

## Overview
The technology company behind the prototype [[Sending Phone]] — emblem of the new industrial age flowing from [[Phandelver]]. Its name (and almost certainly its founder) traces back to [[Glimmerspark Tumpleton]], the gnome artificer the [[Wolfpack]] met in [[Leilon]] during [[Lost Mine of Phandelver|the previous age]].

## Known History
- Prototype phones reached [[Wild Fury]] (via [[Halia]]) and [[Icarus]] (via his father, restricted) in [[Session 1]]; [[Blackjack]] promptly stole the latter.
