## Overview
A jungle island in the [Sea of Swords](Sea%20of%20Swords.md) — refuge of [Koa](Koa.md). Home to Ankhegs whose (recently tainted) meat causes powerful hallucinations, poisonous fish, and — for a time — the whispering parrot [Polly](Polly.md).
