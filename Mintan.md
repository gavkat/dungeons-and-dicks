# Mintan

**Tags:** #Location #Island

---

## Overview
A jungle island in the [[Sea of Swords]] — refuge of [[Koa]]. Home to Ankhegs whose (recently tainted) meat causes powerful hallucinations, poisonous fish, and — for a time — the whispering parrot [[Polly]].

## Session History

### Session 5
- The party crash-landed via portal; killed an Ankheg and the pursuing [[Mage Guard]]; met [[Koa]]; traced the "pieces of six" whispers to [[Polly]].

### Session 6
- Two days of foraging and raft-building; farewell to [[Koa]].
