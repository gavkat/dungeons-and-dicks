# Mage Guard

**Tags:** #Faction #Authority

---

## Overview
Enforcers of the [[Draconic Containment Accord]] and the armed arm of [[The Consortium]]. They demanded the party surrender [[Dave]] for "sanitization" or be classified as "collaborators in contamination."

## Known History
- Intercepted the party over the [[Sea of Swords]] ([[Session 5]]); outwitted (their own banishing box used to save Dave); their pursuers died on [[Mintan]] — one to an Ankheg, the rest to the party and [[Koa]]. Their dead were, regrettably, made into travel kebabs ([[Session 6]]).
- **[[Session 13]]** — **Consortium** Mage Guards ambushed the trek north to [[Hartsvale]] and were easily beaten by the [[Boner Bros]]. Cornered, the surviving ground soldier called in a **"Code Black"**, summoning [[The Ashen Figure]], who abducted [[Dave]]. This was the first confirmation the Guards answer to [[The Consortium]].
- The party are presumably still fugitives in their eyes.
