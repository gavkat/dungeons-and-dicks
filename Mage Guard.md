# Mage Guard

**Tags:** #Faction #Authority

---

## Overview
Enforcers of the [Draconic Containment Accord](Draconic%20Containment%20Accord.md). They demanded the party surrender [Dave](Dave.md) for "sanitization" or be classified as "collaborators in contamination."

## Known History
- Intercepted the party over the [Sea of Swords](Sea%20of%20Swords.md) ([Session 5](Session%205.md)); outwitted (their own banishing box used to save Dave); their pursuers died on [Mintan](Mintan.md) — one to an Ankheg, the rest to the party and [Koa](Koa.md). Their dead were, regrettably, made into travel kebabs ([Session 6](Session%206.md)).
- The party are presumably still fugitives in their eyes.
