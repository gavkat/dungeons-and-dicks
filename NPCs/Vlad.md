# Vlad

**Tags:** #character #npc #Giant #Campaign2

## Description
A massive, **twenty-foot Frost Giant** — yet timid and hunched, nothing like the brutes his size suggests. He emerged from a screaming whiteout blizzard and stepped into the light of the party's sputtering campfire just as the gale finally died down.

## Role in Campaign
Introduced at the very end of [[Session 13]], in the frozen wastes on the road north toward [[Hartsvale]]. Whether Vlad is a threat, a guide, or a refuge for the frostbitten and food-less [[Boner Bros]] is still unknown — but his arrival broke the storm.

## Session History

### Session 13
- Appeared as a twenty-foot shadow over the survivors' camp, then revealed himself as a timid, hunched Frost Giant named Vlad.
