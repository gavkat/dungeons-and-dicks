# Kaelen

**Tags:** #character #npc #SeaElf #Ally

## Description
A sea elf of the bioluminescent sanctuary. Brother of **Valyrian** — the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md) — and of [Vespera](Vespera.md).

## Role in Campaign
Saw the truth of [Blackjack](Blackjack.md)'s bond with the [Shard of the Great Devourer](Shard%20of%20the%20Great%20Devourer.md) and named him: *if Valyrian is the Prophet of the beast, then Jack is the Shepherd.*
