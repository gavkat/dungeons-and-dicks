# The Ashen Figure

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Mystery #Campaign2

## Description
A mysterious figure **cloaked in warping ash**, summoned as [[The Consortium|the Consortium]]'s **"Code Black"** reinforcement. It tore through a portal, out-manoeuvred the entire [[Boner Bros|party]] with ease, and just as swiftly vanished — taking [[Dave]] with it. Identity, nature, and allegiance unknown.

## Role in Campaign
The first sign that [[The Consortium]] commands powers far beyond rank-and-file [[Mage Guard|Mage Guards]]. The abduction of [[Wild Fury]]'s wyvern [[Dave]] makes recovering him — and unmasking this figure — a fresh and urgent thread.

Its arrival "through a portal" amid "warping ash" invites comparison with other shrouded powers of this age: the cursed-gift dealer [[The Traveler]], and the net of darkness the [[Vox of the Dark Six|Voxes]] are said to be weaving ([[Session 13]]). No connection is confirmed.

## Session History
