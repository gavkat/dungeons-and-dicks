# Sha'Argan

**Tags:** #character #npc #Monster #DarkSix

## Description
A gargantuan sea monster — a "black hole lined with teeth" — heralded by the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md) as the maw of [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md).

## Role in Campaign
The instrument of the Devourer's hunger at sea. Whether Sha'Argan *is* the Devourer's body or merely its creature remains unclear; in [Session 9](Session%209.md) [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) dreamt of inhabiting the Devourer's gargantuan body.

## Session History

### Session 4
- Consumed [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md) and the crew of [The Tempest's Temptation](The%20Tempest's%20Temptation.md).

### Session 6
- Its tentacles destroyed the party's raft; swallowed the party whole — who awoke alive on the shores of [Anauroch](Anauroch.md).
