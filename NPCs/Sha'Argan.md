# Sha'Argan

**Tags:** #character #npc #Monster #DarkSix

## Description
A gargantuan sea monster — a "black hole lined with teeth" — heralded by the [[Sovereign Prophet]] as the maw of [[The Great Devourer]].

## Role in Campaign
The instrument of the Devourer's hunger at sea. Whether Sha'Argan *is* the Devourer's body or merely its creature remains unclear; in [[Session 9]] [[Blackjack]] dreamt of inhabiting the Devourer's gargantuan body.

## Session History

### Session 4
- Consumed [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] and the crew of [[The Tempest's Temptation]].

### Session 6
- Its tentacles destroyed the party's raft; swallowed the party whole — who awoke alive on the shores of [[Anauroch]].
