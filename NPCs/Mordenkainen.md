# Mordenkainen

**Tags:** #character #npc #Mystery #WorldBuilding

## Description
Known to the world as **Prometheus** — the figure credited with pumping "a new flame, a new life force" into civilization through the rediscovered mines of [[Phandelver]].

## Role in Campaign
The architect of the new industrial age. The campaign's opening narration pointedly unmasked him: *"thanks to Prometheus, or should we say, Mordenkainen."* His role in the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] reactivation ties him — knowingly or not — to [[The Tethered]] plague.
