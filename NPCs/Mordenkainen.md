# Mordenkainen

**Tags:** #character #npc #Mystery #WorldBuilding

## Description
Known to the world as **Prometheus** — the figure credited with pumping "a new flame, a new life force" into civilization through the rediscovered mines of [Phandelver](Phandelver.md).

## Role in Campaign
The architect of the new industrial age. The campaign's opening narration pointedly unmasked him: *"thanks to Prometheus, or should we say, Mordenkainen."* His role in the [](Phandelver.md#The%20Forge%20of%20Spells|Forge%20of%20Spells) reactivation ties him — knowingly or not — to [The Tethered](The%20Tethered.md) plague.
