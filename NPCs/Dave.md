# Dave

**Tags:** #character #npc #Companion #Beast

## Description
[[Wild Fury]]'s wyvern companion. Long-suffering, frequently "wrecked," reliably heroic.

## Role in Campaign
Mount, escape vehicle, and the reason the [[Mage Guard]] branded the party violators of the [[Draconic Containment Accord]].

## Session History

### Session 1
- Kept [[Wild Fury]] company through his obsessive search for his father.

### Session 4
- Carried the party off [[The Tempest's Temptation]] as it fell to [[Sha'Argan]].

### Session 5
- Target of the [[Mage Guard]]'s "sanitization" demand; saved via the guards' own banishing box.

### Session 6
- Too "wrecked" to fly the party off [[Mintan]].

### Session 12
- Flew the party to [[Holly Hollow]] through the frozen tundra.
