# Dave

**Tags:** #character #npc #Companion #Beast

## Description
[Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md)'s wyvern companion. Long-suffering, frequently "wrecked," reliably heroic.

## Role in Campaign
Mount, escape vehicle, and the reason the [Mage Guard](Mage%20Guard.md) branded the party violators of the [Draconic Containment Accord](Draconic%20Containment%20Accord.md).

## Session History

### Session 1
- Kept [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) company through his obsessive search for his father.

### Session 4
- Carried the party off [The Tempest's Temptation](The%20Tempest's%20Temptation.md) as it fell to [Sha'Argan](Sha'Argan.md).

### Session 5
- Target of the [Mage Guard](Mage%20Guard.md)'s "sanitization" demand; saved via the guards' own banishing box.

### Session 6
- Too "wrecked" to fly the party off [Mintan](Mintan.md).

### Session 12
- Flew the party to [Holly Hollow](Holly%20Hollow.md) through the frozen tundra.
