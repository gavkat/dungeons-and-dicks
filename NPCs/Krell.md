# Krell

**Tags:** #character #npc #BottomFeeders

## Description
First Mate of [The Tempest's Temptation](The%20Tempest's%20Temptation.md). Disdainful of the party from the start. Kept a wyrmling, [Chomp](Chomp.md), who went missing.

## Role in Campaign
Chief accuser when [Riptide](Riptide.md) and [Sauriv](Sauriv.md) were murdered. Fate after the ship's fall is unknown.

## Session History

### Session 2
- With [Sauriv](Sauriv.md), challenged [Rik](Rik.md) and [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md) to the tag-team wrestling match — and lost.

### Session 3
- His wyrmling [Chomp](Chomp.md) went mysteriously missing.

### Session 4
- Publicly accused the party of the murders, turning the crew hostile. Last seen in the chaos of [Sha'Argan](Sha'Argan.md)'s attack.
