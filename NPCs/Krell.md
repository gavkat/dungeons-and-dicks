# Krell

**Tags:** #character #npc #BottomFeeders

## Description
First Mate of [[The Tempest's Temptation]]. Disdainful of the party from the start. Kept a wyrmling, [[Chomp]], who went missing.

## Role in Campaign
Chief accuser when [[Riptide]] and [[Sauriv]] were murdered. Fate after the ship's fall is unknown.

## Session History

### Session 2
- With [[Sauriv]], challenged [[Rik]] and [[Wild Fury]] to the tag-team wrestling match — and lost.

### Session 3
- His wyrmling [[Chomp]] went mysteriously missing.

### Session 4
- Publicly accused the party of the murders, turning the crew hostile. Last seen in the chaos of [[Sha'Argan]]'s attack.
