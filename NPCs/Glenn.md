# Glenn

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally #FatherKlaus

## Description
[[Wild Fury]]'s former associate and the Head of HR of his guild. Despite only five years passing, Glenn has aged terribly into an old man — unexplained. Accompanied by his wolf **Walter**, now a levitating stallion wolf.

## Role in Campaign
Reunited with the party at [[Holly Hollow]]'s Mid-Winter Jubilee while playing the village's "Father Klaus." After [[Agatha the Hag]]'s defeat, he claimed her magical coat — which shifted into an elegant red garment — and ascended as the *true* Father Klaus, departing by sleigh.

His mysterious aging may connect to whatever happened to [[Wild Fury]]'s guild and missing father.

## Session History

### Session 12
- Revealed as Holly Hollow's festive legend; fought the Culinary Crusaders alongside the party; became the true Father Klaus.
