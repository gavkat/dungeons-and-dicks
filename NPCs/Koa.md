# Koa

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally

## Description
A tusked warrior hiding on the island of [[Mintan]]. Initially simple of speech, later surprisingly eloquent.

## Role in Campaign
Former friend of the prince who became the [[Sovereign Prophet]]. Revealed the Prophet's origin — a prince twisted by an amulet of [[The Great Devourer]], one of [[The Dark Six]] — and warned the party they are now *marked*. Refuses to return to the sea.

## Session History

### Session 5
- Helped the party kill the Ankheg and the [[Mage Guard]] pursuers. Revealed the Prophet's history and asked the party to investigate the "pieces of six" whispers.

### Session 6
- Urged the party to stay; bid them a tearful, newly-eloquent farewell as they set off by raft.

### Session 9
- Aided [[Blackjack]]'s dramatic return via a runic nautilus shell at the oasis battle.
