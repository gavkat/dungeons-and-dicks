# Koa

**Tags:** #character #npc #Ally

## Description
A tusked warrior hiding on the island of [Mintan](Mintan.md). Initially simple of speech, later surprisingly eloquent.

## Role in Campaign
Former friend of the prince who became the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md). Revealed the Prophet's origin — a prince twisted by an amulet of [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md), one of [The Dark Six](The%20Dark%20Six.md) — and warned the party they are now *marked*. Refuses to return to the sea.

## Session History

### Session 5
- Helped the party kill the Ankheg and the [Mage Guard](Mage%20Guard.md) pursuers. Revealed the Prophet's history and asked the party to investigate the "pieces of six" whispers.

### Session 6
- Urged the party to stay; bid them a tearful, newly-eloquent farewell as they set off by raft.

### Session 9
- Aided [Blackjack](Blackjack.md)'s dramatic return via a runic nautilus shell at the oasis battle.
