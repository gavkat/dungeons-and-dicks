# The Traveler

**Tags:** #character #npc #Mystery #DarkSix

## Description
A shrouded figure who deals in cursed gifts.

## Role in Campaign
Made the dark pact that gave [[Riptide]] the [[Shard of the Great Devourer]] — the amulet now bound to [[Blackjack]]. Identity, motives, and allegiance unknown; possibly connected to [[The Dark Six]] (in some traditions the Traveler is counted among them).

## Session History

### Session 3
- Named in [[Riptide]]'s confession as the source of the cursed amulet.
