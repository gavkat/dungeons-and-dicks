# The Traveler

**Tags:** #character #npc #Mystery #DarkSix

## Description
A shrouded figure who deals in cursed gifts.

## Role in Campaign
Made the dark pact that gave [Riptide](Riptide.md) the [Shard of the Great Devourer](Shard%20of%20the%20Great%20Devourer.md) — the amulet now bound to [Blackjack](Blackjack.md). Identity, motives, and allegiance unknown; possibly connected to [The Dark Six](The%20Dark%20Six.md) (in some traditions the Traveler is counted among them).

## Session History

### Session 3
- Named in [Riptide](Riptide.md)'s confession as the source of the cursed amulet.
