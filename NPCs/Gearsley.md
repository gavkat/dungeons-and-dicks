# Gearsley

**Tags:** #character #npc #BottomFeeders #Ally

## Description
[[Icarus]]'s impeccable butler and driver of the "Aurora Sovereign." Unflappable, surprisingly lethal, and hiding something — he *revived a dead man*.

## Role in Campaign
Loyal retainer and de-facto fifth party member. His ability to bring [[Icarus]] back from death ([[Session 1]]) has never been explained.

## Session History

### Session 1
- Drove the Aurora Sovereign; his "accident" with [[Blackjack]] was the cover for the phone theft. Fought surprisingly well in the Crow's Nest battle and **revived the dead [[Icarus]]**.

### Session 3
- Fell unconscious fighting the ghost pirates in [[The Choke]].

### Session 6
- Magically held by [[Skum]] while the [[Sovereign Prophet]] delivered his ultimatum.

### Session 8
- Spiraled with grief over Icarus's execution, then hot-wired the getaway vehicle for the escape.

### Session 11
- Gunned down the Naga ([[Ouraeus]]) and fired the shot that destroyed [[Tzentak]]'s heart.
