# Gearsley

**Tags:** #character #npc #BottomFeeders #Ally

## Description
[Icarus](Icarus.md)'s impeccable butler and driver of the "Aurora Sovereign." Unflappable, surprisingly lethal, and hiding something — he *revived a dead man*.

## Role in Campaign
Loyal retainer and de-facto fifth party member. His ability to bring [Icarus](Icarus.md) back from death ([Session 1](Session%201.md)) has never been explained.

## Session History

### Session 1
- Drove the Aurora Sovereign; his "accident" with [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) was the cover for the phone theft. Fought surprisingly well in the Crow's Nest battle and **revived the dead [Icarus](Icarus.md)**.

### Session 3
- Fell unconscious fighting the ghost pirates in [The Choke](The%20Choke.md).

### Session 6
- Magically held by [Skum](Skum.md) while the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md) delivered his ultimatum.

### Session 8
- Spiraled with grief over Icarus's execution, then hot-wired the getaway vehicle for the escape.

### Session 11
- Gunned down the Naga ([Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md)) and fired the shot that destroyed [Tzentak](Tzentak.md)'s heart.
