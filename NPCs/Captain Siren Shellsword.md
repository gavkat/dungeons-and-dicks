# Captain Siren Shellsword

**Tags:** #character #npc #BottomFeeders #Deceased

## Description
The eccentric captain of [[The Tempest's Temptation]] and founder of the [[Bottom Feeders]] crew. Owner of [[Polly]], the teleporting macaw.

## Role in Campaign
Recruited the party — then was revealed to be in league with the [[Sovereign Prophet]], having promised 40 souls as sacrifice to [[The Great Devourer]]. Five years ago he promised [[Riptide]] salvation in the [[Tomb of Tzentak]].

**Status: Dead.** Snatched from his own deck and consumed by the Devourer as punishment for falling short of his quota ([[Session 4]]).

## Session History

### Session 1
- Recruited the party into the [[Bottom Feeders]] at [[Leilon#The Crow's Nest|The Crow's Nest]].

### Session 2
- His prized macaw became the target of [[Icarus]]'s heist plot; protected by [[Skum]]'s counter-magic.

### Session 3
- Arrived with [[Riptide]] to turn the ghost-ship battle. Overheard plotting a "reward" and the need to "survive one more day."

### Session 4
- Exposed as the Prophet's collaborator; devoured by [[The Great Devourer]].
