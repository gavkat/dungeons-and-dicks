# Captain Siren Shellsword

**Tags:** #character #npc #BottomFeeders #Deceased

## Description
The eccentric captain of [The Tempest's Temptation](The%20Tempest's%20Temptation.md) and founder of the [Bottom Feeders](Bottom%20Feeders.md) crew. Owner of [Polly](Polly.md), the teleporting macaw.

## Role in Campaign
Recruited the party — then was revealed to be in league with the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md), having promised 40 souls as sacrifice to [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md). Five years ago he promised [Riptide](Riptide.md) salvation in the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md).

**Status: Dead.** Snatched from his own deck and consumed by the Devourer as punishment for falling short of his quota ([Session 4](Session%204.md)).
