# Tlincalli

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased

## Description
The scorpion-man ruler of the [[Itju-Sha]] — spiked flail, grappling chain, paralytic tail sting, and a taste for pit fights.

**Status: Dead.** Impaled by [[Blackjack]]'s spectral tentacle at the oasis ([[Session 9]]).

## Session History

### Session 7
- Captured the party; paralyzed and caged [[Icarus]]; threw [[Rik]] into the fighting pit, then threw Icarus in after.

### Session 8
- Hosted the champion [[Rik]] in his den; his pursuit vehicles "The Harpoon" and "The Pig" were destroyed in the escape.

### Session 9
- Led the burning of [[Ouraeus]]'s oasis and the ambush there; killed by [[Blackjack]].
