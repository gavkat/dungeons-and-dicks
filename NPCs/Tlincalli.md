# Tlincalli

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased

## Description
The scorpion-man ruler of the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md) — spiked flail, grappling chain, paralytic tail sting, and a taste for pit fights.

**Status: Dead.** Impaled by [Blackjack](Blackjack.md)'s spectral tentacle at the oasis ([Session 9](Session%209.md)).

## Session History

### Session 7
- Captured the party; paralyzed and caged [Icarus](Icarus.md); threw [Rik](Rik.md) into the fighting pit, then threw Icarus in after.

### Session 8
- Hosted the champion [Rik](Rik.md) in his den; his pursuit vehicles "The Harpoon" and "The Pig" were destroyed in the escape.

### Session 9
- Led the burning of [Ouraeus](Ouraeus.md)'s oasis and the ambush there; killed by [Blackjack](Blackjack.md).
