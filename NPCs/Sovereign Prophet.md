# The Sovereign Prophet (Valyrian)

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #DarkSix

## Description
Once a sea-elf prince named **Valyrian** — brother to [[Kaelen]] and [[Vespera]] — twisted into the herald of [[The Great Devourer]] by one of its amulets. Speaks with a powerful, disembodied voice; commands sahuagin and the monster [[Sha'Argan]].

## Role in Campaign
The campaign's central antagonist so far. Collects sacrifices for the Devourer, consumed [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] for failing his quota, and has given [[Icarus]] one lunar cycle to deliver the thief of the [[Shard of the Great Devourer]] — unknowingly [[Blackjack]], the Shepherd and his own counterpart.

## Session History

### Session 4
- Announced himself; had the Devourer consume [[Captain Siren Shellsword]]; his sahuagin fed the crew to [[Sha'Argan]]. Refused [[Icarus]]'s bargain.

### Session 5
- His origin revealed by [[Koa]]: a prince corrupted by a Devourer amulet.

### Session 6
- Boarded the party's raft with [[Skum]]; delivered the ultimatum to [[Icarus]] (one lunar cycle, "head northeast"), then had tentacles swallow the party.

### Session 9
- Named as **Valyrian**, brother of [[Kaelen]] — making [[Blackjack]] the Shepherd to his Prophet.

### Session 13
- Reached the still-comatose [[Blackjack]] with an **apocalyptic warning**, the vision that finally stirred him awake.
