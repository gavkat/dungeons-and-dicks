# The Sovereign Prophet (Valyrian)

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #DarkSix

## Description
Once a sea-elf prince named **Valyrian** — brother to [Kaelen](Kaelen.md) and [Vespera](Vespera.md) — twisted into the herald of [The Great Devourer](The%20Great%20Devourer.md) by one of its amulets. Speaks with a powerful, disembodied voice; commands sahuagin and the monster [Sha'Argan](Sha'Argan.md).

## Role in Campaign
The campaign's central antagonist so far. Collects sacrifices for the Devourer, consumed [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md) for failing his quota, and has given [Icarus](Icarus.md) one lunar cycle to deliver the thief of the [Shard of the Great Devourer](Shard%20of%20the%20Great%20Devourer.md) — unknowingly [Blackjack](Blackjack.md), the Shepherd and his own counterpart.

## Session History

### Session 4
- Announced himself; had the Devourer consume [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md); his sahuagin fed the crew to [Sha'Argan](Sha'Argan.md). Refused [Icarus](Icarus.md)'s bargain.

### Session 5
- His origin revealed by [Koa](Koa.md): a prince corrupted by a Devourer amulet.

### Session 6
- Boarded the party's raft with [Skum](Skum.md); delivered the ultimatum to [Icarus](Icarus.md) (one lunar cycle, "head northeast"), then had tentacles swallow the party.

### Session 9
- Named as **Valyrian**, brother of [Kaelen](Kaelen.md) — making [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) the Shepherd to his Prophet.
