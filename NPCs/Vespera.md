# Vespera

**Tags:** #character #npc #SeaElf #Ally

## Description
A regal sea elf of the bioluminescent sanctuary beneath the [Sea of Swords](Sea%20of%20Swords.md). Sister (with [Kaelen](Kaelen.md)) to **Valyrian**, the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md).

## Role in Campaign
Rescued [Blackjack](Blackjack.md) from drowning and helped train him as the Shepherd. The target of Jack's disastrously unsuccessful wooing — and the seed of his "sea girlfriend" tall tale.
