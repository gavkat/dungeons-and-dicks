# Vespera

**Tags:** #character #npc #SeaElf #Ally

## Description
A regal sea elf of the bioluminescent sanctuary beneath the [[Sea of Swords]]. Sister (with [[Kaelen]]) to **Valyrian**, the [[Sovereign Prophet]].

## Role in Campaign
Rescued [[Blackjack]] from drowning and helped train him as the Shepherd. The target of Jack's disastrously unsuccessful wooing — and the seed of his "sea girlfriend" tall tale.

## Session History

### Session 4
- With [[Kaelen]], pulled [[Blackjack]] from the massacre at sea.

### Session 9
- Hosted and trained Jack in the sanctuary; coldly rebuffed his romantic efforts.
