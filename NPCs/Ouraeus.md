# Ouraeus

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #MedjiTzentak

## Description
A serpentine figure met in the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md) cages — hands that melt into snakes, a snake-charmer's tune, and a destiny he spoke of often. In truth, a **naga**.

## Role in Campaign
Claimed to be the last of the [Medji-Tzentak](Medji-Tzentak.md), "Sons of the Keeper," destined to free the party. The truth: he slaughtered his own village as "repentance" and sought to **revive the Pharaoh [Tzentak](Tzentak.md)**, using the party to reach the tomb's heart.

**Status: Dead.** Gunned down by [Gearsley](Gearsley.md) in the final battle ([Session 11](Session%2011.md)).

## Session History

### Session 7
- Met the party in the cages; promised escape after they "survived" the pit.

### Session 8
- Stole the keys with serpent hands; revealed himself (to [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md)) as the last Medji-Tzentak. Summoned spectral fangs to destroy "The Pig."

### Session 9
- Led the party home to his oasis — found burned by the [Itju-Sha](Itju-Sha.md). Fought in the ambush.

### Session 11
- Revealed his true allegiance with a snake-charmer's tune, leading the charmed [Rik](Rik.md) to defend [Tzentak](Tzentak.md)'s heart. Killed by [Gearsley](Gearsley.md).
