# Ouraeus

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased #MedjiTzentak

## Description
A serpentine figure met in the [[Itju-Sha]] cages — hands that melt into snakes, a snake-charmer's tune, and a destiny he spoke of often. In truth, a **naga**.

## Role in Campaign
Claimed to be the last of the [[Medji-Tzentak]], "Sons of the Keeper," destined to free the party. The truth: he slaughtered his own village as "repentance" and sought to **revive the Pharaoh [[Tzentak]]**, using the party to reach the tomb's heart.

**Status: Dead.** Gunned down by [[Gearsley]] in the final battle ([[Session 11]]).

## Session History

### Session 7
- Met the party in the cages; promised escape after they "survived" the pit.

### Session 8
- Stole the keys with serpent hands; revealed himself (to [[Wild Fury]]) as the last Medji-Tzentak. Summoned spectral fangs to destroy "The Pig."

### Session 9
- Led the party home to his oasis — found burned by the [[Itju-Sha]]. Fought in the ambush.

### Session 11
- Revealed his true allegiance with a snake-charmer's tune, leading the charmed [[Rik]] to defend [[Tzentak]]'s heart. Killed by [[Gearsley]].
