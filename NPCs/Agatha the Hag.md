# Agatha the Hag

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased

## Description
A hag who masqueraded as the legendary fiend **Krampus**, operating a twisted industrial factory in a murky swamp reached by a fireplace portal near [Holly Hollow](Holly%20Hollow.md).

**Status: Dead.** Destroyed by [Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md)'s divine smite and [Icarus](Icarus.md)'s lightning ([Session 12](Session%2012.md)); reverted to her true form in death. The party speaks of her as "familiar" — a possible past encounter.

## Role in Campaign
Abducted five children from the Mid-Winter Jubilee (using the Culinary Crusaders as a diversion) and was morphing them into gremlin-like creatures on a conveyor belt. Her magical coat passed to [Glenn](Glenn.md), making him the true Father Klaus.

## Session History

### Session 12
- Defeated in her Krampus form; two of the five children did not survive.
