# Agatha the Hag

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Deceased

## Description
A hag who masqueraded as the legendary fiend **Krampus**, operating a twisted industrial factory in a murky swamp reached by a fireplace portal near [[Holly Hollow]].

**Status: Dead.** Destroyed by [[Wild Fury]]'s divine smite and [[Icarus]]'s lightning ([[Session 12]]); reverted to her true form in death. The party speaks of her as "familiar" — a possible past encounter.

## Role in Campaign
Abducted five children from the Mid-Winter Jubilee (using the Culinary Crusaders as a diversion) and was morphing them into gremlin-like creatures on a conveyor belt. Her magical coat passed to [[Glenn]], making him the true Father Klaus.

## Session History

### Session 12
- Defeated in her Krampus form; two of the five children did not survive.
