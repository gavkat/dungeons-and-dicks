# Tzentak

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Undead #Deceased

## Description
An ancient pharaoh whose tomb dominates the [[Anauroch]] desert. His power flowed from a beating, **rotten heart** hidden in a cavern beneath his tomb — the fruit of a deal with [[The Dark Six]].

## Role in Campaign
The "salvation" promised in his tomb drew [[Riptide]], and the cure for [[Marisya]] drew [[Rik]]. His heart charmed Rik instantly and flooded him with visions of the pharaoh's origins.

**Status: Destroyed.** Pummeled by a Giant Ape ([[Wild Fury]]) and ended when [[Gearsley]] shot the heart ([[Session 11]]). His destruction triggered the vision of a darkness-consumed city and the party's transport to frozen tundra.

## Session History

### Session 11
- His rotten heart charmed [[Rik]]; [[Ouraeus]] attempted his revival; destroyed for good when the heart was shot.
