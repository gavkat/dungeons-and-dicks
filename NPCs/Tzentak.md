# Tzentak

**Tags:** #character #npc #Antagonist #Undead #Deceased

## Description
An ancient pharaoh whose tomb dominates the [Anauroch](Anauroch.md) desert. His power flowed from a beating, **rotten heart** hidden in a cavern beneath his tomb — the fruit of a deal with [The Dark Six](The%20Dark%20Six.md).

## Role in Campaign
The "salvation" promised in his tomb drew [Riptide](Riptide.md), and the cure for [Marisya](Marisya.md) drew [Rik](Rik.md). His heart charmed Rik instantly and flooded him with visions of the pharaoh's origins.

**Status: Destroyed.** Pummeled by a Giant Ape ([Wild Fury](Wild%20Fury.md)) and ended when [Gearsley](Gearsley.md) shot the heart ([Session 11](Session%2011.md)). His destruction triggered the vision of a darkness-consumed city and the party's transport to frozen tundra.
