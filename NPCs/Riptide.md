# Riptide

**Tags:** #character #npc #BottomFeeders #Deceased

## Description
A powerful warrior of [[The Tempest's Temptation]] and an ally to [[Rik]] — slowly consumed by black rot from a cursed amulet.

## Role in Campaign
Gave [[Rik]] the lead about a cure for [[Marisya]] in [[Anauroch]]. Made a dark pact with [[The Traveler]] for the amulet later revealed as the [[Shard of the Great Devourer]]. Five years ago, [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] promised him salvation in the [[Tomb of Tzentak]].

**Status: Dead.** Murdered overnight aboard ship ([[Session 4]]); the amulet was already gone.

## Session History

### Session 1
- Gave [[Rik]] the [[Anauroch]] cure lead.

### Session 3
- Helped turn the ghost-ship battle. Confessed his pact with [[The Traveler]] and the rot consuming his body; apologized for bringing [[Rik]] aboard.

### Session 4
- Found murdered alongside [[Sauriv]].
