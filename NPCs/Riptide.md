# Riptide

**Tags:** #character #npc #BottomFeeders #Deceased

## Description
A powerful warrior of [The Tempest's Temptation](The%20Tempest's%20Temptation.md) and an ally to [Rik](Rik.md) — slowly consumed by black rot from a cursed amulet.

## Role in Campaign
Gave [Rik](Rik.md) the lead about a cure for [Marisya](Marisya.md) in [Anauroch](Anauroch.md). Made a dark pact with [The Traveler](The%20Traveler.md) for the amulet later revealed as the [Shard of the Great Devourer](Shard%20of%20the%20Great%20Devourer.md). Five years ago, [Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md) promised him salvation in the [Tomb of Tzentak](Tomb%20of%20Tzentak.md).

**Status: Dead.** Murdered overnight aboard ship ([Session 4](Session%204.md)); the amulet was already gone.

## Session History

### Session 1
- Gave [Rik](Rik.md) the [Anauroch](Anauroch.md) cure lead.

### Session 3
- Helped turn the ghost-ship battle. Confessed his pact with [The Traveler](The%20Traveler.md) and the rot consuming his body; apologized for bringing [Rik](Rik.md) aboard.

### Session 4
- Found murdered alongside [Sauriv](Sauriv.md).
