# Polly

**Tags:** #character #npc #Companion #Beast

## Description
[Captain Siren Shellsword](Captain%20Siren%20Shellsword.md)'s prized macaw — a *teleporting* parrot that bamfs away from danger (or from [Icarus](Icarus.md)'s teeth) and whispers *"pieces of six."*

## Role in Campaign
First a heist target, then a mystery: her whispers haunted [Mintan](Mintan.md) before the party found her. She has adopted [Icarus](Icarus.md), calling him "daddy." What "pieces of six" means — and its echo of [The Dark Six](The%20Dark%20Six.md) — remains unanswered.
