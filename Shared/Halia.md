# Halia

**Tags:** #character #npc #Shared #Campaign1 #Campaign2 #QuestGiver

> **Shared across campaigns.** Halia Thornton aided the [[Wolfpack]] in [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]] (Campaign 1) and remains [[Wild Fury]]'s connected, mysterious contact in [[Echoes of Phandelver]] (Campaign 2).

## Description
**Halia Thornton** — a Vedalken druid, daughter of [[The Bookkeeper]], and a strong ally of [[Wild Fury]]. Informed, connected, and never quite showing her whole hand.

## In Campaign 1 — Lost Mine of Phandelver
A mysterious druid in [[Phandalin]] with a grudge against [[The Redbrands]] and a habit of steering the party from the shadows.

- Fed [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]] intelligence on the Redbrand base, flirted with [[Glenn]], and fought in the chaos of Phandalin in Owlbear form (Sessions 4–5).
- Revealed at [[Old Owl Well]] as the **Bookkeeper's daughter**, appearing first as the Scarlet Great-horned Owl (Session 17). Wild-shaped into a giant eagle and a Giant Ape to carry and protect the party through the climb to the [[Wave Echo Cave|Forge of Spells]].
- **Epilogue:** now a respected member of the [[Lionshield Coster]], she delivered the news that broke [[Wild Fury]]'s world — *his father was still alive.*

## In Campaign 2 — Echoes of Phandelver
Still [[Wild Fury]]'s contact — informed, connected, and carrying prototype [[Glimmerspark Industries]] technology.

Set [[Wild Fury]]'s quest in motion: told him of [[The Tethered]] — people falling ill since the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] reactivated — gave him a prototype [[Sending Phone]], and the lead that his father was last seen near the [[Ice Spires]].

### Campaign 2 Session History
- **[[Session 1]]** — Met with [[Wild Fury]]; delivered the tethered warning, the phone, and the father lead.
