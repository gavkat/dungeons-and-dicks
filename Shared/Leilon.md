# Leilon

**Tags:** #Location #Shared #Campaign1 #Campaign2 #City

> **Shared across campaigns.** The port city destroyed by a dragon in [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]] (Campaign 1) is the rebuilt city where [[Echoes of Phandelver]] (Campaign 2) begins.

## Overview
A picturesque port city on the Sword Coast, home to the Fountain of Fortune and, once, an Adventuring Academy. Five years ago it suffered a catastrophic **dragon attack** — the source of [[Wild Fury]]'s deepest trauma, having failed to save civilians there.

## In Campaign 1 — Lost Mine of Phandelver
The [[Wolfpack]] came to Leilon seeking the Fountain of Fortune to heal [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] and recruit allies (Session 18) — and walked into disaster (Session 19).

- Met Captain [[The Thornes|Eammon Thorne]] and his father [[The Thornes|Alaric]], priest of the Fountain of Fortune (created by [[Tymora]]); revived [[Gundren Rockseeker|Gundren]] in its waters.
- Recruited Academy teachers [[Garrick Dreldan]] and [[Tolgun Aer'thi]]; met [[Glimmerspark Tumpleton]] and [[Sylvinia Salazar|Silvinia]].
- Then a massive **red dragon** — [[Switchblade]], reincarnated by the [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba]] — razed the town: the Academy fell, [[Eldrin Willowbrook|Headmaster Willowbrook]] was crushed, [[Tolgun Aer'thi]] killed, and the [[The Thornes|Thornes]] died protecting the fountain, whose waters ran red with their blood. It is this massacre that haunts [[Wild Fury]].

## In Campaign 2 — Echoes of Phandelver
The rebuilt port city where the new campaign opens. [[Wild Fury]]'s panic attacks trace directly to the dragon attack five years prior.

### Sub-locations
- **The Crow's Nest** — the tavern where [[Captain Siren Shellsword]] recruited the party into the [[Bottom Feeders]], and where pirates with blackened eyes first attacked ([[Session 1]]).

### Campaign 2 Session History
- **[[Session 1]]** — The party's origins converged here; the Crow's Nest massacre.
- **[[Session 2]]** — The party fled by sea; departure stirred [[Wild Fury]]'s memories of the dragon attack.
