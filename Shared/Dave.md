# Dave

**Tags:** #character #npc #Shared #Campaign1 #Campaign2 #Companion #Beast

> **Shared across campaigns.** The wyvern egg [[Wild Fury]] saved in [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]] (Campaign 1) is the wyvern Dave of [[Echoes of Phandelver]] (Campaign 2).

## Description
[[Wild Fury]]'s wyvern companion. Long-suffering, frequently "wrecked," reliably heroic.

## In Campaign 1 — Lost Mine of Phandelver
Dave's origin: at [[Wyvern Tor]], a vision of [[Wild Fury]]'s grandfather led him to the remains of a defeated wyvern and a single **egg** nestled in his grandfather's armor. Wild Fury vowed to safeguard it (Session 20). By the epilogue he was raising the hatchling — the wyvern that would become Dave.

## In Campaign 2 — Echoes of Phandelver
Mount, escape vehicle, and the reason the [[Mage Guard]] branded the party violators of the [[Draconic Containment Accord]].

### Campaign 2 Session History
- **[[Session 1]]** — Kept [[Wild Fury]] company through his obsessive search for his father.
- **[[Session 4]]** — Carried the party off [[The Tempest's Temptation]] as it fell to [[Sha'Argan]].
- **[[Session 5]]** — Target of the [[Mage Guard]]'s "sanitization" demand; saved via the guards' own banishing box.
- **[[Session 6]]** — Too "wrecked" to fly the party off [[Mintan]].
- **[[Session 12]]** — Flew the party to [[Holly Hollow]] through the frozen tundra.
