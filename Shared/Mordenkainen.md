# Mordenkainen

**Tags:** #character #Shared #Campaign1 #Campaign2 #Mystery #WorldBuilding

> **Shared across campaigns.** The Warforged wizard **Prometheus** of [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]] (Campaign 1) *is* the archmage **Mordenkainen** — and the "Prometheus" who lit the new industrial age in [[Echoes of Phandelver]] (Campaign 2). Wikilinks to `[[Prometheus]]` resolve here.

## Description
**Mordenkainen** — the greatest wizard of the age, inventor of the warforged, and the architect of *Project Prometheus*: immortality achieved by refusing the [[The Dark Six|Dark Six]] and stealing divine fire instead. Reborn as the Warforged wizard **Prometheus**, he is at once a member of the [[Wolfpack]] and the most consequential figure in the world's history.

## In Campaign 1 — Lost Mine of Phandelver
Reanimated on the High Road by what claimed to be a goddess (in truth the [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba]]), the warforged **Prometheus** joined the [[Wolfpack]] to recover the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]] and stop the Black Spider.

- An original creation of the [[Wave Echo Cave|Forge of Spells]], rebuilt and given purpose; cast fireballs liberally along the way.
- Visions and the living statue of [[King Dumathoin]] revealed the truth: Prometheus is **Mordenkainen reincarnated** (Session 24). Long ago Mordenkainen invented the warforged and refused the [[The Dark Six|Dark Six]] — for which his best friend [[Szass Tam]] accepted their bargain and became a lich.
- Attuned the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]] at [[Wyvern Tor]] (Session 20), then at the climax chose **Conjunction** over Disjunction — reigniting the [[Wave Echo Cave|Forge of Spells]] and ushering in the new age of technology (Session 25).
- **Epilogue:** ripped from reality the instant his world-saving spell completed, Prometheus/Mordenkainen hangs tethered in a mysterious dimension, his soul a siphon for an undead Beholder's negative energy — kept alive and suffering by [[Szass Tam]].

## In Campaign 2 — Echoes of Phandelver
Known to the world as **Prometheus**, the figure credited with pumping *"a new flame, a new life force"* into civilization through the rediscovered mines of [[Phandelver]]. The campaign's opening narration pointedly unmasked him: *"thanks to Prometheus, or should we say, Mordenkainen."* His reactivation of the [[Phandelver#The Forge of Spells|Forge of Spells]] ties him — knowingly or not — to [[The Tethered]] plague.

### Campaign 2 Session History
_Not yet encountered directly; named in the campaign prologue. Whether he is the public benefactor "Prometheus," still tortured by [[Szass Tam]], or something stranger remains an open thread._
