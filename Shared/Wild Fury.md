# Wild Fury

**Tags:** #character #pc #Shared #Campaign1 #Campaign2 #BottomFeeders #BonerBros

> **Shared across campaigns.** A player character in *both* [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]] (Campaign 1) and [[Echoes of Phandelver]] (Campaign 2) — the same Tabaxi paladin, five years on.

## Description
**Wild Fury III** — a Tabaxi paladin and heir to the [[Lionshield Coster]], the covert guild that quietly keeps Faerûn's political order. A divine warrior who channels the power of his ancestors, bonded to his wyvern [[Dave]], and forever chasing the shadow of his missing father.

## In Campaign 1 — Lost Mine of Phandelver
First found locked in a prison cell in [[Cragmaw Castle]], "Wild Fury III" was freed by the [[Wolfpack]] and joined their crusade against the Black Spider.

- Empowered by an amulet against the vampire [[Lightning Steel]]; inherited the sword [[Lightbringer]] from [[Frederick Iron-Thunder|Frederick]] in exchange for a giant Elk steed (Session 11).
- In the trials of [[Old Owl Well]], his test — *the Roar of Pride* — forced a confrontation with his father (Session 17). He was then poisoned by the shapeshifter [[Talice]] wearing [[Halia]]'s face.
- At [[Wyvern Tor]], a vision of his **grandfather** revealed the origins of the [[Lionshield Coster]]. There he recovered his grandfather's armor and sword — and a single **wyvern egg**, which he vowed to protect (Session 20). That egg hatched into [[Dave]].
- His blood was among the three the Black Spider harvested to mirror the Ilefarn ritual at the [[Wave Echo Cave|Forge of Spells]] (Session 25).
- **Epilogue:** with his father absent, Wild Fury III assumed leadership of the [[Lionshield Coster]] and raised [[Dave]]. Then [[Halia]] delivered the revelation that shattered his peace — *his father was still alive.*

## In Campaign 2 — Echoes of Phandelver
Five years later, Wild Fury is one of the [[Boner Bros]], still haunted and still searching.

**Core traits:**
- Channels devastating divine smites ([[Session 10]], [[Session 12]]) and spells like Command and Suggestion.
- Still bonded to [[Dave]].
- Suffers panic attacks rooted in his failure to save civilians from the dragon that destroyed [[Leilon]] five years ago — the very dragon ([[Switchblade]]) the Wolfpack faced in Campaign 1.

**Personal quest:**
- Find his missing father, last seen near the [[Ice Spires]] — a lead from [[Halia]] ([[Session 1]]), and the same "father is alive" thread left dangling at the end of Campaign 1. Also framed as hunting a "missing guildmate" ([[Session 5]]) of the Lionshield Coster.
- Carries a prototype [[Sending Phone]] from [[Halia]], who told him of [[The Tethered]].
