# Phandelver

**Tags:** #Location #Shared #Campaign1 #Campaign2 #Lore #WorldBuilding

> **Shared across campaigns.** The lost mines and the [[Wave Echo Cave|Forge of Spells]] are the climactic prize of [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]] (Campaign 1) and the engine of the new age in [[Echoes of Phandelver]] (Campaign 2).

## Overview
The rediscovered mines at the heart of the new age — *"pumping a new flame, a new life force, into civilisation and humanity"* under the stewardship of Prometheus, secretly [[Mordenkainen]]. Five years ago, the battle here against [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]] (won by the [[Wolfpack]]) ushered in the era of technology, industrialization, and warforged.

The mines themselves lie within the [[Wave Echo Cave]]; the frontier town of [[Phandalin]] sits in their shadow. Both were dug under the ancient [[Phandelver's Pact]].

## The Forge of Spells
The ancient forge whose **reactivation** marks the turning point of the age — and the onset of [[The Tethered]] plague. *"Whispers... of people changing... of eyes turning black... The Wolfpack saved Phandelver, but at what cost?"*

In Campaign 1 the [[Wolfpack]] fought to the Forge through the [[Wave Echo Cave]], where [[Prometheus]] chose **Conjunction** and reignited it — the very act that lit Campaign 2's industrial age. For the full account see [[Wave Echo Cave]].
