# Glenn

**Tags:** #character #Shared #Campaign1 #Campaign2 #FatherKlaus

> **Shared across campaigns.** Glenn Anderson fought beside the [[Wolfpack]] in [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]] (Campaign 1) and resurfaces, mysteriously aged, in [[Echoes of Phandelver]] (Campaign 2).

## Description
**Glenn Anderson** — a Human barbarian/bard and self-described "totally normal dude" with an unexpected knack for battle (and bardic mischief). Surrogate father to the wolves [[Walter & Kyle]]. Originally revealed in Campaign 1 to be an *experiment* of the wizard [[Finethir Shinebright]].

## In Campaign 1 — Lost Mine of Phandelver
A founding member of the [[Wolfpack]], present from the [[Prequel - The Wild Sheep's Chase|Prequel]] onward.

- Tamed the wolves **Kyle** and **Walter** ([[Walter & Kyle]]) at [[Cragmaw Hideout]] (Session 3) and used Speak with Animals and animal charm to befriend beasts throughout the campaign.
- Captured by the Cragmaw tribe and dragged to [[Cragmaw Castle]]; thrown from a window by [[King Grol]], he transformed into his full bestial form mid-fall and survived (Session 12).
- Ran his infamous "Human Resources" infiltration of the smeltery and exposed the shapeshifter [[Talice]] with the codeword "Meatloaf" (Session 24).
- **Epilogue:** retired to a cottage in [[Littlefoot]] with **Walter** — until a black-eyed [[Droop]] knocked on his door rasping *"Howdy Glenn,"* and lunged. One of the first omens of [[The Tethered]].

## In Campaign 2 — Echoes of Phandelver
Five years later, Glenn is [[Wild Fury]]'s former associate and the Head of HR of his guild (the [[Lionshield Coster]]). Despite only five years passing, he has aged terribly into an old man — unexplained — and is still accompanied by **Walter** ([[Walter & Kyle]]), now a levitating stallion wolf.

Reunited with the party at [[Holly Hollow]]'s Mid-Winter Jubilee while playing the village's "Father Klaus." After [[Agatha the Hag]]'s defeat he claimed her magical coat — which shifted into an elegant red garment — and ascended as the *true* Father Klaus, departing by sleigh. His mysterious aging may connect to whatever befell [[Wild Fury]]'s guild and missing father.
