# The Wolfpack

**Tags:** #Faction #Shared #Campaign1 #Campaign2 #Lore #WorldBuilding

> **Shared across campaigns.** The Wolfpack are the player party of [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]] (Campaign 1) — and the legendary "heroes of the previous age" whose deeds set the stage for [[Echoes of Phandelver]] (Campaign 2).

## Overview
The adventuring party who recovered the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]], defeated [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]], and reignited the [[Wave Echo Cave|Forge of Spells]] — birthing the age of technology, industry, and warforged.

*"The Wolfpack saved Phandelver, but at what cost?"* — Campaign 2's opening question. The reactivated Forge is now linked to [[The Tethered]].

## Members (Campaign 1)
- [[Wild Fury]] — Wild Fury III, Tabaxi paladin; heir to the [[Lionshield Coster]]; bonded to the wyvern [[Dave]]. *(Continues as a PC in Campaign 2.)*
- [[Glenn]] — Glenn Anderson, Human barbarian/bard; surrogate father of [[Walter & Kyle]]. *(Returns, mysteriously aged, in Campaign 2.)*
- [[Prometheus]] — the Warforged wizard, secretly [[Mordenkainen]] reincarnated.
- [[Ilbryn Darksight]] — blind Half-Elf monk/rogue; the Elven Oracle.
- [[Frederick Iron-Thunder]] — Tortle cleric; wielder of [[Lightbringer]]; left the party in Session 11, returned in Session 20.
- Companions: the wolves [[Walter & Kyle]].

**Former / prequel members:** [[Ethel Elkhid]], [[Dew-Dew Bun]], [[Switchblade]], [[Tlaloc Nightfoot]].

## Known History
- Formed from strangers at the [[Daggerford]] Saint's Day fair ([[Prequel - The Wild Sheep's Chase|Prequel]]).
- Recruited by [[Gundren Rockseeker]] to escort cargo to [[Phandalin]]; drawn into the hunt for the [[Wave Echo Cave]] and the war against [[Finethir Shinebright|the Black Spider]].
- Battle at the [[Wave Echo Cave|Forge of Spells]]; the Black Spider defeated and the Forge reignited (five years before [[Session 1]] of Campaign 2).
- See the full chronicle: [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]].
