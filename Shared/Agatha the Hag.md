# Agatha the Hag

**Tags:** #character #npc #Shared #Campaign1 #Campaign2 #Antagonist

> **Shared across campaigns.** The vain hag the [[Wolfpack]] bargained with in [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]] (Campaign 1) is the "familiar" Krampus the party slew in [[Echoes of Phandelver]] (Campaign 2).

## Description
**Agatha** — a vain, ancient hag who trades cryptic answers for tribute. In Campaign 1 the price was a silver comb; in Campaign 2 she had reinvented herself as an industrialist monster.

## In Campaign 1 — Lost Mine of Phandelver
Dwelling in a lair in the woods of [[Conyberry]], Agatha would answer a single question in exchange for a silver comb. The [[Wolfpack]] sought from her the whereabouts of the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]].

- [[Sister Garaele]] first sent the party to seek her out (Session 4).
- The party bartered the comb for a cryptic riddle about [[Old Owl Well]] (Session 10). [[Prometheus]]'s impatience nearly earned her wrath before a swift apology spared them.

## In Campaign 2 — Echoes of Phandelver
Five years on, Agatha masqueraded as the legendary fiend **Krampus**, running a twisted industrial factory in a murky swamp reached by a fireplace portal near [[Holly Hollow]]. The party noted she seemed *"familiar"* — and well they might.

**Status: Dead.** Destroyed by [[Wild Fury]]'s divine smite and [[Icarus]]'s lightning ([[Session 12]]); reverted to her true hag form in death. She had abducted five children from the Mid-Winter Jubilee (using the Culinary Crusaders as a diversion), morphing them into gremlin-like creatures on a conveyor belt. Her magical coat passed to [[Glenn]], making him the true Father Klaus.

### Campaign 2 Session History
- **[[Session 12]]** — Defeated in her Krampus form; two of the five children did not survive.
