# The Dark Six

**Tags:** #Lore #Shared #Campaign1 #Campaign2 #WorldBuilding #Pantheon

> **Shared across campaigns.** The six malevolent gods loom over both [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]] (Campaign 1), which reveals their origin, and [[Echoes of Phandelver]] (Campaign 2), where they are the gathering threat.

## Overview
Six malevolent gods. Campaign 1 uncovers what they are: the **six leaders of the [[The Ilefarn|Ilefarn]] nation**, ancient High Elves who performed the *tri animalus arcanum* ritual — sacrificing an "unknowing" harengon, an "unproven" grung, and an "unguided" aarakocra — to seize godhood. They became the Dark Six; the rest of the Ilefarn were cursed into Nothics, and the deed birthed the Elven Oracle prophecy.

The orc god [[Maglubiyet]] once bargained with them to extinguish the warforged ([[Prologue|Prologue]]); the lich [[Szass Tam]] accepted their offer of eternal life when [[Mordenkainen]] refused it.

## Known / Suspected Members
- **[[Beshaba]]** — Lady Mischief; an Elf-turned-God, sister of [[Tymora]]. Her [[Vox Spiritus of Beshaba|Vox Spiritus]] reanimated [[Prometheus]] and hunted the [[Spellbook of Mordenkainen]] (Campaign 1).
- **[[The Great Devourer]]** — god of the hungering sea (named in Campaign 2).
- Possibly **[[The Traveler]]** — the shrouded dealer of cursed gifts (Campaign 2).
- The remaining members unconfirmed; the whispers *"pieces of six"* carried by [[Polly]] may yet name them.

## In Campaign 1
- The [[Prologue|Prologue]] and Sessions 22–25 trace the Dark Six to the Ilefarn ritual at the [[Wave Echo Cave|Forge of Spells]].
- **Epilogue:** [[Ilbryn Darksight|Ilbryn]]'s oracle vision shows the Dark Six mid-ritual amid images of **ocean, arctic tundra, and desert**, then their voices as one: *"The Dark Six are coming."* — the bridge directly into Campaign 2.
