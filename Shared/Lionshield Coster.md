# Lionshield Coster

**Tags:** #Faction #Shared #Campaign1 #Campaign2

> **Shared across campaigns.** [[Wild Fury]]'s family guild — revealed in [[Lost Mine of Phandelver]] (Campaign 1) and the "guild" he and [[Glenn]] still belong to in [[Echoes of Phandelver]] (Campaign 2).

## Overview
A covert guild that quietly works to ensure Faerûn's political order. Outwardly a trading company; inwardly something far more deliberate. The guild is the inheritance of [[Wild Fury]], whose father led it and whose grandfather's story (glimpsed in a vision at [[Wyvern Tor]]) revealed its true origins.

## In Campaign 1 — Lost Mine of Phandelver
- [[Wild Fury|Wild Fury III]] is named the heir to "the Lionshield Coster" when freed from [[Cragmaw Castle]] (Session 11).
- A vision of his grandfather at [[Wyvern Tor]] revealed the Coster's origins — a legacy of sacrifice and protection — alongside the wyvern egg that became [[Dave]] (Session 20).
- **Epilogue:** with his father missing, Wild Fury III assumed leadership of the Coster; [[Halia]] joined as a respected member and later revealed that his father still lived.

## In Campaign 2 — Echoes of Phandelver
Five years on, the guild persists. [[Glenn]] serves as its Head of HR, and [[Wild Fury]]'s hunt for his missing father — framed at one point as a search for a "missing guildmate" ([[Session 5]]) — is bound up with whatever has befallen the Coster. [[Halia]] still moves within its orbit, dispensing leads and prototype [[Glimmerspark Industries]] technology.
