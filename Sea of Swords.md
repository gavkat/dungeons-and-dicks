# Sea of Swords

**Tags:** #Location

## Overview
The great western sea — hunting ground of the [Sovereign Prophet](Sovereign%20Prophet.md) and [Sha'Argan](Sha'Argan.md), and home (in its depths) to the bioluminescent sea-elf sanctuary of [Vespera](Vespera.md) and [Kaelen](Kaelen.md).
