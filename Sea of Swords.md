# Sea of Swords

**Tags:** #Location

## Overview
The great western sea — hunting ground of the [[Sovereign Prophet]] and [[Sha'Argan]], and home (in its depths) to the bioluminescent sea-elf sanctuary of [[Vespera]] and [[Kaelen]].
